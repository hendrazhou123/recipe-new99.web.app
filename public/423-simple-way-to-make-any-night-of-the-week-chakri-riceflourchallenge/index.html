  2. Prepare 125 gms butter
  3. Make ready 1/2 gram flour
  4. Take 2 tbsp yoghurt
  5. Get 3 tbspSesame seeds,1tbsp green chillies,1tbsp ginger paste
  6. Make ready Salt to taste,1tbsp sugar,1 bunch corriander
