Instructions to make Healthy Soup-weight loss soup:

  2. Heat oil in a cooker add garlic ginger & chilli sauté it
  3. Then add onion sauté it & add all the veggies and salt
  4. Cook for a minute then add 2-3 cups of water and cook till 2-3 whistles
  5. As the pressure release open cooker and churn/blend it
