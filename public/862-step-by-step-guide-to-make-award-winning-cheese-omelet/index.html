The ingredients needed to make Cheese Omelet:
  1. Make ready 2 eggs
  2. Make ready 1 chopped onion medium size
  3. Prepare 1 chopped potato medium size
  4. Get 4 tbsp oil
  5. Take to taste salt
  6. Take 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  7. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper
  8. Prepare as needed Cheddar cheese / mozzarella cheese
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp chopped cabbage

Instructions to make Cheese Omelet:
  1. First chop the onion, cabbage and potato in a medium size.and soak potato's in salted water.
  2. Cut the cheese in a strip shape.
  3. In a bowl break eggs and add red chilli, black pepper, salt
  4. Beat well and make sure that the whole mixture full mix.
  5. In an another pot fry onion potato and cabbage for 5-8 min till potato become soft
  6. Now add fried onion, potato, cabbage
  7. Pre heat the pan in a medium flame. pour egg mixture and spread it.
  8. Now keep cheese strips on top of egg and covered by turning egg sides.
  9. When egg become cook in one side turns and cook other side till it become light brown in color
  10. Its ready to serve. Cut into square shape. Enjoy !
