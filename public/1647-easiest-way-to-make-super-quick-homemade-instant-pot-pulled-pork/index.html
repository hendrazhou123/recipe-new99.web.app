The ingredients needed to make Instant Pot Pulled Pork:
  1. Make ready 1 1-2 lbs pork butt
  2. Make ready Pork dry rub
  3. Get Liquid smoke
  4. Take 1 can coca cola
  5. Prepare 1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
  6. Prepare 1 large bottle of BBQ sauce

Instructions to make Instant Pot Pulled Pork:
  1. Pour some liquid smoke on the pork but then add a generous amount of dry rub on all sides of pork butt, wrap in foil/plastic baggie and refrigerate overnight.
  2. In a bowl combine 1 half bottle of BBQ sauce, the coca cola and the apple cider vinegar.
  3. Cut pork but into 3 pieces to fit in bottom of instant pot.
  4. Select sautee/brown function on instant pot. When done warming up add the pork butt and brown on both sides for 2 minutes each. (When done select stop on instant pot.)
  5. Add BBQ mixture in bowl to the instant pot over pork butt.
  6. Select the no steam/pressure cook nozzle. On instant pot lid.
  7. Seal the top in the locked position and select the meat function, make sure on high setting and adjust timer to 40 minutes.
