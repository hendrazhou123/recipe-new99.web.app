Instructions to make Zarda (Sweet Colourful rice):
  1. Now Boil rice in a pot and cook it upto 80 percent cooked.
  2. Now add different colors to the boiled rice and set a side.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry nuts. Keep aside.
  4. Now heat up some oil and add rice to it layer it up with sugar and add nuts now add some more rice layer it up with sugar and nuts and keep it on low flame until sugar is melted.
  5. Now mix the rice and serve. Delicious to taste.
