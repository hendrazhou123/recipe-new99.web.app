The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Sweet Potato üç† Black Bean Chilli:
  1. Get 4-5 pounds sweet potatoes (about 5 big sweet potatoes)
  2. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  3. Take 2 tablespoons chilli powder
  4. Take 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  5. Take 1 can (28 ounce) crushed tomatoes
  6. Take 1 can (16 ounce) refried beans
  7. Make ready 2 cans (15.5 ounce) black beans, undrained
  8. Get 1 can (14.5 ounce) chicken broth (low sodium)
  9. Get 2 onions, sliced
  10. Make ready 1 green pepper, chopped
  11. Make ready 6 cloves garlic, minced
  12. Take Shredded cheddar cheese
  13. Make ready Sour cream

Steps to make Crockpot Sweet Potato üç† Black Bean Chilli:
  1. Rinse and peel your sweet potatoes, then cut into chunks….
  2. Place sweet potato chunks into large crockpot…….
  3. Add to sweet potatoes your black pepper, chilli powder and ground cumin…..
  4. Now add your crushed tomatoes, refried beans, black beans and chicken broth…..
  5. Now add your sliced onions, green pepper and minced garlic, stir well….
  6. Cover and cook on Low heat for 8 hours, stirring occasionally…..
  7. Serve topped with shredded cheddar cheese and sour cream and enjoy üòâ!!!
