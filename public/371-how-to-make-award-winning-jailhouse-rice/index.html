The ingredients needed to make Jailhouse rice:
  1. Prepare 1 tsp olive oil
  2. Make ready 1 1/2-2 lbs ground turkey
  3. Take 1/2 large or 1 medium onion chopped
  4. Get 3-4 celery stalks chopped
  5. Prepare 1 can mixed veggies (optional)
  6. Prepare 1 quart (4 cups) chicken broth
  7. Prepare 1 1/2-1 3/4 cups parboiled rice
  8. Take 1 can cream of celery soup- healthy request
  9. Get to taste Adobo seasoning
  10. Take to taste Garlic powder
  11. Make ready to taste Dried parsley
  12. Make ready to taste Salt & pepper

Instructions to make Jailhouse rice:
  1. Sautée the celery and onions in olive oil til translucent
  2. Add the ground turkey and brown. Add seasoning at this point. No need to drain. Add the broth and bring to a boil. Add the rice and simmer for 18 min
  3. The last min it’s cooking add the can of cream of celery and the can of veggies. Enjoy!
