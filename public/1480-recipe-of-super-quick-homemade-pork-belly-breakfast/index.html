The ingredients needed to make Pork Belly Breakfast:
  1. Take Grits———————
  2. Make ready 1-1/2 cup sharp cheddar cheese shredded
  3. Prepare 4 cup water
  4. Make ready 1 cup grits
  5. Take 1/2 stick butter
  6. Get 1 teaspoon kosher salt, for grits
  7. Get Eggs—————
  8. Get 2-1/2 tablespoons butter
  9. Make ready 6 large eggs
  10. Prepare To taste kosher salt
  11. Prepare Pork Belly————-
  12. Make ready 1/4 pound pork belly
  13. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  14. Get 1/2 teaspoon ground sage

Steps to make Pork Belly Breakfast:
  1. Heat the water and add the salt. When boiling add the grits. Boil till done stirring often so as to not having lumps. Add the butter and cheese.
  2. Stir in and Cover off heat.
  3. Cut the pork belly fry in a pan with salt and sage.
  4. Turn as needed and season the second side.
  5. Fry crispy
  6. Beat the eggs with salt and add the butter to the pan and fry till done. Serve I hope you enjoy!!
