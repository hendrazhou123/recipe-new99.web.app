The ingredients needed to make Cheese macroni:
  1. Get 250 gms macroni
  2. Make ready 500 ml water
  3. Take As required Mozzarella cheese
  4. Make ready As per taste Pizza pasta sauce
  5. Take 1 tsp Herbs and chilli flakes
  6. Prepare 2 onions
  7. Take 4 garlic

Steps to make Cheese macroni:
  1. Boil water add 1 tablespoon of oil in it.
  2. Put macaroni in boil water, let it boil till it gets soft.
  3. Heat oil in a different pan add 2 tablespoons of oil.
  4. Add chopped garlic and onion, let them cook well. Till they get translucent.
  5. Add pizza pasta sauce, 3 tablespoons, mix it well add some herbs, chilli flakes and salt as per taste.
  6. Now add boiled macaroni in it mix well, if you find the sauce is less then you can add more sauce at this stage also.
  7. Now grate mozzarella cheese mix it well and serve hot. Macaroni is ready to eat. Small children can also enjoy this as this is not spicy
  8. You can add spices if you want to eat spicy. It's taste is awesome try it once.
