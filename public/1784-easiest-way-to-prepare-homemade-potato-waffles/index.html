The ingredients needed to make Potato waffles:
  1. Take 4 boiled potatoes
  2. Make ready 1 cup chopped onion
  3. Get 2-3 green chili chopped
  4. Make ready Half cup boiled peas
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp rock salt
  6. Get 1 tsp red chili powder
  7. Prepare 1 tsp black pepper
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp garam masala
  9. Get 1 tbsp chaat masala
  10. Get 1 tsp kasoori methi
  11. Get 1 tsp cumin powder
  12. Prepare 1 tsp coriander powder
  13. Make ready slices Bread
  14. Take Ketchup
  15. Take Butter
  16. Prepare Mayo

Steps to make Potato waffles:
  1. Ketchup and mayo is optional to spread on one side of each slice of inside
  2. Mix all the ingredients and spices into mashed potatoes and mix well except ketchup and mayo
  3. Spread ketchup on one slice of bread on one side and spread mayo on second slice of bread on one side only and grease butter on other side
  4. Fill the mixture on ketchup greased side and join it to mayo spread side and grease butter on outside layer of waffle and exactly this way prepare the other waffle and roast it in waffle maker
