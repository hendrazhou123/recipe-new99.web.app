The ingredients needed to make Folded Browned Folded Eggs with Spam Sandwich:
  1. Make ready 4 ounces thinly sliced spam
  2. Prepare 3 slices bread
  3. Take 3 teaspoons mayonnaise
  4. Make ready 3 large eggs
  5. Prepare To taste salt
  6. Prepare 2 tablespoons butter

Steps to make Folded Browned Folded Eggs with Spam Sandwich:
  1. Toast the bread and set aside. Add mayonnaise to 3 slices.
  2. Crack the eggs, add butter to skillet, and beat the eggs.
  3. Add eggs to the skillet. The browned butter will add a deeper flavor to the eggs. Cook till done divide and fold them. Add to two slices of the bread.
  4. Slice the spam into 4 slices. Fry in the skillet. Once they are caramelized its done.
  5. Add two slices to the eggs.
  6. Repeat and then stack them on top of each other.
  7. Add final slice and serve, I hope you enjoy!!!!!!
