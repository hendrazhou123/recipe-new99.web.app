The ingredients needed to make Spiced Ram Meat:
  1. Take Clean cut ram meat
  2. Get 2 tbsp paprika
  3. Get 2 tbsp all spice
  4. Take 1 tbsp tumeric powder
  5. Get 1 tbsp curry
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp thyme
  7. Prepare 4 tbsp ground Scotch bonnet
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp ginger and garlic paste
  9. Take 1 medium size diced onion
  10. Take 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  11. Take to taste Seasoning
  12. Take 1 tbsp salt

Instructions to make Spiced Ram Meat:
  1. Clean meat properly with water, drain and keep in bowl
  2. Arrange all ingredients
  3. Add all the ingredients at a time into the bowl of meat and mix together with clean hands to marinate
  4. Cover with foil paper, and put in fridge for few hours (but mine was overnight)
  5. Remove from fridge, turn into pot cover and set on medium flame to cook soft
  6. Note: I did not add water
  7. Remove from put if already cooked(soft). Enjoy cooked or Fry as desired
