Steps to make Minced meat sticks:
  1. Marinate the minced meat with spices and seasoning of your choice and put it in fridge for 1-2 hours.
  2. Mix your marinated minced meat withScotch bonnet,habanero,onions. Bind with a beaten egg and corn flour. Shape into croquettes with your hands and insert on skewer(I divide the skewers in to two)shallow fry in a non stick pan turning both sides till well fried.
  3. Garnish it with mint leaves and a mixture of ketchup and mayonnaise.
  4. Serve with food of your choice.
