The ingredients needed to make Mediterranean Rice (rice cooker):
  1. Prepare 300 gr basmati rice
  2. Prepare 575 ml chicken broth
  3. Get 3/4 tsp salt
  4. Get 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  5. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon clove powder
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp nutmeg powder
  7. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  8. Take to taste Pepper powder
  9. Get 2 bay leaves
  10. Make ready As needed frozen mix vegetable

Instructions to make Mediterranean Rice (rice cooker):
  1. Wash the rice. Drain then put it into a rice cooker.
  2. Add all remaining ingredients except mix vegetables. Stir well. Taste, add salt if it is less salty.
  3. Press the button to cook rice. Wait until the rice is cooked, then put frozen vegetables into it. Mix well, cover the rice cooker, leave it in the "warm" button for 5-10 minutes.
