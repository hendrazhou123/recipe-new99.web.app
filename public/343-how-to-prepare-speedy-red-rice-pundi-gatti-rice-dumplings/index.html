The ingredients needed to make Red rice Pundi Gatti (Rice dumplings):
  1. Take 3 cups red rice or white rice
  2. Prepare 0.25 cup thick puffed rice or poha
  3. Take 3 tsp coconut oil
  4. Prepare 0.25 cup shredded coconut
  5. Take 2 tsp mustard seeds
  6. Make ready 1 tsp Urrad dal
  7. Make ready 1 tsp Channa dal
  8. Make ready 0.5 tsp fenugreek seeds
  9. Get 3 Red chilli
  10. Take 0.25 tsp hing
  11. Make ready leaves Curry
  12. Get leaves Coriander
  13. Take 1 tsp jaggery
  14. Make ready Salt

Steps to make Red rice Pundi Gatti (Rice dumplings):
  1. Soak red rice + thick puffed rice in water for 5-7 hours
  2. Now blend it in mixer with less water
  3. In a pan take oil + mustard seeds + urrad dal + Channa dal + fenugreek seeds + curry leaves + red chilli + cumin seeds and sauté well. Now add blended mixture to it and 0.5 cup water and mix well. Add coconut and coriander leaves.
  4. Let it cool for around 15 minutes. Now take small portions of it and make round balls. Place it in a container gland steam it(Add water in the base and place a plate on top) for around 15 minutes.
  5. Serve the steamed pundi with coconut chutney
