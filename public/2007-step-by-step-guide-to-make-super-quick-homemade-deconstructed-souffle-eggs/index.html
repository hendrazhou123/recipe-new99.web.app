The ingredients needed to make Deconstructed Soufflé Eggs:
  1. Prepare 4 eggs separate yolk and white
  2. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  3. Get 1 tsp pepper
  4. Take Basil
  5. Prepare Dried red chilli
  6. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
  7. Take 1/3 butter

Instructions to make Deconstructed Soufflé Eggs:
  1. Whisk egg yolk until medium peaks add sugar and salt
  2. On a separate bowl, beat egg yolks and add pepper
  3. Fry in butter separately
  4. Plate, add basil and Chili then serve!
