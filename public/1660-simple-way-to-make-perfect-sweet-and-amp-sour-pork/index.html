The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Sour Pork:
  1. Prepare Adobo cut or Pork liempo
  2. Get Onion
  3. Prepare Capsicum
  4. Get 1 tsp Chinese Five Spice
  5. Prepare Marinade
  6. Get Soy sauce
  7. Take Garlic powder
  8. Take Ground black pepper
  9. Get Sauce
  10. Make ready Soy sauce
  11. Prepare White vinegar
  12. Take Brown sugar
  13. Get Tomato ketchup
Instructions to make Sweet & Sour Pork:
  1. Remove the skin part of the pork
  2. Marinate the pork with the marinade ingredients. Set aside.
  3. Heat pan with oil. Saute onion. Add the sliced capsicum.
  4. Pour in the sauce mixture
  5. Mix well. Add the chinese five spice.
  6. Let the mixture simmer for 2 mins.
  7. In a separate pan, fry the marinated pork until meat turns golden brown.
  8. Add the cooked pork into the sauce mixture.
