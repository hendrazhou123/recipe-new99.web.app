The ingredients needed to make Fried goat meat:
  1. Take 1 fullFresh Goat meat washed and well seasoned

  5. Get to taste Salt
  6. Prepare 6 knorr cubes
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp Mixed spices
  8. Take 1/2 tbsp Curry and thyme
  9. Make ready Oil for frying
