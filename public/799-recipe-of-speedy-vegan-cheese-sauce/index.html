The ingredients needed to make Vegan Cheese Sauce:
  1. Get 1 Med Onion chopped
  2. Take 1 Head Garlic chopped
  3. Take 3/4 cup Veggie Stock
  4. Prepare 1 Tbsp Miso white
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup Veggie Stock
  6. Take 1/3 cup Nutritional Yeast
  7. Make ready 1/2 Tsp Turmeric
  8. Take 1/2 Tsp Pepper
  9. Get 1/2 Tsp Sweet Paprika
  10. Make ready 1/2 Tsp Cumin
  11. Make ready 1/2 Tsp Chilli Powder
  12. Prepare 2 med cooked potatoes

Steps to make Vegan Cheese Sauce:
  1. Saute 1st 3 ingredients 5 min.
  2. Whisk next 8 ingredients together.
  3. Add spice mix to saute pan. Simmer 5 min or so.
  4. Add all ingredients plus 2 cooked potatoes to blender and puree til smooth and creamy
  5. Enjoy over potatoes or steamed vegetables.
