The ingredients needed to make Sauteed Kale with Crispy Salted Mackerel Fish:
  1. Get 1-2 pcs Kale
  2. Prepare 1 slice Dried Salted Mackerel Fish (thick 1 cm.)
  3. Make ready 3 pcs Red Chili (smashed)
  4. Prepare 1 tsp White Soy Sauce
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp Oyster Sauce
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp Maggi Sauce
  7. Get 1 tsp Sugar
  8. Prepare 2 drops Black Soy Sauce
  9. Get 3 cloves Garlic (smashed)
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp Water

Instructions to make Sauteed Kale with Crispy Salted Mackerel Fish:

  2. Put vegetable oil in pan then add garlic, fry until scent.
  3. Add kale and seasoning with white soy sauce, oyster sauce, maggi sauce and water. Stir well and fast.
  4. Add salted fish and red chili, stir little and put in serving plate.
