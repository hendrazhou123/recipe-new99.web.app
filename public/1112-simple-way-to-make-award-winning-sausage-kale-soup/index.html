The ingredients needed to make Sausage Kale Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 medium yellow or sweet onion chopped
  2. Take 1/2 of a Kale bunch (I like Italian) chopped
  3. Take 1/4 of a whole Cabbage chopped
  4. Prepare Carrots chopped
  5. Get 3-4 garlic cloves minced
  6. Prepare 1 can heat roasted diced tomatoes
  7. Take 1 /2 pound sausage (sweet) buy bulk
  8. Get 1/2 pound sausage (hot) buy bulk
  9. Make ready 8 cups chicken broth or whatever broth
  10. Get evoo
  11. Make ready Parmesan

Instructions to make Sausage Kale Soup:
  1. Sauté onion for 2-3 minutes in evoo. Add sausage, break apart as it cooks. Cook thru, remove from skillet/Dutch oven onto paper towels to soak up fat.
  2. Throw in chopped kale, cabbage and carrots (think! You want bite size so make sure you chop well.) Toss well, sauté with yummy pan drippings for 3-4 minutes. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Add sausage back in and toss with veggies. Add in heat roasted tomatoes, toss, and finally add broth. Stir all together, bring to boil and simmer 20 minutes.
  4. I like to add Parmesan to a serving, or see my Vodka Pink Sauce recipe. You can add a spoonful into your soup serving for next level flavor!
