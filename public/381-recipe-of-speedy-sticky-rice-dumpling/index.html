The ingredients needed to make Sticky Rice Dumpling:
  1. Take 5 cups sticky rice
  2. Prepare 1 salted egg yolk
  3. Take Red bean
  4. Get Pork or chicken meat

Steps to make Sticky Rice Dumpling:
  1. Cook pork or chicken in light soy sauce, garlic, black soy sauce. Set aside
  2. Boil the red bean. Boil also the salted egg. Then just used the yolk
  3. In a leaf put sticky rice first then mix all ing in the middle then cover with rice again. Tie the leaves tightly so that it will not come out the rice. Steam for 30 to 40 mins
