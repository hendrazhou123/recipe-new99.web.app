The ingredients needed to make Beef Bulgogi (불고기):
  1. Get Marinade
  2. Prepare 6 tbsp Japanese or Korean soy sauce
  3. Get 2 tbsp mirin
  4. Get 2 tbsp sugar (optional)
  5. Make ready 1/2 Korean nashi pear or 1 small red apple
  6. Take 1/2 onion (about 80 g)
  7. Get 3 garlic cloves
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  9. Get 1 tbsp sesame oil
  10. Make ready Main
  11. Get 1 kg beef, sliced into thin (approx. 3 mm) slices
  12. Get 1 onion, sliced
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
  14. Get 1 tbsp sesame oil
  15. Make ready 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  16. Take 1 carrot, julienned (optional)
  17. Get 2 stalks chopped scallions (optional)

Bulgogi - Authentic Korean Beef BBQ Recipe. Bulgogi (불고기) is a classic Korean preparation of beef or pork in which thinly shaved meat is marinated in a sweet and savory sauce, and grilled on a barbecue or on a stove-top griddle.

Steps to make Beef Bulgogi (불고기):
  1. Combine all marinade ingredients except the sesame oil in a blender. Puree until smooth. Pour marinade over beef and add sesame oil. Mix thoroughly, and let marinate for 4 hours at room temperature or overnight in the refrigerator.
  2. Set a skillet on medium high heat with both the vegetable and sesame oil until hot but not smoking. Fry the meat and vegetables for 3–5 minutes or until cooked to desired level of doneness. Toss in sesame seeds and give a quick stir.
  3. Serve with steamed rice and Korean side dishes (e.g. kimchi).
