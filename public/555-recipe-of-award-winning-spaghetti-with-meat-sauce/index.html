The ingredients needed to make Spaghetti with Meat Sauce:
  1. Get 1 lb spaghetti
  2. Get 1 lb ground beef
  3. Prepare 1 onion
  4. Get 4 garlic cloves, minced
  5. Take 8 button mushrooms, sliced
  6. Get 28 oz canned diced tomatoes (2 cans)
  7. Prepare 15 oz tomato sauce (1 can)
  8. Make ready 6 oz tomato paste (1 can)
  9. Get 2 tsp dried oregano
  10. Prepare 2 tsp dried basil
  11. Take to taste Kosher salt, pepper
  12. Make ready Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions to make Spaghetti with Meat Sauce:
  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil and add garlic, onions, and mushrooms. Toss gently for a few minutes until vegetables are softened
  2. Add ground beef and season beef with salt and pepper. Sear until meat is browned.
  3. Drain grease from the pan, then add diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, and basil. Stir until combined, then simmer for at least an hour
  4. Cook spaghetti per boxed instructions. Drain pasta, then add to meat sauce.
  5. Serve hot and top with fresh grated parmesan cheese
