The ingredients needed to make Cheese fondue:
  1. Prepare 1 cup milk scald
  2. Take 1 cup stale bread crumbs
  3. Make ready 1 cup mild cheese cut in small pieces
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon butter
  5. Make ready 3 egg yolks
  6. Get 3 egg whites
  7. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
  8. Make ready Pepper
  9. Get Paprika

Steps to make Cheese fondue:
  1. Mix milk bread crumbs cheese butter and salt together add well beaten egg yolks
  2. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites pour in buttered baking dish bake about 20 minutes in 350 oven
  3. Add paprika before serving
