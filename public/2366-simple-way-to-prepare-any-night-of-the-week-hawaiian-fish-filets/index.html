Steps to make Hawaiian fish filets:
  1. Chop green pepper and drain cans of fruit into a measuring cup adding water if necessary to make 2 cups.
  2. In a saucepan combine and bring to boil these ingredients stirring constantly - frequently until thickened. About 15-20 minutes.
  3. While sauce is thickening, add fish filets to frying pan with butter, season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and a splash of soy sauce. Cook thoroughly and cover while the sauce finishes.
  4. Once sauce is thickened add canned fruit and green pepper and continue simmering for an additional 15-20 minutes.
  5. Prepare rice and when all is done serve the sauce over the fish and a bed of rice.
