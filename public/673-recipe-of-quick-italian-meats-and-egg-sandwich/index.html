The ingredients needed to make Italian Meats and Egg Sandwich:
  1. Take 4 slices cotto salami luncheon meat
  2. Get 1/3 pound pepperoni
  3. Prepare 2 large eggs
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon chopped parsley flakes
  5. Get 1/2 teaspoon basil
  6. Get As needed tomato slices
  7. Take To taste salt
  8. Prepare To taste ground black pepper
  9. Prepare 3 slices bread
  10. Get 1/4 pound Bacon
  11. Get 1/3 pound hot Italian sausage
  12. Take 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  13. Get 1 medium sized kosher pickle sliced lengthwise
