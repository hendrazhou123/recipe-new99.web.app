The ingredients needed to make Chinese Homestyle Pork Belly:
  1. Make ready 3/4 pound pork belly cut into thin slices
  2. Make ready 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
  3. Take 1/2 tbsp light soy sauce
  4. Get 1/2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp onion powder
  6. Make ready 1/8 tsp white pepper powder
  7. Get 2 stalks green onion cut into 1 inch sections

Instructions to make Chinese Homestyle Pork Belly:
  1. Marinate the pork belly slices with all the ingredients, except green onion, for at least one hour.
  2. Wrap the pork in a lightly greased aluminum foil. Air fry at 400F (200C) for 8 minutes.
  3. Unwrap the foil, stir the pork slices and air fry at 380F (190C) for about 4 minutes until the meat is cooked through.
  4. Add in the green onion and air fry again at 380F (190C) for 1-2 minute.
