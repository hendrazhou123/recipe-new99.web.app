The ingredients needed to make Pineapple cheese ball:
  1. Get 2 (8 oz) packages cream cheese softened
  2. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
  3. Get 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  4. Make ready 1-2 (8 oz) cans of crushed pineapple drained well
  5. Get 1 bag (2 cups) shredded sharp cheese
  6. Prepare 2 tablespoons diced pimentos
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup minced green green onion
  8. Make ready 1-2 jalapeños seeds & membranes removed finely minced optional
  9. Make ready 1 cup chopped toasted pecans divided
  10. Get Ritz Crackers

Steps to make Pineapple cheese ball:
  1. To toast pecans. Place on a cookie sheet in a single layer. Toast in a preheated 350°F oven for 6-8 minutes. Cool completely]
  2. With a mixer beat cream cheese, salt and pepper together
  3. Then mix in 1/2 cup pecans and then mix in rest till well mixed
  4. Roll into a ball and lightly coat outside with other 1/2 cup pecans and wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight.
  5. Enjoy with ritzs crackers
