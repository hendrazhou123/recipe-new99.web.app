The ingredients needed to make Slow cooked Fish and Sweet Potato Curry:
  1. Take 400 grams firm white fish fillets (eg. Cod, basa, flake)
  2. Make ready 3 cloves garlic, diced
  3. Prepare 3 cm piece of ginger, finely diced
  4. Take 3 small hot red chillies (or to taste)
  5. Make ready 1 medium sweet potaoto (cut into bite size chunks)
  6. Take 7 x pieces baby corn
  7. Make ready 1 x tin coconut cream
  8. Get 2 tablespoons your favourite curry powder (or make your own!)
  9. Prepare Yoghurt and bread (to serve)

Steps to make Slow cooked Fish and Sweet Potato Curry:
  1. Cut your fish into large bite-sized pieces, noting that they might break down in the slow-cooker if too small.
  2. Place all ingredients in a (non preheated slow cooker). Stir very well, making sure the curry powder is mixed through and that he coconut milk is liquid.
  3. Turn slow-cooker on to a low setting. Let sit on a low heat for 3 - 4 hours, or for 30minutes after you can hear it starting to boil.
  4. Serve on bread with yoghurt, enjoy
