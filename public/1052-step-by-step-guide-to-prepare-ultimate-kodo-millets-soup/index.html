The ingredients needed to make Kodo Millets Soup:
  1. Get 1 small cup of Kodo Millets
  2. Get 2 cups water
  3. Prepare to taste salt
  4. Take 1 tsp pepper powder
  5. Get 1 carrot
  6. Make ready 1/2 beetroot
  7. Make ready 7 beans

Steps to make Kodo Millets Soup:
  1. Coarsely grind the Kodo Millets and soak overnight
  2. In the morning in a large bowl take water and add millets and all abovementioned veggies.
  3. Boil for half an hour add salt and pepper and serve hot.
  4. May add more vegetables as per your taste.
