The ingredients needed to make Turnip Meat:
  1. Get 1/2 kg Beef
  2. Prepare 1/2 turnip
  3. Make ready 1.5 tsp Red chilli powder
  4. Get 1 tsp turmeric powder
  5. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  6. Get 1/2 tsp All spice's powder
  7. Prepare 2 tsp coriander powder

  9. Prepare 1 Onion Medium size sliced
  10. Get 1/4 cup yoghurt
  11. Take 1/2 cup oil
  12. Make ready 2 Green chillies
  13. Get coriander strands for garnishing
  14. Prepare 1 Glass water for meet tender
