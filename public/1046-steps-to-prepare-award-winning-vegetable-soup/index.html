The ingredients needed to make Vegetable Soup:
  1. Take 1/2 stick butter
  2. Make ready 3 cups potato pieces
  3. Get 2 cups baby carrots sliced in half
  4. Get 3 cups sliced yellow Crook-necked squash
  5. Make ready 1 quart water
  6. Prepare 1-1/2 teaspoon salt
  7. Take 2-1/2 cups chopped cabbage
  8. Get 1-1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  9. Make ready To taste red wine vinegar or vinegar hot sauce optional
  10. Prepare To taste grated Parmesan cheese optional

Instructions to make Vegetable Soup:
  1. The potato pieces were the parts I cut away from my chateau potatoes. Slice the carrots, slice the squash, and chop the cabbage. Add to a pot with the water and salt.
  2. Boil the vegetables for 30-40 minutes. Add water if necessary. This is where the butter is added. Simmer 15 minutes covered. Serve add red wine vinegar/ hotsauce, if you desire. Add cheese if using. I hope you enjoy!!!
