The ingredients needed to make Beef bihari boti:
  1. Take 1 kg Undercut steak pieces
  2. Prepare 100 grams Yoghurt
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp Papaya paste
  4. Take 1 Onion (fried and ground)
  5. Get 2 tbsp Ginger/garlic paste
  6. Make ready 1 tsp Roasted and crushed cumin seeds
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp National Tikka boti masala
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Crushed red chillies
  9. Take 1 tsp Hot spices powder
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  11. Take 4 tbsp Roasted & ground Bengal grams
  12. Prepare 4 tbsp Mustard oil
  13. Get to taste Salt

Steps to make Beef bihari boti:
  1. Marinate steak pieces with papaya paste for at least 4 hours.
  2. Mix steak pieces, onion, yoghurt, ginger/garlic, mustard oil, turmeric powder, hot spices powder, red chilies, Nation Tikka boti masala, cumin seeds, grams and salt in a bowl.
  3. After 1 hour you can either put them on skewers and cook them over coals, steam and fry them You can also cook it in the oven..
  4. Garnish with sliced lemon, coriander leaves, green chilies and sliced onion.
  5. Serve delicious Bihari Boti with ‘roti, salad, raita, national ketchup and tamarind/mint chtuney.
