The ingredients needed to make Momma’s Tater Salad:
  1. Prepare About 4 medium potatoes or 2# red potatoes
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 cups your favorite mayonnaise (give or take on how you like)
  3. Prepare 4 tablespoons yellow or Dijon mustard
  4. Make ready 1/2 red onion chopped fine
  5. Take 1/2 sweet onion chopped fine
  6. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon black pepper (or to taste)
  7. Get 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika (or regular if you’re out)
  8. Make ready 20 (or so) slice dill (or favorite) pickle chopped
  9. Prepare 2 tablespoons (3ish for me) pickle juice
  10. Get 4 boil eggs chopped
  11. Get 3 celery stalks chopped fine
  12. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon onion powder
  13. Make ready 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder

Instructions to make Momma’s Tater Salad:
  1. Chop potatoes bite size, boil with teaspoon salt until fork tender, not mushy
  2. In a bowl add chopped eggs, celery,onions, pickles, mix, add pepper paprika, garlic and onion powder, fold in mayonnaise, pickle juice, mustard
  3. After potatoes are done drain and cool when cool put in big bowl and fold in mayonnaise mixture
  4. Sprinkle paprika on top and chill couple hours or over night
