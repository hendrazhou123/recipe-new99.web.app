The ingredients needed to make Steak, Onions, and Eggs:
  1. Prepare Steak
  2. Prepare 1 pound leftover bottom round steak see my recipe
  3. Get 1/2 cup beef broth
  4. Prepare 1 medium onion
  5. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
  6. Take 1-1/2 tablespoons butter
  7. Make ready Eggs
  8. Make ready 4 large eggs
  9. Prepare To taste seasoned salt
  10. Take To taste ground black pepper
  11. Take 1-1/2 tablespoons butter
  12. Make ready As needed water to steam the whites

Instructions to make Steak, Onions, and Eggs:
  1. Melt the butter in a skillet. When butter melts, turn down heat and add the eggs. Add seasonings, when the bottom of whites turn white add water to steam the tops of the whites and cover.
  2. When just the white are done remove to a plate. Melt the butter. Slice the steaks. Dice the onions
  3. When butter is melted add the onion and season. When the onions turn translucent add the steak and beef broth. Let the liquids reduce. Serve I hope you enjoy!!!
