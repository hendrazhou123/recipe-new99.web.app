The ingredients needed to make Simple rice porridge:
  1. Prepare 1 clove garlic, minced (optional)
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp oil (optional)
  3. Take 1 cup cooked rice
  4. Prepare water
  5. Take 1/4 tsp dashi powder or ½ chicken broth cube
  6. Prepare 1 tsp soy sauce (optional)
  7. Take 1 egg (optional)

Instructions to make Simple rice porridge:
  1. Heat the oil in a small saucepan under medium heat until hot but not smoking. Sauté the garlic for one minute. You can skip this step for a lighter flavoured porridge.
  2. Add the rice and just enough water to cover it. Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add dashi powder/chicken broth cube, stir until it dissolves. Stir in soy sauce. Reduce heat to low and let simmer for a couple of minutes.
  4. Optionally, add an egg, stir it into the porridge and let it poach. Serve hot.
