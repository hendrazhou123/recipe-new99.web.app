The ingredients needed to make Sausage and Yellow Rice Soup:
  1. Get 3-1/2 quarts water
  2. Prepare 1 tablespoon saffron threads
  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
  4. Get 24 ounces smoked beef sausage
  5. Prepare 1/2 stick butter
  6. Get 3 envelopes yellow rice 1 pound total
  7. Take 1 cup petite English peas

Steps to make Sausage and Yellow Rice Soup:
  1. In a pot melt the butter. Add the salt, saffron, and water.
  2. Remove casing from the sausage if any. Slice the sausage into medallion pieces. Add to the pot when it boils.
  3. Add the rice envelopes. Stir well and simmer for 45 minutes. Stirring as needed.
  4. Add the English peas. Allow to rest 7 minutes covered. Serve I hope you enjoy!!!
