The ingredients needed to make Pork Chili Verde:
  1. Make ready 2 lb Smoked pork butt
  2. Prepare 6 tbsp Flour
  3. Take 2 can Diced tomatoes
  4. Get 2 can Cream of chicken soup
  5. Get 2 can Water
  6. Make ready 2 cup Green chiles, roasted, peeled, chopped
  7. Prepare 6 clove Garlic
  8. Get 1 Jalepenos, roasted, peeled, chopped
  9. Prepare 4 Tomatillos
  10. Take 1 Onion, chopped
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp Chili powder
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp Cumin
  13. Make ready 2 tsp Salt
  14. Prepare 1 Red potatoes (optional)

Steps to make Pork Chili Verde:
  1. Brown pork in a little bit of oil or bacon grease, coat with flour and brown some more.
  2. Add tomatoes and 2 cans of water. Add soup and 2 more cans of water.
  3. Add peppers, garlic and the rest of the ingredients.
  4. Simmer on the stove top or cook all day in a crock pot.
