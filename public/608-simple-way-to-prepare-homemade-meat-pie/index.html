Steps to make Meat pie:
  1. Place a pot on fire,wash beef and put inside the pot add 1maggi cube,thyme and onions,cover and allow to cook until soft
  2. Take off the cooked beef,onions, scotch bonnet and ground it in a mortar
  3. In a clean bowl add in your flour, baking powder and pinch of salt,mix all ingredients together and add water,mix and foam a dough
  4. Use your rolling pin and board to roll the dough cut into circle place each circle on a meat pie cutter then you put the filling and close it
  5. Rub butter on a baking tray and dust it with flour then you arrange the meat pie. On the oven to preheat for 5-7mnts
  6. Egg wash the pies an place the tray and bake for 20mnts on medium to low heat
  7. Serve
