The ingredients needed to make Broccoli Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 tablespoon oil
  2. Take 1 onion (chopped)
  3. Get 1 carrot (chopped)
  4. Take 2 cloves garlic (chopped)
  5. Take 1 large potato (cut into chunks)
  6. Take 2 broccoli (cut into florets)
  7. Get 500 ml vegetable or chicken broth
  8. Get 1/4 cup evaporated milk (optional)
  9. Prepare Salt and pepper

Instructions to make Broccoli Soup:
  1. Heat up the oil.
  2. Saute the garlic, onion, carrot, potato until veggies develop some colour.
  3. Add the broccolis and cover with stock. Allow it to simmer until potato and broccoli softens.
  4. Blend the soup.
  5. Add evaporated milk and heat for about one minute.
