The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Sour Fish:
  1. Make ready Sauce
  2. Take 4 cups Water
  3. Take Sugar (preferably white)
  4. Prepare Tomato Ketchup
  5. Get White Vinegar
  6. Get Bell pepper(red and green)
  7. Prepare Onion
  8. Take Carrots (optional)
  9. Get Corn starch mix w/ water
  10. Make ready Salt
  11. Prepare White pepper
  12. Make ready Main Ingredient
  13. Prepare Fish
  14. Take Salt
Instructions to make Sweet & Sour Fish:
  1. Boil water and mix all liquid ingredients (sauce ingredients). Let it simmer.
  2. Add the solid ingredients (sauce section).
  3. Season fish with salt and fry in a pan with heated oil. Set aside.
  4. Pour the sauce into the cooked fish.
