The ingredients needed to make Baked Pork Chops with Yummy Sauce:
  1. Prepare 2 pork chops with bone
  2. Get 2 cans cream of mushroom
  3. Take 1 handful fresh mushrooms cut up
  4. Make ready 1 small onion cut up
  5. Make ready 1 can milk
  6. Get Salt
  7. Make ready Pepper
  8. Get Onion powder
  9. Take Garlic powder

Steps to make Baked Pork Chops with Yummy Sauce:
  1. Cut up the mushrooms and onions and set a side… and pre head the oven too 400
  2. In a casserole dish add the cream of mushroom and the can of milk mix well.
  3. Add the salt pepper garlic and onion and mix well. Add the fresh mushrooms and onion to it. But dont mix it.
  4. Get the pork chops add salt and pepper to both side and place it on top.
  5. Put them in the oven for about 30 mins.
  6. Take them out and flip them add the mixture to the top and put them back in for 10 mins.
