The ingredients needed to make Panfried Paprika Ayu Fish || Hot Fried Fish:
  1. Prepare Fresh Ayu fish pieces (cleansed)
  2. Prepare 1/2 tea spn tumeric
  3. Prepare 1 tea spn curry powder
  4. Get 1/2 tea spns chilli
  5. Get 1/2 tea spn black pepper
  6. Make ready 2 lemons
  7. Take 1 tea spn paprika
  8. Prepare Salt to taste

Steps to make Panfried Paprika Ayu Fish || Hot Fried Fish:
  1. Place the washed fish pieces in a bowl, add the tumeric, curry powder, chilli, black pepper, paprika and mix the ingredients to incorporate all flavors.
  2. Add about a tea spoon of salt and squeeze a whole lemon, all over the fish pieces evenly. Mix slightly.
  3. Cover the bowl with a cling film and place the fish pieces in a fridge to marinate for about a 1/2 an hour. This allows all the flavors to slowly incorporate into the meat.
  4. Take the bowl out the fridge after 30 minutes. In a pan, heat cooking oil just above medium, place the fish and fry into some crusty crispiness and some tender cooked interior.
  5. Drain the fish on a paper towel. Dust the fish with some rosemary leaves & paprika and you know what, enjoy that tasty fish!
  6. Serve with ugali, rice with stew, make some fish wraps…do it how you like it!
