The ingredients needed to make MeatZa:
  1. Take 500 grams beef mince
  2. Prepare 3 clove garlic, chopped
  3. Get 1 onion, chopped
  4. Take 1 dash salt
  5. Prepare 1 dash pepper
  6. Take 1 dash dried oregano
  7. Take 1 dash paprika
  8. Take 1 dash chili flakes
  9. Take 1 egg
  10. Get 2 can chopped tomatoes, drained
  11. Prepare 1/2 eggplant
  12. Take 1/2 cup oats or breadcrumbs
  13. Take 100 grams cheese, grated

Steps to make MeatZa:
  1. Preheat oven to 200ºC
  2. Mix meat with garlic, onion, egg, herbs, spices and oats/breadcrumbs
  3. Press mixture flat in a baking tin
  4. Spread out the tomatoes on top
  5. Top with thinly sliced eggplant and finish off with cheese
  6. Bake 25 minutes
