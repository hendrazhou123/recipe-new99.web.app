The ingredients needed to make Black Bean Soup:
  1. Make ready 2 cups beef broth
  2. Take 2 cups water
  3. Prepare 1 cup diced peppers
  4. Make ready 1 cup diced tomatoes
  5. Get 2 cans black beans rinsed
  6. Make ready 1 can corn
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup salsa
  8. Make ready 1 tbs cumin
  9. Get 1 tbs chili powder
  10. Take 1 tbs basil
  11. Take 1 cup uncooked shell pasta

Instructions to make Black Bean Soup:
  1. Put all ingredients in pot except the pasta and bring to a boil.
  2. Once it comes to a full boil turn to medium and simmer for 20 mins or until peppers are soft.
  3. Add in pasta and simmer for 10 mins.
  4. Turn off burner cover pot and let sit for 10 mins so pasta will be soft.
  5. Enjoy this makes 4 2 cup servings.
