The ingredients needed to make Sweet Potato Brownies:
  1. Take 1 1/3 cup steamed and mashed sweet potato
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup oat flour
  3. Get 3/4 cup cacao
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup agave / maple syrup
  5. Take 1/4 cup brown sugar
  6. Get 1/2 cup peanut butter
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp flaxseeds + 5tbsp water
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking soda

Instructions to make Sweet Potato Brownies:
  1. Blend 2 tbsp flaxseeds with 5 tbsp water in a high speed blender for a "vegan egg".
  2. In a bowl mix all ingredients until just combined.
  3. Add mixture to a lined 9x12 inch / 20x30cm backing dish.
  4. Bake at 160°C for approx 25mins.
  5. Cut into pieces and serve with whipped cream or ice cream.
