The ingredients needed to make Sausage Spaghetti Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 Tbsp butter
  2. Make ready 1/2 onion, minced
  3. Take 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Take 3 pcs beef sausages, cut 1 cm
  5. Prepare 100 gr spam, cut rectangle
  6. Prepare 1 pc boneless chicken thigh, boiled, diced
  7. Take 2 carrots, peeled, cut
  8. Prepare 100 gr frozen peas
  9. Prepare 2 tomatoes, cut into 8 wedges
  10. Make ready 1500 ml chicken stock
  11. Get 100 gr spaghetti, boiled al dente according to the package directions, set aside
  12. Make ready 2 tsp salt or to taste
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp white pepper powder
  14. Get 1/2 tsp mushroom bouillon
  15. Prepare 3/4 tsp nutmeg powder

Instructions to make Sausage Spaghetti Soup:
  1. Heat butter in a pot. Sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken stock.
  3. Add chicken, sausage, and spam. Cook until chicken stock is boiling.
  4. Add carrots, peas, salt, white pepper powder, mushroom bouillon, and nutmeg powder. Keep boiling until the vegetables are cooked. Test the taste. Remove from the heat.
  5. Place spaghetti in the bowls. Pour the sausage soup before serving. Yum! 😋
