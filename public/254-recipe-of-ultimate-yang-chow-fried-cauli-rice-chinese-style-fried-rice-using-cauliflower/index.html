The ingredients needed to make Yang Chow Fried Cauli Rice - Chinese Style Fried Rice using Cauliflower:
  1. Prepare 1 red onion, chopped
  2. Get 5 garlic cloves, chopped
  3. Make ready 1 portion chopped mixed veggies (carrots, green beans, cabbage, white pechay)
  4. Get 1 portion cauliflower rice
  5. Prepare 1 portion chopped mixed proteins (pre-cooked or leftover: chicken, tasty sausages, shrimp, scrambled egg, pork)
  6. Prepare 2 pinches pre-fried shredded ginger
  7. Get as needed Soy Liquid Seasoning (i use Knorr),
  8. Take Few drops of Sesame Oil
  9. Get 2 Tbsp Cooking oil for sauteeing
  10. Prepare your choice of chili/spice blend (i use Shichimi Togarashi, a Japanese chili/spice mix)
  11. Make ready 1 pinch Himalayan Black Salt (optional)
  12. Make ready to taste Salt
  13. Prepare to taste Ground pepper

Instructions to make Yang Chow Fried Cauli Rice - Chinese Style Fried Rice using Cauliflower:
  1. Heat a little oil and sauté the onions and garlic in a pan until cooked.
  2. Add a little more oil and lightly fry for a few minutes: the (pre-cooked in water) sausages (choose a tasty one, it will flavor the dish. I used Hungarian sausages), chicken, shrimp, pork or whatever protein you want to add.
  3. Spread the chopped veggies on top and sprinkle some: salt, shichimi, pepper, fried ginger. Add your choice of soy sauce or liquid seasoning (I use Knorr) then sprinkle a little sesame oil. Sauté and mix well.
  4. Make sure you have squeezed out the excess water from the cauliflower rice. Add to the pan and season with black salt or salt. Mix, taste and adjust seasonings as desired by adding a few more drops of knorr or soy seasoning and shichimi/spices for more heat.
  5. Sauté until chopped veggies are cooked and flavors mixed in well. Serve hot.
