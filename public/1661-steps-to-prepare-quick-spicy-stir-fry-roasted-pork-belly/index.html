The ingredients needed to make Spicy Stir Fry Roasted Pork Belly:
  1. Take 250 g Roasted Pork Belly
  2. Make ready 3 cloves minced garlic
  3. Prepare 1/2 chopped red onion
  4. Make ready 1 handful chopped chilli
  5. Get 2 teaspoons light soy sauce
  6. Make ready 3 Tablespoons Sweet Dark Soy Sauce

Steps to make Spicy Stir Fry Roasted Pork Belly:
  1. Fry the red onion for a while.
  2. Then add garlic and fry until almost golden brown.
  3. Then add the chopped chilli and pork belly follow by soy sauces.
  4. Mix them well and it's ready
