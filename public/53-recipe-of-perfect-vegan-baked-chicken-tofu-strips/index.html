The ingredients needed to make Vegan Baked ‘Chicken’ [Tofu] Strips:
  1. Prepare 2 pcs firm tofu
  2. Take 6 tbsp all-purpose flour
  3. Make ready 6 tbsp unsweetened plant-based milk (I use soy milk)
  4. Take 3 tbsp water
  5. Prepare 1 tsp garlic powder
  6. Prepare 1 tsp paprika powder
  7. Prepare 1 cup breadcrumbs (I use Panko)
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup red hot wings sauce (or you can use BBQ sauce)
  9. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
  10. Take 1 pinch salt
  11. Get 1 pinch pepper

Instructions to make Vegan Baked ‘Chicken’ [Tofu] Strips:
  1. Preheat the oven to 160ºC.
  2. Drain and cut tofu into strips.
  3. In large bowl, combine all ingredients. Mix well.
  4. Coat tofu sticks into batter. Roll them in the breadcrumbs.
  5. Line baking sheets with parchment paper. Arrange tofu strips on the baking sheets (Don’t stack).
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown. Serve hot.
