Instructions to make Macaroni and Cheese:
  1. Cook pasta…add carrots during the last 2 minutes.
  2. Drain and place back in pan with rest of ingredients (except for parmesan). Stir to melt cheese and butter.
  3. Once cheese starts to melt and ingredients are combined, pour into greased casserole, top with parmesan.
  4. Bake 400 degrees for 25 minutes until top is browned and bubbly.
