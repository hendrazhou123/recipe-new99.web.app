The ingredients needed to make Meat nachos:
  1. Make ready 400 g nachos
  2. Make ready 200 g minced meat
  3. Take 1 fresh paprika
  4. Make ready 1 onion
  5. Take 2 gloves of garlic
  6. Take 500 g pulped tomatoes
  7. Get cheese
  8. Get jalapenos
  9. Take black pepper
  10. Take paprika spice
  11. Make ready salt

Instructions to make Meat nachos:
  1. Slice onion and garlic into small pieces and fry on a pan until onion gets slightly golden
  2. Add minced meat, salt, paprika spice and black pepper. Fry until done.
  3. When meat is done. Add paprika in small pieces and heat them for ~2 minutes
  4. Add pulped tomatoes and chili. Fry for additional 2 minutes.
  5. Put nachos on a oven plate. Pour meat mix upon them and add jalapenos and cheese
  6. Heat 10 minutes in 100C oven.
  7. Enjoy the food
