The ingredients needed to make Minestrone Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 box vegetable stock
  2. Make ready 2 cups water
  3. Prepare 1 can diced tomatoes with juice
  4. Prepare 1/2 can garbanzo beans
  5. Get 1/2 can red kidney beans
  6. Prepare 1/2 diced onion
  7. Take 1/4 cup corn
  8. Get 1/4 cup green beans
  9. Take 2 cloves garlic, minced
  10. Get 1 Tbsp Italian seasoning
  11. Take 1 tsp salt
  12. Take 1 tsp black pepper
  13. Get 1 cup shell pasta
  14. Take Parmesan and basil leaves for garnish
