The ingredients needed to make Cheese Stuffed Bread:
  1. Prepare 3 Cups All Purpose Flour
  2. Get 1 Cup Warm Water
  3. Make ready Pinch Salt
  4. Take Pinch Sugar
  5. Take Vegetable Oil, Espresso Cup Size
  6. Prepare 2 Tea Cup Spoons of Bread Yeast
  7. Get Collection of Cheese
  8. Make ready Sesame Seeds
  9. Take Butter

Steps to make Cheese Stuffed Bread:
  1. Mix all ingredients and knead them into a dry big ball of dough.
  2. Leave the dough to rest for at least two hours.
  3. Cut some cheese and put aside.
  4. Cut the dough into small balls and stuff them with cheese, seal them properly and roll over one of the sides in sesame seeds, then stack them close to one another in a pan brushed with vegetable oil.
  5. Melt some butter and brush them all over the top of the dough pieces in the pan. This will give a nice light golden color once baked.
  6. Heat the oven and bake at medoum temperature for around 20 minutes and then bake from the top for 5 minutes. Keep the pan at mid-level in the oven to avoid burning.
