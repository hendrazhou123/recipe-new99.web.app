Instructions to make Green Zuchini with eggs *Vegetarian:
  1. Clean and cut up green zuchini.
  2. Put them in a saute pan, cover them and stir occasionally until soften.
  3. Crack eggs on top of them while soften.
  4. Sprinkle salt and pepper, mix them well until the eggs cooked.
  5. Enjoy.
