The ingredients needed to make Rice_with_green_beans:
  1. Get Cooked green beans
  2. Make ready 2 cups rice (soak for 10 mins)
  3. Prepare 1 onion, diced
  4. Get 1 tablespoon ghee or vegetable oil
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
  6. Prepare 3 cups water or broth
  7. Prepare To prepare the minted cucumber yogurt
  8. Take 4 cucumbers, diced
  9. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon salt
  10. Get 1/3 teaspoon dry mint

Instructions to make Rice_with_green_beans:
  1. In a saucepan add some gee or oil & saute the onion till translucent & golden
  2. Add the cooked  green beans and stir
  3. Drain the rice before adding to the saucepan. Add salt to taste
  4. Add the water or broth & bring to a boil. - Once boiled reduce to low heat, cover & simmer for 15 minutes until the rice is fully cooked
  5. Serve next to minted cucumber yougurt.
  6. Preparation of the minted cucumber yogurt: - In a deep mixing bowl add the yogurt, salt, cucumber & dry mint and whisk well until smooth.
  7. Serve in a deep dish garneshed with some more dry mint.
