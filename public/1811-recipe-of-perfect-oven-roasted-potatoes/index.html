The ingredients needed to make Oven Roasted Potatoes:
  1. Make ready 5 Medium Red Potatoes
  2. Make ready 1 Pouch Lipton Onion Soup Mix
  3. Prepare 1 Tbsp Olive Oil
  4. Get 1 Tsp Paprika
  5. Get 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
  6. Prepare 2 Tsp Cajun Seasoning
  7. Take 1 Tsp Onion Powder

Steps to make Oven Roasted Potatoes:
  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit. Cut potatoes into small cubes. No larger than 1/2 inch.
  2. Coat potatoes in olive oil.
  3. Combine remaining ingredients and add to potatoes mixture. Mix well.
  4. Bake for approximately 40 minutes in a 9x9 baking dish. Potatoes should be fork tender when ready.
