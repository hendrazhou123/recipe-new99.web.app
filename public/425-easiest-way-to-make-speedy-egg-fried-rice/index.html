The ingredients needed to make Egg Fried rice:
  1. Prepare 2 eggs
  2. Prepare 2 cups boiled rice
  3. Prepare 1 Green capsicum Chopped
  4. Take 1 carrot chopped
  5. Get 2 tablespoons Boiled Corn
  6. Prepare 1 large onion Chopped
  7. Take 2 tablespoons butter
  8. Take to taste Salt
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon jeera (cumin seeds)
  10. Prepare 1 Green Chilli
  11. Get 1 teaspoon Sugar
  12. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon Pepper powder

Instructions to make Egg Fried rice:
  1. Heat butter in a pan.
  2. Add jeera. When it crackles, add chopped capsicum, carrot and onions. Fry and put in boiled corns.
