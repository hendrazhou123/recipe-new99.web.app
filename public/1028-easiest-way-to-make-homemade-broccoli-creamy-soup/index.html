The ingredients needed to make Broccoli creamy soup:
  1. Get 200 g broccoli
  2. Make ready 1 onion
  3. Get 1 cube chicken stock
  4. Make ready 1 cup water
  5. Prepare 1 cup milk
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  7. Make ready 1 TBSP butter
  8. Take To taste Salt
  9. Take To taste Pepper

Steps to make Broccoli creamy soup:
  1. Cut broccoli into pieces. Microwave for 2 minutes.
  2. Thinly slice onions. In a medium pan, medium heat melt butter and sauté onion until the onion gets clear.
  3. Add water, chicken stock and broccoli. Cook together for 10 minutes.
  4. Put them in a blender.
  5. Add milk and heavy whipping cream. Adjust the flavor with salt and pepper.
