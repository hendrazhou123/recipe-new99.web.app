Instructions to make Sweet Corn Soup:
  1. Make a thick paste from 1/2 cup of boiled sweet corn.
  2. Now add butter to a preheated pan.
  3. And soutte the capsicum for 5 min
  4. Now add the thick paste to it
  5. Then add 1cup of water and boil it.
  6. After the soup gets boiled add salt and black pepper and 1/2 cup of boiled sweet corn.
  7. And let it boil for 5 min at medium flame.
  8. Now your soup is ready to serve…
