The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Cheese Ball:
  1. Take 16 oz. Cream cheese, room temp
  2. Get 1 1/2 C. Shredded cheddar cheese
  3. Prepare 3 Tbsp. Minced onion
  4. Get 2 tsp. Ground cumin
  5. Take 2 Tbsp. Salsa
  6. Prepare 1 tsp. Minced jalapeño
  7. Get 1 bell pepper stem
  8. Prepare Cheese flavored tortilla chips
  9. Take 1 bag blue corn tortilla chips

Instructions to make Pumpkin Cheese Ball:
  1. Mix cream cheese, shredded cheese, onion, cumin, salsa, and jalapeño until well combined.
  2. Scoop onto plastic wrap and use the wrap to form into a 5 in pumpkin shaped ball. Chill at least 2 hours.
  3. To serve unwrap and roll in crushed cheese flavored tortilla chips and press a bell pepper stem into the top. Serve with blue corn chips.
