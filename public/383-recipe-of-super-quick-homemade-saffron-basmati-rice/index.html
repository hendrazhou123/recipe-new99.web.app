Steps to make Saffron Basmati Rice:
  1. Put the basmati rice and water in a rice cooker and cook as usual.
  2. Heat the saffron in the microwave on low for 1 minute. Add 1 tablespoon of hot water to bring out the color.
  3. Mix the cooked rice well and pour the saffron liquid around the edges.
  4. Use a spoon to mix in one place to make saffron rice.
  5. When the yellow has become evenly distributed, mix the saffron rice into the white parts.
  6. Transfer to a plate and it's done. Pack into a bowl and flip over.
  7. It goes great with sautéed fish.
  8. Have it with some simmered meatballs and vegetables.
