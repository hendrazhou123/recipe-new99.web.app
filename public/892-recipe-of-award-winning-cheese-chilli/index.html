The ingredients needed to make Cheese chilli:
  1. Take 250 gms cheese
  2. Prepare 1 capsicum
  3. Take 2 onions
  4. Make ready 1 tsp ginger and garlic paste
  5. Make ready 1 tsp red chilli powder and blk pepper powder
  6. Prepare 1 tsp soya sauce
  7. Get 1 tsp vinegar
  8. Take 1 tsp tomato sauce
  9. Take 2 tsp corn flour
  10. Take To taste salt

Steps to make Cheese chilli:
