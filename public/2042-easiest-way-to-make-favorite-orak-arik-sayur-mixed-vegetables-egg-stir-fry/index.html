The ingredients needed to make Orak Arik Sayur (Mixed Vegetables Egg Stir Fry):
  1. Get 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
  2. Take 100 gr green beans, diagonally sliced
  3. Get 2 medium carrots, julienned
  4. Get 100 gr bean sprouts
  5. Take 2 Thai bird eye chilli peppers, cut into small pieces (optional)
  6. Prepare 2 large eggs, beaten
  7. Prepare 1 tsp salt or to taste
  8. Take 1/2 tsp mushroom bouillon
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp white pepper powder or to taste

Steps to make Orak Arik Sayur (Mixed Vegetables Egg Stir Fry):
  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium high heat.
  2. Sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add green beans and carrots. Cook until vegetables a bit soft.
  4. Add green beans and chilli peppers (optional).
  5. Season with salt, white pepper powder, and mushroom bouillon. Mix well for 30 seconds.
  6. Add the beaten eggs slowly little by little while stirring the vegetables. Remove from the heat.
  7. Serve over warm white rice. Yum! 😋
