Instructions to make Meat-pie:
  1. Place the flour in to a bowl, Add butter and mixed until the butter vanishes in to the flour add baking butter and egg mix them together……
  2. Add the quantity of water and make a soft dough.
  3. Roll out the dough on a flat table lightly Dunlop and cut round by using meat-pie cutter or round cup.
  4. Put in your already meat source in the center and cover edges
  5. Brush the body with egg white before baking
  6. Incase of frying: fry in deep oil
