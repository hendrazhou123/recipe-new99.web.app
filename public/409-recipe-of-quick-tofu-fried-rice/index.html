The ingredients needed to make Tofu Fried Rice:
  1. Take 2 packs tofu
  2. Prepare 3.5 cups brown rice raw
  3. Prepare 1 onion
  4. Get Spring onion
  5. Get Chopped garlic
  6. Take Chopped ginger
  7. Prepare Peas and carrots

  3. Fry onions. Add garlic, ginger near end. As spring onions. Add peas and carrots. Add chilli and pepper.
  4. Add oil to pot, put in rice and fry. Mix everything else back in.
