The ingredients needed to make My Creamy Chicken Curry with Rice (Crock-Pot):
  1. Prepare 2-3 slices cooked bacon
  2. Make ready 4 skinless boneless chicken tenderloins
  3. Take 2 (10.5) once cans cream of mushroom soup
  4. Take 14.5 ounce can chicken broth or chicken soup base
  5. Make ready 1 small white onion, chopped
  6. Take 2 tsp curry powder
  7. Take 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  8. Take 2 cups uncooked long grain rice
  9. Prepare 1 S&B mild curry block –> just 1 slice (optional)
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper (optional)

Steps to make My Creamy Chicken Curry with Rice (Crock-Pot):

  3. Combine all the ingredients in your slow cooker.
  4. You can add a bag of frozen carrots to your curry as well.
