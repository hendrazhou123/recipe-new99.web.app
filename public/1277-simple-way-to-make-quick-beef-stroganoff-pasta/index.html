The ingredients needed to make Beef stroganoff pasta:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 pounds beef sirloin steak, 1/2 inch thick
  2. Make ready 2 cups sliced button mushrooms
  3. Make ready 2 chopped medium size onions
  4. Make ready 1 chopped clove garlic
  5. Take 1 1/2 cup beef broth
  6. Take 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  8. Prepare 1 1/2 cups sour cream
  9. Take 3 cups cooked egg noodles
  10. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
  11. Make ready Chopped parsley for garnishing
