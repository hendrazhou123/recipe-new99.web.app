  4. Prepare 2 cayenne pepper (optional)
  5. Get 2 red onions
  6. Get 1 garlic
  7. Make ready 1 spring onion
  8. Get 1 tomato
  9. Take 1 tsp salt
  10. Take 1 tsp bouillon powder
  11. Get 100 ml water
  12. Get Vegetable oil or olive oil
