Instructions to make Meat pie:
  1. Start by putting your meat in a pot and adding water seasons salt onion cook on till meat is soft put d meat in a food processor and blend
  2. In a bowl cut your onions and keep a side the put a pan on the stove pour in a little oil an put in the the onions fry till it's translucent then pour in the meat add the seasoning,garlic and ginger power and fry till is a little dark brown then keep a side
  3. In a bowl pour in your flour, baking powder some salt and combine them together then pour in the oil and meat stock and knead for a better meat pie coat texture use cool meat stock that means u should refrigerate it
  4. Start rolling and putting the fried meat in the middle and folding it
  5. All done then start frying turn from time to time till golden brown
  6. It's all ready
