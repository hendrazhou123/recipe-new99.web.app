The ingredients needed to make Egyptian street juicy meat shawerma:
  1. Get 1/2 kilo fillet cut into strips
  2. Take 3 tbsp vinegar
  3. Get 1 tbsp garlic powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 tbsp onion powder
  5. Prepare 2 diced into cubes Tomatoes
  6. Prepare 1 meat bouillant powder
  7. Get 1 medium to large onion
  8. Take 3 tsp Seseme paste- tehina
  9. Get pack Yogurt
  10. Prepare 1 tsp 7 spices
  11. Make ready 1 tsp coriander dry powder
  12. Get 1/4 tsp ginger powder
  13. Get Onion water
  14. Make ready Chilli powder (if u want it spicy)
