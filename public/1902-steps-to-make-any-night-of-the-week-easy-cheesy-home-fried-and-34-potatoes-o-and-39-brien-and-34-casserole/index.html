Instructions to make Easy, Cheesy, Home Fried "Potatoes O'Brien" Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 450°. Place frozen potatoes in baking pan. Add in cheese, soups, and seasonings to taste. Mix thoroughly.
  2. Top with crushed up corn flakes, cover, and bake in the oven for 40-60 mins or until cornflakes begin to turn golden brown.
  3. Serve. Enjoy! 🤤
