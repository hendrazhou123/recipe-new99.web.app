The ingredients needed to make Zesty Italian Roasted Pork Loin:
  1. Prepare 1 pork loin, 2-3 pounds
  2. Take 1/2 (1- 12oz bottle) zesty Italian Dressing
  3. Take 2 TBSP House Seasoning
  4. Take Olive oil for searing
  5. Get Clove garlic

Instructions to make Zesty Italian Roasted Pork Loin:
  1. Rub the pork loin with house seasoning on both sides. Transfer to a ziploc and pour in dressing. Marinade over night.
  2. Heat oven to 375 F°.
  3. Heat a cast iron skillet on medium heat, add oil.
  4. Remove loin from ziplock, rub more seasoning on both sides and transfer, fat side down, into the cast iron. Sear on both sides. Add a clove of garlic
  5. Pour remaining marinade to cover loin. Roast in oven, 60- 75 minutes until internal temp is 140.
  6. Rest 10 min. And serve.
