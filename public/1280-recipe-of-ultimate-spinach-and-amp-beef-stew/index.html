The ingredients needed to make Spinach & beef stew:
  1. Get 500 gms lean beef
  2. Take 2 cups chopped spinach
  3. Make ready 2 tbls garlic paste
  4. Make ready 1 chopped medium onion
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 cup blend tomatoes
  6. Make ready 2 tbls tomato purée
  7. Make ready 1 green chilli
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  9. Prepare Lime wedges to serve
  10. Prepare to taste Salt & pepper

Instructions to make Spinach & beef stew:
  1. Cut up the beef in cubes and set to marinate
  2. To marinate beef, add 1 tbls garlic paste and salt & pepper and mix well to make sure all beef pieces are coated. Set aside to marinate for atleast 30 minutes
  3. In a pot, add a tbls of vegetable oil and sauté the rest of the garlic, onions and tomato purée until golden brown and tomato purée turn colour
  4. Add in the beef and mix well to coat. Immediately add in the blend tomatoes and allow beef to cook in the tomatoes over medium high heat
  5. Once the beef is cooked through, add in the spinach and allow to cook with the beef for about 10 minutes over medium heat
  6. Reduce heat to medium low and allow stew to simmer and flavour to blend together. Once the liquid reduces, remove from heat and add cilantro before serving with rice or the chia and flaxseed bread
