The ingredients needed to make Cheese Straws Pastry:
  1. Prepare 3 sheets Puff Pastry
  2. Get 100 gr cheddar cheese (finely grated)
  3. Take 100 gr parmesan cheese (finely grated)
  4. Get 50 gr rosemary leaves (finely chopped)
  5. Get 1 tbsp margarine

Steps to make Cheese Straws Pastry:
  1. Preheat the oven 160°C, prepare the baking tray, brush margarine all over the tray, put aside.
  2. Mix the grated cheddar, parmesan cheese and chopped rosemary.
  3. Cut up puff pastry sheets long shaped.
  4. Coat the puff pastry with the cheese mixture one by one and twist them lightly.
  5. Arrange them on the baking tray and bake them for 5-10 mins. (be careful it burns quickly)
  6. When they turn golden, take them out and enjoy them with the loved ones.
