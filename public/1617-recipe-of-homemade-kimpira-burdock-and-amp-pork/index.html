The ingredients needed to make Kimpira Burdock & Pork:
  1. Make ready 200 grams Burdock
  2. Make ready 1 pcs Carrot
  3. Take 200 grans Sliced Pork
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp Sugar
  5. Take 4 tbsp Sake (Japanese cooking wine)
  6. Take 4 tbsp Soy Sauce

Instructions to make Kimpira Burdock & Pork:
  1. Scrape off the burdock skin and cut into long, thin shavings. (It’s easy to shave the burdock by using peeler.) - Put the shavings in water for 15 minutes and drain.
  2. Cut the carrot into thin strip. - Cut the sliced pork into bite-size.
  3. Heat the oil, and stir-fry the pork, burdock and carrot over high heat. - When the moisture evaporates and the oil has seeped in, add Sugar, Sake and Soy Sauce.
  4. Stir from time to time on medium heat until the moisture is gone.
