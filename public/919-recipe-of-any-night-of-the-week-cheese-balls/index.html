The ingredients needed to make Cheese balls:
  1. Take 4 boiled potatoes
  2. Get 3 tbsp grated cheese
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp bread crumbs
  4. Take 2 Chopped garlic
  5. Prepare 1 tsp grated ginger
  6. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  7. Get to taste salt
  8. Make ready 3/4 tsp chilli flakes
  9. Make ready 2 tsp corn flour
  10. Make ready 1-2 tbsp oil

Instructions to make Cheese balls:
  1. First mash the potatoes
  2. Add 3tbsp bread crumbs, salt black pepper powder.1/2tsp chilli flakes,garlic,ginger and mix well
  3. Make small rounds and keep aside
  4. Now make very small rounds of grated cheese and keep aside
  5. Take one potato ball press and keep one cheese ball in the centre and cover it very well
  6. Do the same process with remaining balls
  7. Mix corn flour with water and make a very thin slurry.
  8. In a plate take 1tbsp bread crumbs chilli flakes and garlic and mix
  9. Dip the ball in corn flour slurry and coat them in bread crumbs.
  10. Coat the bread crumb to all the balls and keep it in fridge for 20-30mins.
  11. Heat a non-stick pan… brush with little oil and shallow fry all the cheese balls
  12. OR brush a appe pan with oil and put balls in each cavity…do not cover.flip and fry from all the sides.
  13. Ready…serve with tomato ketchup.
