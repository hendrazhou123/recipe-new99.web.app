The ingredients needed to make Gluten Free Egg Frittata:
  1. Make ready 8 eggs
  2. Prepare 2 cups milk
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp butter
  4. Get 2 pinch’s of Penzy’s Forward seasoning or a Cajun seasoning
  5. Prepare 2 cups cheddar cheese
  6. Take 2 green onions sliced
  7. Take 2-4 sausage links cooked and sliced or sub for bacon sliced
  8. Get 3-4 small red potatoes sliced
  9. Get 1 red and orange pepper chopped
