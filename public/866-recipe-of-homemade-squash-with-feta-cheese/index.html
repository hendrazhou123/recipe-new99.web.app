The ingredients needed to make Squash with Feta Cheese:
  1. Make ready 2-4 yellow squash
  2. Make ready 2 (5 oz) feta cheese containers
  3. Make ready 1 cup milk
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Take 1/4 tsp black pepper
  6. Take Dash dill weed

Instructions to make Squash with Feta Cheese:
  1. Wash your squash.
  2. Slice your squash.
  3. Use a small or large baking pan. Cover with aluminum if desired.
  4. Add the squash and 1 cup of milk. I use Almond milk. Add your 2 feta containers. ½ tsp salt and ¼ tsp black pepper. Mix all together and bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes. Sprinkle with dill weed to top it off.
  5. Half way through, take it out of oven, mix everything together again.
  6. Give it one more good stir together.
