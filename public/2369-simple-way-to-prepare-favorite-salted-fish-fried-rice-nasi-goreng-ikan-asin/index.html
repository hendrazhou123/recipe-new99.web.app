The ingredients needed to make Salted Fish Fried Rice (NASI GORENG IKAN ASIN):
  1. Take 300 grams cooked white rice (at room temperature)
  2. Get 100 grams salted fish fried, shredded/torn rough
  3. Prepare 1 egg, beaten off and fry while in scrambled
  4. Make ready 1 leek/leaf chives, cut askew
  5. Take 5 cayenne peppers (to taste), thinly sliced
  6. Get 2 cloves garlic, minced
  7. Prepare 1 small onion, chopped
  8. Take 1 1/2 tsp soy sauce
  9. Take 1 tsp sesame oil
  10. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
  11. Get Chicken flavoring (optional)
  12. Make ready Oil for frying

Steps to make Salted Fish Fried Rice (NASI GORENG IKAN ASIN):
  1. Saute onion, garlic and cayenne pepper until fragrant.
  2. Add rice, salted fish and eggs. Stir until blended.
  3. Add soy sauce, salt, pepper and flavoring (optional). Stir until all blended.
  4. Towards the end, add sesame oil and scallions. Cook, stirring frequently until wilted scallions.
  5. Arrange fried rice on plate and serve while warm.
