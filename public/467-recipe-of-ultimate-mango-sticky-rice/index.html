The ingredients needed to make Mango Sticky Rice:
  1. Take 250 g short-grain rice
  2. Make ready 350 + 50ml canned light coconut milk
  3. Take 100 g erythritol
  4. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  5. Take 1 tsp cardamom

Instructions to make Mango Sticky Rice:
  1. Rinse and soak rice for 30min
  2. Drain rice, steam for 30min or until soft
  3. Separately combine 350ml coconut milk, erythritol, salt & cardamom. Bring to almost a boil
  4. Combine rice and sauce, let sit 20-30min
  5. Refrigerate
  6. Add extra 50ml coconut milk (and water) if too dry
