The ingredients needed to make Kastengel / Cheese Tarts:
  1. Get 100 gram Butter
  2. Get 50 grams Margarine
  3. Take 200 grams Cake flour
  4. Take 50 grams Cornflour
  5. Prepare 3 eggs
  6. Make ready 1 sachet milk powder
  7. Take 1 tbs salt
  8. Take 1 tbs royco powder
  9. Take 1 tbs vanila
  10. Take 100 grams gouda/edam cheese

Steps to make Kastengel / Cheese Tarts:
  1. Whip the butter for 1 minute on low speed and add egg yolk and whip on high speed for 5 minutes. Add the cheese, flour, milk powder and use a spatula to stir to mix into a non-sticky dough
  2. Preheat oven to 330 F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper. Place a parchment paper or silicon mat on a working surface. Roll the dough out to about 1-inch thickness. Use a knife to cut the dough about 1-inch in width and then cut into individual about 3 inches long.Arrange these on the cookie sheet. Brush with egg wash and sprinkle with shredded cheese. Bake for 20 minutes or until golden brown.
  3. Let the kastengel cool off on the baking sheet for 1 minute and then remove to cooling rack to let them cool down completely. Store in air-tight container and they can last up to one month
