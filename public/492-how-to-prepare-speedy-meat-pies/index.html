Steps to make Meat pies:
  1. Make dough
  2. Mix grounded meat of choice with veggies of choice
  3. Add meat to circle of dough and fold like an omlette
  4. Press fork around edge to seal
  5. Fry in oil such as veggie oil, canola oil, peanut oil, etc. Choose one.
  6. Wait until golden brown
  7. Serve
