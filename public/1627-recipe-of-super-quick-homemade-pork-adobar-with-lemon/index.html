The ingredients needed to make Pork Adobar with Lemon:
  1. Take 1/2 kg Pork
  2. Make ready 3 tbsp Garlic Minced
  3. Take 2 tbsp Onion Minced
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup Soy Sauce
  5. Take 1/2 cup Vinegar
  6. Make ready 1 Tbsp Sugar
  7. Prepare 1 Lemon whole cut into circles
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp Chilli Powder
  9. Get Spring onion
  10. Make ready 1 cup water
  11. Make ready for plating
  12. Get Spring Onion
  13. Take Lemon

Instructions to make Pork Adobar with Lemon:
  1. Sauté 2 tbsp onion, 3 tbsp garlic and spring onion before the oil gets hot.
  2. Add pork until brown then add lemon and 1 tbsp chilli powder or depends on your taste.
  3. Add 1/2 cup soy sauce, 1/2 cup vinegar, Sugar and 1 cup water.
  4. Cook over high heat until mixture come to boil. Reduce heat to medium. Let simmer. Cook until sauce reduce and thicken.
  5. Done.
