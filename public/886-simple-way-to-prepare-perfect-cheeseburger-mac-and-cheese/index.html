The ingredients needed to make Cheeseburger Mac And Cheese:
  1. Prepare 1 lb Lean ground beef
  2. Make ready 1/4 box Velveeta process cheese, cubed
  3. Get 2 cups Milk or half and half, divided
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper
  5. Get 1 Salt and pepper
  6. Prepare 1 Onion and garlic powders, I used Everglades seasoning
  7. Prepare 1 lb Pasta of choice, I used bow ties
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp AP flour
  9. Take 1 cup Panko bread crumbs mixed with 2 tablespoons oil and salt and pepper

Instructions to make Cheeseburger Mac And Cheese:
  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Melt cubed velveeta and 1 cup milk or half and half in microwave til smooth.
  3. Par-boil pasta for about 3 minutes, drain.
  4. Brown ground beef and season. Once browned stir in flour til absorbed. Splash in last cup of milk and stir to a boil and it thickens. Turn off heat. Stir cheese sauce into beef mixture. Add pasta and stir.
  5. Poor into sprayed dish and cover with foil. Bake about 20 minutes. Remove foil and add bread crumbs. Crank heat to 425 til browned. Enjoy
