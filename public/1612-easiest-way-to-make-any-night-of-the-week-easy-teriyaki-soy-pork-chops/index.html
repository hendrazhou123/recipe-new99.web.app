The ingredients needed to make Easy Teriyaki - Soy Pork Chops:
  1. Make ready 1 large pack boneless pork chops (I used center cut, about 2 inch thick)
  2. Prepare Sprinkle black pepper
  3. Get Sprinkle garlic powder
  4. Make ready 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  5. Take 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  6. Take 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  7. Prepare 2 onions, sliced
  8. Get Hot cooked white rice

Steps to make Easy Teriyaki - Soy Pork Chops:
  1. Rinse and pat dry your pork chops…..
  2. Place chops in a baking pan…..
  3. Season both sides of chops with black pepper and garlic powder…..
  4. In a bowl combine cream of mushroom soup, soy sauce and teriyaki sauce, mix well…..
  5. Place teriyaki mixture over each pork chop…….spread well…..
  6. Place sliced onions on top of pork chops……
  7. Cover pan and place in a preheated 400 degree oven for 1 hour or until pork chops are done……
