The ingredients needed to make Garlic and cheese knots:
  1. Make ready 2 cups flour
  2. Prepare 1/2 water
  3. Get 1/4 cup oil
  4. Take For the sauce
  5. Take 1/2 stick butter
  6. Get 2 cloves garlic or 2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  7. Make ready 1 tea spoon onion powder
  8. Get 1 teaspoon parsley
  9. Prepare 2-4 cups cheese
