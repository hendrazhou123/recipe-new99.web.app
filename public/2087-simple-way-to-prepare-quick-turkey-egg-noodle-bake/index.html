The ingredients needed to make Turkey Egg Noodle Bake:
  1. Take 1 package egg noodles (16 ounce)
  2. Make ready Salt for boiling water
  3. Prepare Olive oil
  4. Prepare 1 onion, slices
  5. Prepare 1 green pepper, sliced
  6. Get 6 cloves garlic, minced
  7. Get 2 packages (16 ounce) ground turkey
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon thyme leaves
  9. Take 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  10. Make ready 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  11. Take 1 can cream of chicken soup
  12. Take 2 soup cans milk
  13. Prepare Canola oil cooking spray
  14. Prepare 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
