The ingredients needed to make Chicken Soup:
  1. Take 250 gm Chicken with bone
  2. Get 1 Onion chopped
  3. Make ready 1 tomato chopped
  4. Make ready 2/3 Clove garlic
  5. Prepare Turmeric powder
  6. Get 1/2 tsp Chilli powder
  7. Get 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  8. Take 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  10. Get to taste Salt
  11. Prepare 1 tsp Pepper
  12. Prepare leaves Coriander
  13. Make ready 1/2 Cup Water
