The ingredients needed to make Baked mac and cheese:
  1. Prepare 1 lb large elbow noodles
  2. Get 32 oz chicken broth
  3. Prepare 1 cup heavy cream
  4. Make ready 2 eggs
  5. Prepare 1 cup sour cream
  6. Make ready 2 cups sharp cheddar
  7. Get 1 cup Monterey jack
  8. Get 1 tsp onion powder
  9. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
  10. Get 1 tsp chili powder
  11. Make ready 1 tsp paprika
  12. Prepare 1 stick butter

Steps to make Baked mac and cheese:
  1. Boil elbow noodles in chicken broth to Al dente
  2. Mix heavy cream and 2 eggs together in a bowl and set aside
  3. Grease your cast iron with butter and preheat oven to 375
  4. Drain noodles and stir in butter (sliced), sour cream,seasoning and heavy cream mixture
  5. Once incorporated mix 1 cup cheddar and 1/2 cup of Monterey jack
  6. Spoon in half of the mixture and layer 1/2 cup cheddar and 1/4 cup Monterey jack
  7. Layer in the remaining noodles and cheese. Sprinkle with a dusting of paprika
  8. Bake for about 40 minutes or until golden crust forms
