The ingredients needed to make Cheese Soufflè:
  1. Get 100 g cheese (emmentaler or gouda, or same style)
  2. Get 3 cups milk
  3. Make ready 3 tbsp butter
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  5. Prepare Pinch salt
  6. Get 2 eggs
  7. Make ready butter for the soufflè shapes

Instructions to make Cheese Soufflè:
  1. Make a thick beshamell sauce. Heat the butter in a pan. Add flour, salt, stir well, add milk.
  2. Keep stirring until thick, then take it off from the flame
  3. Separate two eggs. Beat the egg white with some salt till firm. Mix the yolks till foamy
  4. Butter the soufflè shapes
  5. Shred the cheese and stir it into the now cold beshamell
  6. Gently add the egg whites and yolks too
  7. In the soufflè shapes,bake it till fork comes clean from middle
