The ingredients needed to make Potato wedges:
  1. Prepare 2 russet potatoes
  2. Prepare 1 tsp chilly powder
  3. Take 1/4 tsp pepper
  4. Prepare 2 tsp oil
  5. Get 2 tbsp corn flour
  6. Get Salt as required

Instructions to make Potato wedges:
  1. Preheat oven at 350 degrees F
  2. Cut potatoes into lengthy pieces
  3. Boil it in water for 3 mins and drain the water
  4. Combine chilli powder, corn flour, salt, pepper and oil. Coat potatoes evenly with this mixture.
  5. Bake at 350 degree for 15 mins one side and 10 mins other side
