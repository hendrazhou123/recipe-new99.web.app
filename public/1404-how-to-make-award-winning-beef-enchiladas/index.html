Instructions to make Beef enchiladas:
  1. Make crepes by adding small quantity of batter in a shallow pan with 1 Tablespoon of oil
  2. 1 cup flour will make 5 crepes
  3. For sauce, add ketchup, tomato paste, salt, chili sauce and bring it to boil
  4. Then add cornflour which is dissolve in 1 cup of water
  5. During adding the cornflour continuously stir the sauce to avoid the formation of lumps
  6. Make it thicker in the form of a gravy
  7. Fill the center of the crepes with aloo keema mixture
  8. Roll it and place it in a oven proof glass dish
  9. Pour the red sauces
  10. Then shred the mozzarella cheese
  11. Garnish it with oregano, Rosemary and red chilli flakes
  12. Bake it for 5 minutes or when the cheese melts
  13. Serve it with red sauce, a wholesome and delicious meal is ready ☺️☺️
