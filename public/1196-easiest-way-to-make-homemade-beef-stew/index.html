The ingredients needed to make Beef stew:
  1. Make ready 1 pound beef tips cut into bite size
  2. Get 16 oz bag of baby carrots (I used the multi color ones) cut in half
  3. Make ready 24 oz bag of red potatoes (I used ones with different ones for color) cut into bite size
  4. Get 1 large red (or favorite) onion diced
  5. Take 32 oz beef broth
  6. Prepare 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
