The ingredients needed to make Chicken chukka:
  1. Prepare 750 gm chicken
  2. Make ready 4 tbsp homemade chicken masala
  3. Take 3 tbsp grinded coconut with 3 cashew
  4. Prepare to taste salt
  5. Make ready 3 tbsp oil
  6. Take 2 tsp curry leaves
  7. Get 2 green chillies
  8. Make ready 1 chopped onion
  9. Get 1 inch Cinnamon stick
  10. Prepare 2 cloves
  11. Prepare 1 bay leaf

Instructions to make Chicken chukka:
  1. Clean chicken and cut it to small pieces
  2. Take a pan, add oil then add cinnamon, bay leaf, cloves, then add onion and ginger garlic paste. Saute till onion turns brown and then add chicken
  3. Cover and cook for 10 min, then add grounded coconut paste and chicken masala
  4. Adjust salt to taste, wait till all water evaporates and you get a semi gravy consistency
  5. Serve hot
