The ingredients needed to make Vegetable Zucchini Soup:
  1. Get Half a Lg. Zucchini, cubed
  2. Get 2-3 Lg. Potatoes, cubed
  3. Prepare 1 Lg. Can Mixed Vegetables, drained
  4. Make ready 1 Lg. Can Whole Kernel Corn, drained
  5. Prepare 1 Lg. Can Chopped Spinach, drained
  6. Take 2-3 Quarts Chicken Broth
