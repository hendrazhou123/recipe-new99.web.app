The ingredients needed to make Sweet n sour chicken:
  1. Prepare For chicken
  2. Get 1 cup breast fillet (thin sliced)
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp ginger paste
  4. Take 2-3 pods chopped garlic
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp soy sauce
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp vinegar
  7. Make ready 3 tbsp corn starch
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  9. Prepare 2 sticks spring onions (finely chopped)
  10. Take 1 onion sliced
  11. Make ready Bunch peanuts (optional)
  12. Make ready For noodles
  13. Make ready 1 packet ching’s hakka noodles
  14. Prepare 2 eggs scrambled
  15. Get 2 green chillies (finely chopped)

Steps to make Sweet n sour chicken:
  1. In a pan add the cut onions,ginger,garlic and medium fry. Add in the soy sauce vinegar sugar to it. Coat the chicken with salt and corn flour and sauté well separately. Add in the sauce mix and garnish with spring onions and peanuts.
  2. Boil the noodles separately and drain well. Shallow fry with egg and chillies.
