The ingredients needed to make Tomato Soup:
  1. Make ready 600 cc water
  2. Take 1 Can tomatoes
  3. Take 3 pieces Cabbage
  4. Get 200 g Bacon
  5. Get 1/2 Carrot
  6. Make ready 1 Onion (small size)
  7. Take 15 cc Olive oil
  8. Take to taste Salt
  9. Get Grated garlic (1 tsp)
  10. Get 2 consomme cubes

A simple, homemade soup made with fresh tomatoes is a perfect summertime treat when the best tomatoes are ripe in gardens and farmers' markets. Everyone will love the fresh sweet taste and. Tomato soup is a healthy, low-calorie comfort food. It is also a great way to warm up on a rainy day and it goes perfectly with grilled cheese sandwiches or Parmesan crisps.

Steps to make Tomato Soup:
  1. Put olive oil and grated garlic in a pot, and fry vegetables and bacon.
  2. Add water, consomme cube, and a can of tomatoes.
  3. Boil the vegetables until they get tender. Then, add salt and black pepper to taste.
