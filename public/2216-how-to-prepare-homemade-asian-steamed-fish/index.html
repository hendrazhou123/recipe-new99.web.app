The ingredients needed to make Asian steamed fish:
  1. Take 300 grams fish
  2. Prepare 2 tablespoon soy sauce
  3. Take 2 tablespoon cooking wine
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  5. Take 20 grams ginger
  6. Take 10 grams spring onion
  7. Take 1 chili (optional)
