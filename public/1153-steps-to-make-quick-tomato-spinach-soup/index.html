Instructions to make Tomato spinach soup:
  1. Wash and drain spinach leaves and add them in a pressure cooker.
  2. Cut the tomatoes and add them to the cooker along with 1/4 cup of water.
  3. Add some chopped ginger, salt and black pepper powder.
  4. Close the lid of pressure cooker and cook for 3-4 minutes till you get 1 whistle.
  5. Let the mixture cool down. Then grind with a hand blender.
  6. Pass the mixture through a strainer to filter out any rind.
  7. Add the soup to a pan along with 2 cups water.
  8. Add some roasted cumin powder.bring the soup to a boil and let it cook for 3-4 minutes.
  9. Tomatoes spinach soup is ready.Serve it.
