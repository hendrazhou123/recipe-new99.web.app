The ingredients needed to make Creamy Potato Apple Salad:
  1. Take 2 Fuji apples, sliced to bite size
  2. Get 1 stalk (w/ or w/o leaves) Celery, minced
  3. Prepare 1 bunch Parsley, minced
  4. Make ready 4 Potatoes, boiled & peeled
  5. Prepare 1 sml can Pineapple triangles, drained
  6. Get 1 box nestle cream
  7. Prepare 1 square packet mayonnaise, ladies choice
  8. Take 1 carrot, diced
  9. Take 1 bunch green onion, garnish cut (optional)

Steps to make Creamy Potato Apple Salad:
  1. Cut boiled potatoes into bite size slices, as well as the apples. Peel if desired.
  2. Mix together: apples, potatoes and pineapple. Add mayo and cream. Mix well until it fully covers all.
  3. Garnish with a sprinkle of celery, parsely or green onion and carrots for color. Mix again. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
