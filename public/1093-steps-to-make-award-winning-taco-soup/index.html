The ingredients needed to make Taco Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 lb. hamburger
  2. Take 1 small onion, chopped
  3. Make ready 1 (14.5 oz) can petite diced tomatoes
  4. Get 1 (14 oz) can beef broth
  5. Take 1 (15.5 oz) can black beans
  6. Make ready 1 (15.5 oz) can pinto beans
  7. Make ready 1 (15.5 oz) can kidney beans
  8. Make ready 1 (15.25 oz) can corn
  9. Take 1 (10 oz) can Rotel
  10. Prepare 1 pkt. Ranch Dip Mix
  11. Prepare 1 pkt. Taco Seasoning
  12. Take Toppings as desired: sour cream & cheddar cheese

Steps to make Taco Soup:
  1. Cook hamburger along with onions until thoroughly cooked; drain off grease.
  2. Open all cans and prepare for crockpot.
  3. To the crockpot add the tomatoes, beef broth and Rotel.
  4. Drain the beans and corn and add to pot.
  5. Stir in seasoning packets and hamburger.
  6. Cook on low for 4 hours. Serve with toppings of choice. Enjoy!
