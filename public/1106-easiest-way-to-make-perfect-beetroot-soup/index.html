The ingredients needed to make Beetroot Soup:
  1. Make ready 3 medium beetroot thinly sliced
  2. Take 1 medium onion
  3. Get 2 cloves garlic
  4. Prepare 1 medium potato
  5. Take 2 medium carrot
  6. Take to taste Salt
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  8. Prepare 1 large tomato
  9. Get 1 tbsp fresh cream
  10. Take 2 tbsp butter

Instructions to make Beetroot Soup:
  1. Heat butter in a deep non-stick pan.Add chopped onion & garlic to the pan & saute.
  2. Then add thinly sliced carrot & potato.Add salt,black pepper powder & stir.Cover & cook forn2-3 mints.
  3. Add beetroot & 3 cups water.Cover & cook till the vegetables are almost done.
  4. Add chopped tomato.Strain the vegetables.Allow to cool.Blend into a smooth puree adding a little.cold water.
  5. Transfer the puree back into the pan, add the strained cooking liquid & mix well.
  6. Heat the soupThen pour it into a soup bowl,garnish with cream & serve hot.
