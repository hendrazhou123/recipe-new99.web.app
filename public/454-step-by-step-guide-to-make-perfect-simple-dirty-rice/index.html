The ingredients needed to make Simple Dirty Rice:
  1. Prepare 4 cup parboiled rice
  2. Make ready 1 med chicken liver
  3. Get 1 small red pepper
  4. Prepare 1/4 small onion
  5. Get 1 tsp fresh garlic
  6. Get Dash yellow food coloring
  7. Prepare Dash cumin
  8. Take Dash oregano
  9. Prepare to taste Salt & pepper
  10. Prepare 2 cubes or small packs of chicken bullion
  11. Take 1/2 cup bacon bits (optional)
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup canned peas
  13. Make ready 1/2 cup canned sweet corn
  14. Get 2 tblsp olive oil
  15. Prepare 1 cup white cooking wine
  16. Make ready 1 bay leaf
  17. Take 8 oz beer (light)

Instructions to make Simple Dirty Rice:
  1. Boil chicken liver for 10 min or until cooked, let cool and dice finely
  2. Sauté onion, pepper,and garlic in the olive oil about 3 to 4 min,then add the wine and bring to boil
  3. Add rice, bullion and seasonings, a pinch of coloring,oregano, cumin, salt& pepper, bacon bits,,bay leaf, along with 4 cup of water, cover and cook on med for 20 min. Uncover, add the beer, the corn and the peas, mix well and let cook for another 5 to 6 min. Adjust seasonings for final taste. Serve and enjoy
