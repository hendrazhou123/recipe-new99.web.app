The ingredients needed to make Chinese Corn Soup:
  1. Take 1 can Canned Cream style Corn ●
  2. Get 800 ml Water●
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp Chinese Soup Stock●
  4. Get 2 tsp Potato Starch ◎
  5. Take 4 tsp Water ◎
  6. Prepare 1 piece Egg
  7. Get 1 tsp Sesame oil

Instructions to make Chinese Corn Soup:
  1. Put ● ingredients (Canned cream style Corn, Water, Chinese soup stock) in a pot. Bring to a boil over medium heat.
  2. Put ◎ ingredients (Potato Starch, water) in a small bowl and mix it well. Pure into the boiling corn soup.
  3. Gradually add the beaten egg while stirring the soup. Add the sesame oil for fragrance.
