The ingredients needed to make Tomato soup:
  1. Get 1 quart tomatoes
  2. Get 1 teaspoon salt
  3. Take 1 onion chopped
  4. Prepare 1 bay leaf
  5. Prepare 1 tablespoon butter
  6. Prepare Salt
  7. Prepare 1 cup cream if desired
  8. Prepare Pepper onion seasoning
  9. Make ready Celery salt
