The ingredients needed to make Kabocha Rice Made in Rice Cooker:
  1. Get 500 ml rice
  2. Get 750 ml water
  3. Take pinch salt
  4. Take 1 tbsp oil (I used fermented garlic & candlenut oil)
  5. Get 1 cup peeled and diced kabocha
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp dry sliced garlic (you can use fresh garlic, of course)
