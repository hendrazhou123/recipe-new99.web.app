The ingredients needed to make Pork Mechado:
  1. Make ready 1/2 kg pork shoulder sliced into cubes
  2. Make ready 1 Knorr Pork Cube
  3. Prepare 1 large potato and carrots cubed
  4. Take 1 sachet tomato sauce
  5. Get 3 cloves garlic crushed and chopped
  6. Make ready 1 medium onion diced
  7. Take 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  8. Prepare To taste cheese
  9. Make ready 3 pcs hotdog (sliced)

Instructions to make Pork Mechado:
  1. Saute the garlic and onion until the onion gets soft.
  2. Add the pork. Cook until it pork turns light brown. Add the sliced hotdogs
  3. Pour tomato sauce, knorr cubes and water. Let boil.
  4. Add the potato and carrots. Cook for 10 minutes
  5. Add salt, ground black pepper and cheese to taste
