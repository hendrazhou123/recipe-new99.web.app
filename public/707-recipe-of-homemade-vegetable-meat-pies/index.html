Steps to make Vegetable meat pies:
  1. In a bowl…knead your dough using the one cup of flour and water until smooth. Pat some veg oil and set aside
  2. Beat the eggs in a cup
  3. Fry the onions, carrots, garlic using some vegetable oil in a shallow pan.
  4. Add in the eggs and fry until dry. Add in the spices 1/4 tbsp each
  5. Divide the dough into small balls and spread using a rolling pin into circles
  6. Add in the egg filling evenly and cover into halves…you can achieve the rugged ends using a fork
  7. Heat vegetable oil in a pan and deep fry until golden brown. Drain excess oil and serve with your favourite sauce!!!Enjoy!!!
