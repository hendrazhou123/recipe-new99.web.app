The ingredients needed to make Chicken and rice:
  1. Take Chicken breast or any you have on hand
  2. Prepare 2 cups rice
  3. Prepare 2 carrots
  4. Prepare 2 celery
  5. Prepare Half a onion
  6. Take Green onions
  7. Get 2 cans cream of mushroom
  8. Prepare Olives
  9. Get Salt pepper garlic powder

Steps to make Chicken and rice:
  1. This is super good for left over chicken. If you do not have that. You can boil it and cut it in bite size
  2. Cut up the carrots and the celery and onions and the olives but put those aside
  3. Add the rice carrots celery and onions to the pot. Add the 4 cups of water and cook
  4. Once done add the chicken and the salt and pepper and garlic powder.
  5. Add the 2 cans of mushrooms soup and stir.. taste it you might need more salt and pepper.
  6. Enjoy
