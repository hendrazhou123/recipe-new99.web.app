The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Egg Cups:
  1. Make ready 10 eggs
  2. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  3. Take 1/8 teaspoon salt
  4. Take 1/4 cup heavy cream
  5. Get 1 cup shredded breakfast fiesta cheese
  6. Make ready Sprinkle imitation bacon bits
  7. Get Butter cooking spray
  8. Get Cheese for topping

Steps to make Breakfast Egg Cups:
  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and whisk until mixture is fully combined……
  2. Pour mixture into a muffin pan that has been sprayed with butter cooking spray……
  3. Top off each egg cup with any leftover shredded cheese……
  4. Bake in a preheated 375 degree oven for 20 minutes or until eggs are set…..
  5. Let eggs rest in muffin tin for 10 minutes, then remove and place on a serving platter…..
  6. Serve and enjoy 😉!
