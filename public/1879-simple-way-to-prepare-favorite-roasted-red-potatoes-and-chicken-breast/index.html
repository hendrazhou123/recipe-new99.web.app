Steps to make Roasted Red Potatoes and Chicken Breast:
  1. Marinade chicken with garlic,olive oil and chicken powder, set aside about 15 minutes
  2. Prepare for baking dish,Melt butter and wait untill cold down then mix potatoes with melt butter and salt pepper,place potatoes in the bake dish, and bake in the oven with 250 c untill tender.
  3. While waiting for the potatoes,do the same thing for tomatoes and mushroom also arugula, mix them with melt butter and salt pepper
  4. Heat a pan, add butter,put chicken in the flour and fry untill golden
  5. Check potatoes, when it gets soft put tomatoes, then mushroom and chicken on the top of potatoes, and bake again about 15 minutes.
  6. After 15 minutes put arugula and parmesan cheese on the top and bake again 1-2 minutes. And done..
