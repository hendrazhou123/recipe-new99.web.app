Instructions to make Schezwan soup:
  1. In a pan add garlic ginger and sesame oil and saute
  2. Add mushroom spring onion and mix for 1 minute
  3. Add chicken stock carrot capsicum sauces chicken
  4. Beat eggs and addin soup
  5. Dissolve corn flour in water and add till soup thickens
  6. Garnish with green chillies and spring onion
