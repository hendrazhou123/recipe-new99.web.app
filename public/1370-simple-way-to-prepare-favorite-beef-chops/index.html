The ingredients needed to make Beef chops:
  1. Prepare 1 kg Chops
  2. Prepare 3 Tomatoes
  3. Make ready 2 onion
  4. Make ready 2 Green chili
  5. Take 1 bowl Yogurt (medium)
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon Garlic
  7. Take 1 tablespoon Ginger
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon Crushed coriander
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon Red chili flakes
  10. Get 1 teaspoon Turmeric
  11. Take Salt as per taste
  12. Take 1 teaspoon Crushed cumin
  13. Make ready 1 teaspoon Crushed black pepper
  14. Get 1 teaspoon Hot spice
  15. Prepare 1 cup Oil
  16. Prepare 1 cup Water
