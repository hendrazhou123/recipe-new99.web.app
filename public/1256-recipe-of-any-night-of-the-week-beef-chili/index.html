The ingredients needed to make Beef Chili:
  1. Prepare 2 tablespoons oil
  2. Get 3 medium onions chopped
  3. Prepare 1 red bell pepper chopped
  4. Prepare 1 small jalapeño chopped (with seeds for hotter flavor)
  5. Take 8 garlic cloves chopped
  6. Make ready 1 beef bouillon
  7. Take 2.2 oz ground beef
  8. Prepare 1 tablespoon dried onion
  9. Get 1 tablespoon paprika
  10. Get 2 tablespoons cumin
  11. Take 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  12. Prepare 15 oz can of tomato sauce
  13. Get 15 oz can of crushed tomatoes
  14. Prepare to taste Salt
  15. Make ready To accompany:
  16. Take Sour cream or cream cheese
  17. Prepare Shredded cheddar, Monterrey Jack, Asadero and quesadilla cheeses
  18. Make ready Corn tortilla chips
  19. Make ready Rice
  20. Prepare Black beans
  21. Take Kidney beans
