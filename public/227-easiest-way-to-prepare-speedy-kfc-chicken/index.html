The ingredients needed to make KFC Chicken:
  1. Make ready 250 gm chicken thighs
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup buttermilk
  4. Get To taste salt
  5. Get 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup Maida/ all -purpose flour
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup corn flour
  8. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp oregano
  10. Get 1 tsp ginger and garlic powder
  11. Take 1 bowl chilled water

Instructions to make KFC Chicken:
  1. In a bowl take buttermilk mix some salt ginger garlic paste chilli powder mix and marinate for one night or 4 to 5 hrs
  2. Take a bowl mix all dry ingredients
  3. Take chicken pieces one by one first coat with dry ingredients pressing lightly so that flaky type it looks than put in chilled water for 5 secs
  4. Than again coat flour as you did first do for all pieces same way
  5. Deep dry in medium flame it comes out same as market KFC enjoy
