The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Beef Stroganoff:
  1. Prepare 2 lb. roast; cubed
  2. Take 1 large onion, chopped
  3. Prepare 16 oz. pkg. wide egg noodles
  4. Take 2 cups cheddar cheese
  5. Take 1 cup sour cream
  6. Get 1/2 cup milk
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  8. Prepare 10.5 oz can cream of mushroom soup
  9. Get Seasonings of choice: black pepper, garlic powder, parsley

Instructions to make Cheesy Beef Stroganoff:
  1. In a large pot, cook meat along with onions and seasonings in a little oil until thoroughly cooked.
  2. Meanwhile, cook noodles and drain; set aside.
  3. To cooked meat, add cheddar cheese, sour cream, milk, parmesan cheese and cream of mushroom soup. Mix well.
  4. Allow to completely melt over medium low heat.
  5. Stir in cooked egg noodles.
  6. Enjoy!
