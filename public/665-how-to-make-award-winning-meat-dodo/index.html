Instructions to make Meat Dodo:
  1. Wash your meat and cut into cubes, boil and fry. Set aside.
  2. Cut your plantain into cubes and set aside.
  3. Blend your pepper(Atarugu) and chop your red and green pepper, onions.
  4. In your pot, put a small amount of oil when hot add onions, blended pepper and allow to fry for some minutes.
  5. Add your spice of choice, add meat and followed by plantain. Then add your chopped ingredients.
