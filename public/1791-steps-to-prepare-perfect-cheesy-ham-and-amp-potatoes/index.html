The ingredients needed to make Cheesy ham & potatoes:
  1. Take 3 tbsp butter
  2. Take 1 sm yellow onion, finely chopped
  3. Prepare 2 packages cooked ham steaks
  4. Prepare 3 tbs AP flour
  5. Make ready 1 tsp paprika
  6. Make ready 1 1/2 c milk
  7. Take to taste Kosher salt & pepper
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  9. Prepare 1/2 bag little potatoes (i used the littlepotatocompany)
  10. Make ready 1 cup (1/2 bag) your fav shredded cheese
  11. Prepare Optional: crushed red pepper

Instructions to make Cheesy ham & potatoes:
  1. Preheat oven to 375. In med saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add onions & let cook, stirring occasionally for about 4 minutes. While that is cooking, chop ham into small pieces.
  2. Sprinkle flour & paprika & let cook, stirring occasionally. While whisking, add milk. Bring to a simmer & whisk until thick. Stir in ham, salt, pepper & Italian seasoning.
  3. Cut potatoes in half, spread across a 8x8 or 9x9 pan. Sprinkle salt & pepper over potatoes. Spoon sauce over potatoes. Optional: sprinkle crushed red pepper flakes.
  4. Sprinkle cheese over top. Put aluminum foil over pan & let cook for 40 min (to really get potatoes soft). Then remove aluminum foil & let cook 25 minutes. Let cool.

Stir ham and cheese sauce into the pasta, mix well. Transfer to baking dish and spread evenly. This impressive-looking casserole bakes up puffy and golden with pockets of fresh corn and savory ham.
