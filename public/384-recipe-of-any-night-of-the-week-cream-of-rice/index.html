The ingredients needed to make Cream of rice:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup long grain rice
  2. Prepare 2 cups water
  3. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon salt
  4. Prepare 2 cups broth I used beef
  5. Take 1 1/2 cup yoghurt
  6. Prepare 1 egg yolk
  7. Take 2 teaspoon cornstarch
  8. Take 2 tablespoon cold water
  9. Take Topping
  10. Take 2 teaspoon dry mint
  11. Take 2 teaspoon butter
