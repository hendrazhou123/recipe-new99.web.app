Instructions to make Crockpot BBQ Chicken Sandwiches:
  1. Add chicken breasts to the crock pot with some salt, pepper, oregano, and garlic for 3 hours.
  2. Remove liquid. (I store mine in the freezer until a time I need chicken stock!)
  3. Add your desired BBQ sauce and desired amount. (I used about 1/2 cup on mine. I like my shredded BBQ chicken to be a bit coated in BBQ!)
  4. Serve up on your favorite hamburger buns with pickles!!! (I served my BBQ chicken sandwiches with homemade coleslaw–see recipe for details!)
