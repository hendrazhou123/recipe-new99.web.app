The ingredients needed to make Mexican Mushroom Meat:
  1. Take 1/4 Sweet Onion (chopped)
  2. Take 1/2 Red Bell Pepper (chopped)
  3. Prepare 5 oz Baby Shitake Mushrooms (whole or roughly chopped)
  4. Make ready 1 Clove Garlic (finely diced)
  5. Prepare 6 Sprigs Cilantro finely chopped
  6. Take Juice of 1 Lime
  7. Get 2 Tsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  8. Take 2 Tbsp Paprika
  9. Make ready 1 Tbsp Dried Parsley
  10. Take 2 Tsp Cumin
  11. Prepare 1 Tsp Dried Oregano
  12. Take 1 Tsp Dried Dill
  13. Prepare to taste Salt and Pepper
