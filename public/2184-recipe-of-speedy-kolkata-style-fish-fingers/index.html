The ingredients needed to make Kolkata Style Fish Fingers:
  1. Make ready 3 Basa Fish Fillet (cut in to the shape like fingers)
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  3. Make ready 2 inch Ginger
  4. Take 3 big cloves of garlic
  5. Get 3 nos Green chillies

  12. Make ready As per taste Salt
  13. Make ready 1 cup Bread crumbs
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup All-purpose Flour

Instructions to make Kolkata Style Fish Fingers:
  1. Wash the fish fillets properly.Then cut them in the shape like fingers.Pat them dry with a paper towel.
  2. Marinate the fish fillets first with Lemon juice,Salt & Crushed black peppercorns and keep aside for 15 minutes.
  3. Now in a mixer grinder by using little water make a smooth paste of Ginger,Garlic,Coriander leaves,Mint leaves,Green Chillies & Onion.
  4. Marinate the fish fillets second time with the smooth paste until coated properly and refrigerate for overnight or at least 2 hours.
  5. Take out the marinated fillets from the refrigerator.Beat one egg and egg white in a bowl with some salt.Take all-purpose flour and bread crumbs in two separate plates & spread them on the plate nicely.
  6. Coat the fish sticks one by one with flour first,then dip them into the egg mixture.
  7. Now coat them one by one properly with the bread crumbs.
  8. Heat the oil in a frying pan,fry the fish sticks in 2-3 batches until golden brown.Keep turning the fish sticks and fry each side evenly until cooked properly.Transfer the fried Fish Fingers on a paper towel lined plate to absorb the excess oil.
  9. Now Enjoy them hot with onion cucumber salads & kasundi or mustard sauce or any sauce of your choice.
