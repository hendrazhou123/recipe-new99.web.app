The ingredients needed to make Beef Broccoli:
  1. Get 1 and half pounds round steak, sliced thin then sliced in strips
  2. Take 1-2 large clumps of broccoli
  3. Prepare 1 bottle Stir Fry Sauce
  4. Get 1 can Water Chestnuts
  5. Prepare 1 can bamboo shoots
  6. Take 1 large can of baby corns
  7. Prepare 3 cups cooked rice
  8. Make ready Olive oil

Instructions to make Beef Broccoli:

  2. Add all of the canned ingredients (drained) and the whole bottle of stir fry sauce. To make it go farther fill the bottle with some warm water and shake to get ALL of the sauce..
