The ingredients needed to make Hearty Lentil Soup:
  1. Get 1 tablespoon garlic
  2. Prepare 1 shallot
  3. Make ready 1 cup celery
  4. Take 2 cups carrots
  5. Get 1 cup lentils
  6. Get 3 cups sweet potato
  7. Make ready 2 cups kale
  8. Prepare 3 tablespoons chicken bouillon (or vegetable stock)
  9. Prepare 8 cups water
  10. Make ready To taste salt
  11. Get To taste pepper
  12. Make ready To taste coconut oil

Steps to make Hearty Lentil Soup:
  1. Add garlic, shallot, carrots and celery to coconut oil and cook for 5 minutes
  2. Add sweet potatoes
  3. When everything is nice and brown add water and condiments, bring to BOIL
  4. When everything is boiling add the lentils
  5. When everything is soft, add the kale
  6. Serve with rice
