The ingredients needed to make Sweet Potato Pie:
  1. Get 1 cup mashed sweet potatoes
  2. Make ready 1 stick butter
  3. Prepare 1.5 cups sugar
  4. Take 3 tablespoons flour
  5. Take 2 eggs
  6. Make ready 1 small can evaporated milk
  7. Get 1 teaspoon lemon extract
  8. Take 1 teaspoon vanilla
  9. Get 1 unbaked pie crust
