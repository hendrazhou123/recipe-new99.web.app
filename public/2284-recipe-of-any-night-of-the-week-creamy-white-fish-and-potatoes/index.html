The ingredients needed to make Creamy White fish and Potatoes:
  1. Take 1 lb frozen. Whitefish fillets(tilapia/Pollock/sole)
  2. Make ready 3 medium potatoes peeled and sliced
  3. Make ready 1 large onion sliced
  4. Prepare 1 tin(10 3 /4 oz.) Cream of Mushroom soup
  5. Make ready 1 cup milk
  6. Get to taste Salt and pepper
  7. Get 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional)

Steps to make Creamy White fish and Potatoes:
  1. Arrange fish in bottom of 9x13 baking dish
  2. Layer potatoes and onion on top of fish. Sprinkle layers with salt and pepper.
  3. Combine soup and milk and pour over all.
  4. Sprinkle with shredded cheese
  5. Bake uncovered at 400 f. for 1 hour
