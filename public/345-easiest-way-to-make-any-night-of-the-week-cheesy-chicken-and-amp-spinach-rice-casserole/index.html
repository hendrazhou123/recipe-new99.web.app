The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Chicken & Spinach Rice Casserole:
  1. Prepare 2 chicken breasts cubed
  2. Take 1/2 medium onion
  3. Get 1 tbsp garlic
  4. Make ready 1-1/4 cup chicken broth
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup water
  6. Get 1 cup long grain white rice
  7. Prepare 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  8. Make ready 1 cup frozen or fresh spinach
  9. Take 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  10. Take to taste Salt and pepper

Instructions to make Cheesy Chicken & Spinach Rice Casserole:
  1. In a large pot over medium heat add the olive oil and then and the onions. Saute for 2-3 minutes
  2. Season the chicken with salt and pepper and add to pot. Cook until browned. 5-7 minutes
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute
  4. Add chicken broth, water and rice. Cover with lid and and turn the heat down to low
  5. Cook for 15 minutes stirring halfway through
  6. After 15 minutes, stir in the thawed or fresh spinach and cook for another 7 minutes.
  7. Remove from heat and add cheddar cheese.
  8. Enjoy!
