The ingredients needed to make One pan steak, potatoes & broccoli:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 pounds flank steak or strip steak, cut into strips
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 pounds baby potatoes (cut in 1/2 or 1/4)
  3. Make ready 1 tbs olive oil
  4. Take 3 tbs butter
  5. Take 5 cloces garluc, minced
  6. Take 1 tsp thyme
  7. Make ready 1 tsp fresh rosemary
  8. Prepare 1 tsp oregano
  9. Make ready to taste S&p
  10. Prepare to taste Crushed red pepper flakes
  11. Take 1 head broccoli, chopped
  12. Take Marinade
  13. Get 1/3 c light sodium soy sauce
  14. Take 1 tbs olive oil
  15. Take 1 tbs hot sauce
  16. Get to taste Black pepper

Instructions to make One pan steak, potatoes & broccoli:
  1. In a bowl, combine steak strips w/ the marinade ingredients. Let marinade for about 20 min. In a large skillet over med high heat, mix 1 tbs olive oil & 1 tbs butter. When butter is melted, add potatoes & broccoli, cook 4 min. Stir and cook another 4 min until potatoes are fork tender. Transfer to a plate- set aside
  2. In same skillet, add remaining 2tbs of butter, garlic & red chilli flakes and herbs. Lay steak in even layer on top (do not throw away marinade). Cook steak about 1 min per side.
  3. Stir in marinade with potato mixture and let cook 1 min. Add s&p to your liking. Remove from heat & serve.
