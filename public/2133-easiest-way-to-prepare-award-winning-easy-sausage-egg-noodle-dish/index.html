The ingredients needed to make Easy Sausage Egg Noodle Dish:
  1. Take 2 cups egg noodles, cooked
  2. Take 4 links Italian sausage, (hot or sweet, either one is just fine !)
  3. Make ready 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  4. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  5. Get 5 cloves garlic, minced
  6. Prepare 1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  7. Prepare 1/2 soup can milk
  8. Make ready Sprinkle of parsley flakes
