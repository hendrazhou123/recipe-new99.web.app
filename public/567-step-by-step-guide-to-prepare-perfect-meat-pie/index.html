Steps to make Meat pie:
  1. Mix ur flour simas pinch of salt nd baking powder in a clean bowl
  2. Add water bit by bit to form a dough…knead well nd allow to rest for 30mins
  3. For the fillings…peel ur potatoes cut in cubes shape and boil for 10mins
  4. Cook ur minced meat with seasonings nd spices…when well cooked add lil oil scoth bonnet onions nd boiled potatoes together…fry for 5mins
  5. Divide ur dough into equal size nd take one by one flatten it with a rolling pin nd put it into ur meat pie cutter
  6. U now add 1 spoon of the fillings put water at the edges nd den close it
  7. Fry it until when it is golden brown
