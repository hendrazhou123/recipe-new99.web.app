The ingredients needed to make Chicken cheese samosas:
  1. Prepare 1 cup boiled chicken, shredded
  2. Get 1/2 cup sweetcorn
  3. Get 1/4 cup capsicum chopped
  4. Prepare 3 green chiles chopped
  5. Take 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  7. Get to taste salt
  8. Get 1/2 tsp Chinese salt
  9. Make ready 1 tsp chili flakes
  10. Make ready 1 tsp black pepper
  11. Prepare as needed samosa patti
