Steps to make Chicken kathi roll:
  1. First make crispy lacha prantha… With some ajwain, salt n red chilli powder on it… On slow flame for crispyness..
  2. Marinate chicken with curd, lemon juice, salt, red chilli powder, turmericn Ginger garlic paste… Keep aside
  3. On other side.. take wok. Saute chopped onions dn add marinated chicken… Add chicken masala.. whn chicken get cooked, switch off flame.
  4. Then take prantha.. spread chesse dip on it… Put chicken… Then on top add sliced onions, mayonise n mustard sauce, chilli sauce, chaat masala n roll it..
  5. Half roll it with foil paper n enjoy 😊
