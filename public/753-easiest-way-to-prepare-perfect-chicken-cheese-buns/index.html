The ingredients needed to make Chicken cheese buns:
  1. Prepare For dough
  2. Make ready 2 cups Flour
  3. Take 1/2 tsp Salt
  4. Get 1/2 tsp Sugar
  5. Prepare 1 Egg
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp Yeast
  7. Take 2 tbsp Oil
  8. Take 4 tbsp Milk
  9. Make ready As needed Warm water
  10. Make ready For chicken cheese filling
  11. Make ready 1 cup Chicken (boil)
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup Cheese
  13. Get 1/4 cup Chopped mushrooms
  14. Get 1 Small chopped onion
  15. Get 1 Small chopped capsicum
  16. Prepare to taste Salt
  17. Take 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  18. Get 1 tbsp Chopped garlic
  19. Get as needed Fennel and sesame seeds for Sprinkle

Instructions to make Chicken cheese buns:
  1. Mix of the dough ingredients in a bowl and knead. Rest for 1 hour
  2. In a pan heat oil saute garlic add onion, capsicum, mushrooms, chicken and all spices saute a minute, remove from stove and add cheese amd mix
  3. Take out the dough make 8 small portions
  4. Take one portion spread on a surface add chicken mix in the center and cover like a ball
  5. Made all the balls in the same way
  6. Place the buns on a baking tray spread beaten egg on top Sprinkle seeds
  7. Bake on preheated oven for 20-25 minutes
