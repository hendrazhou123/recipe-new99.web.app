Instructions to make Smashedcheeseburger and German Fried Potatoes:

  2. Add salt and pepper onto the top of the ground beef and onions, put into the oven. Peel wash and slice the potatoes thin. Dice the onions.
  3. Add the onion and potatoes to a skillet with the tablespoon of peanut oil. Coat all the potatoes and onions.
