The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin and Potato Soup:
  1. Prepare 4 pcs potato
  2. Prepare Half round pumpkin
  3. Make ready 1 whole onion, minced
  4. Take To taste Dried basil
  5. Prepare To taste Sprinkle of cinnamon powder
  6. Take As needed Melted butter
  7. Make ready 1 small pack of coconut milk
  8. Take 1 little salt

Instructions to make Pumpkin and Potato Soup:
  1. Boil potato and pumpkin. Then blend it.
  2. Saute onion then add water.
  3. Pour in the blended pumpkin and potato and other ingredients.
  4. Then keep boiling until the soup thickens.
  5. Add a little salt according to your taste.
