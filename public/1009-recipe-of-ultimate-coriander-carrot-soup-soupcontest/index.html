  1. Get 1 tbsp coconut oil, 1 tsp ground coriander, 1/2 tsp grated ginger, 450g carrots-peeled & chopped, 1/4th cup double cream, handful coriander (3 sprigs), 1/4th tsp ground pepper, 1 cup stock/hot water

Steps to make Coriander carrot soup #soupcontest:
  1. Heat 1 tbsp coconut oil in a large pan, add grated ginger, then fry for 2 mins until softened.
  2. Stir in 1 tsp freshly ground coriander and cook for 1 min.
  3. Add chopped carrots, then And 1.2l vegetable stock or hot water, bring to the boil, then reduce the heat.
  4. Cover and cook for 20 mins until the carrots are tender.
  5. Add ground pepper, salt and double cream. Cook for 2 mins.
  6. Tip into a food processor with a handful of coriander then blitz until smooth.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with Ritz crackers.
