Instructions to make Cheese Mashed potatoes Meat Pie:
  1. Wash and peel the potatoes
  2. As the potatoes are going slice the onions and garlic set aside
  3. Set the oven at 350
  4. Cut the steak up into bite sized.
  5. When the potatoes are done drain and set aside add the butter to it
  6. Add the a small bit of oil to it frying pan. And dump the onions and stir them. Add the garlic to it
  7. Add the meat and cook it till it is not all pink. Set aside
  8. Make the masher. Add the milk and garlic and onion powder salt and pepper and a handful of cheese.
  9. In a large or medium casserole dish do it like this. Put masher on the bottom, steak and onion and some cheese.. then more mashers and steak and cheese. Add the rest of the potatoes and add the rest of the cheese
  10. Put it in the oven for about 10 to 15 mins. Booooom done.
  11. It goes well with a side veggies and also a nice salad. Let me know if you like it
