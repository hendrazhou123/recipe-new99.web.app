The ingredients needed to make Thai Basil Porks:
  1. Make ready 150 g Pork (marinade)
  2. Get 1 1/2 tsp Taocu (soybeans paste)
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp Sugar
  4. Make ready Thai Basil
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Garlic
  6. Prepare 150 ml water
  7. Take Potato Starch

Steps to make Thai Basil Porks:

  2. Heat up the wok with 4tbsp vegetables oil and fried some chopped garlic
  3. Add in water, Taocu and the pork meat as well. Water cook with the pork is better feeling… meat softens
