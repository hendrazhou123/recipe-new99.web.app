The ingredients needed to make Beef and Cabbage:

Steps to make Beef and Cabbage:
  1. Take the beef add to a pot to start browning. Wash really good the leek. Chop up the tender parts of the leek. Add leek to the browning beef.
  2. Add the spices. Wash and chop the cabbage and add to the beef. Stir and cover 10 minutes. Add the butter and add cover walk away for 20 minutes.
  3. Add the Cabernet Sauvignon, stir and cover let simmer for 15 minutes. Let rest covered for 8 minutes. Serve add jalapeno peppers if you want. I hope you enjoy!!!!!
