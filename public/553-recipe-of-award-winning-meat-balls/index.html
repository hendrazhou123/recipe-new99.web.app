The ingredients needed to make Meat balls:
  1. Take 1/2 kg boneless mutton
  2. Prepare 6 medium size onion finely chopped
  3. Make ready 2 teaspoon garlic paste
  4. Make ready 1 1/2 teaspoon ginger paste
  5. Prepare 2-3 green chillies finely chopped
  6. Take 1/2 cup coriander leaves finely chopped
  7. Get 2 teaspoon garam masala
  8. Make ready 1 lemon
  9. Take 1 Cup gram flour
  10. Make ready As needed Oil to deep fry
  11. Prepare to taste Salt

Instructions to make Meat balls:
  1. In cooker cook the mutton for three whistle or until done. let it cool and put in a mixer jar and grind it only for 2 seconds.
  2. In a large bowl add onion chillies, coriander leaves, ginger and garlic, garam masala, lemon juice, gram flour, salt and grinded mutton.mix well.
  3. Prepare balls and deep fry till golden colour.
  4. Your tasty meat balls are ready to serve.
