The ingredients needed to make Udon Suki soup:
  1. Get 1/2 kg chicken nibblets
  2. Prepare 2 carrots cut in pieces
  3. Take 1 stalk celery
  4. Get Chinese cabbage
  5. Make ready 5 Garlic cloves
  6. Take 1/2 cup suki sauce
  7. Take 1 tsp white pepper
  8. Take 1 tbsp salt
  9. Take 1 packet udon noddles

Instructions to make Udon Suki soup:
  1. Boil chicken nibblets with carrot, salt, celery, garlic and pepper for 1 hour (you can use ready made chicken stock)
  2. Turn off the heat. Set aside, Let it cool.
  3. Shred the chicken nibblets. Set aside.
  4. In a smaller pot, add udon noodles, 3 tbsp shredded chicken, chinese cabbage, carrots and soup and boil until noodle is cooked. Add in suki sauce and enjoy!
