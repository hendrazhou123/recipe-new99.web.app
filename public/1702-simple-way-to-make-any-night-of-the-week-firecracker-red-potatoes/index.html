The ingredients needed to make Firecracker Red Potatoes 🥔:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 pounds small red potatoes
  2. Take As needed Salt for boiling water
  3. Get 1 stick butter OR margarine
  4. Take 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  5. Take 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper (you can add more if you like for more heat !)
  6. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon seasoned salt
  7. Get 3 onions, sliced
  8. Get 1 green pepper, sliced

Steps to make Firecracker Red Potatoes 🥔:

  2. To potatoes add in your stick of margarine OR butter, black pepper, crushed red pepper, seasoned salt, onions and green pepper, sauté until potatoes and veggies are browned and tender…..
