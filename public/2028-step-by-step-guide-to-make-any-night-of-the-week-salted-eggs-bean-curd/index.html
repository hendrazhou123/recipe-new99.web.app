The ingredients needed to make Salted Eggs Bean Curd 咸蛋豆腐:
  1. Prepare 3 pcs Bean Curd
  2. Make ready 2 pcs Salted Eggs Yolk
  3. Take 20 pcs Curry Leaf
  4. Prepare 1 pc Chili's
  5. Get Pinch Salt
  6. Take Pinch Sugar
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp Butter

Instructions to make Salted Eggs Bean Curd 咸蛋豆腐:
  1. Each bean curd cut into 4pcs. Then deep fried till the surface looks dried.
  2. Hot wok with 3tbsp butter deep fried salted eggs yolk and added salt & sugar
  3. Once the salted eggs yolk occured with bubbles then add in curry leaf & chilies and lastly add in bean curd Stir-fried… then ready to be served.
