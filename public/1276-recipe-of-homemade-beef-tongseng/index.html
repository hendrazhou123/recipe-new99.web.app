The ingredients needed to make Beef Tongseng:
  1. Take 400 gr brisket, cut into bite size
  2. Get 6 small tomatoes or 2 medium tomatoes
  3. Make ready 5 cabbage leaves, roughly cut some bird eye chili peppers (to taste-optional)
  4. Prepare 2 green onions, cut 1 inch
  5. Make ready 1 Tbsp curry powder
  6. Get 2 lemongrass, bruised
  7. Make ready 3-4 kaffir lime leaves
  8. Make ready 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
  9. Take 350 ml water
  10. Make ready 1 Tbsp salt or to taste
  11. Make ready 1/2 tsp white pepper powder or to taste
  12. Get 1/2 tsp sugar
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp beef bouillon
  14. Get 2-3 Tbsp sweet soy sauce
  15. Make ready fried shallots for garnish
  16. Prepare Ground spices:
  17. Get 4 cloves garlic
  18. Get 6 cloves shallots
  19. Make ready 6 red chili peppers
  20. Take 3 bird eye chili peppers (optional)
  21. Make ready 1 inch ginger
  22. Prepare 3 nutmeg

Steps to make Beef Tongseng:
  1. Heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Sauté ground spices, lemongrass, and kaffir lime leaves until fragrant. - Add curry powder. Mix well.
  2. Add beef. Cook until the meat change color. Remove from the heat.
  3. Place the meat in a slow cooker. Add water, salt, sugar, white pepper powder, and beef bouillon. Cover the pot. Cook on high for 4 hours.
  4. Add cabbage, tomatoes, green onions, bird eye chili peppers (if you use some), and sweet soy sauce. Cook again for 15 minutes. Remove the pot.
  5. Serve immediately over steamed with rice and garnish with fried shallots! Yum 😋
