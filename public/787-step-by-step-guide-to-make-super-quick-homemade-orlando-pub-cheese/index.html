The ingredients needed to make Orlando Pub Cheese:
  1. Get 8 oz. Extra sharp cheddar, cut into cubes
  2. Take 8 oz. Monterey Jack, cut into cubes
  3. Prepare 2 cloves fresh garlic, minced
  4. Get 2 T. Worcestershire sauce
  5. Take 1 tsp. Red wine vinegar
  6. Make ready 3/4 c. Dark beer or stout
  7. Prepare 1 T. Dijon mustard
  8. Take 1 tsp. sriracha

Instructions to make Orlando Pub Cheese:
  1. In a food processor with chopping blade, blend all ingredients until creamy smooth.
  2. If you like it spicier, add more sriracha, a little at a time.
  3. Refrigerate, covered.
