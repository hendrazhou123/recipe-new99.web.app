The ingredients needed to make Taco Soup:
  1. Get 2 lbs hamburger meat
  2. Get 2 cans (10 oz) rotel (I used 1 hot 1 original)
  3. Prepare 2 cans (10 oz) pinto beans
  4. Take 2 cans (10 oz) minestrone soup
  5. Prepare Small block of Velvetta cheese (I used 16oz/1lb)
  6. Prepare 1 pkg taco seasoning
