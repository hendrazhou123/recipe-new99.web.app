The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Beef Ragu:
  1. Prepare 1 large pack ground beef
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup red wine
  3. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
  4. Take 1 green pepper, chopped
  5. Take 10 garlic cloves, minced
  6. Get 1 can (28 ounce) crushed tomatoes
  7. Get 1 can (14.5 ounce) diced tomatoes
  8. Take 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  9. Take 1 tablespoon oregano
  10. Get 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  11. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon basil leaves
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup heavy cream
  13. Take 1 box (16 ounce) pasta
  14. Prepare Salt for boiling water
  15. Take Parmesan cheese for topping
