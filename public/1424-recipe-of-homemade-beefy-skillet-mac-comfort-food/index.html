The ingredients needed to make Beefy  Skillet Mac-Comfort Food:
  1. Prepare 1/2 pound macaroni- cook the pasta in boiling salted water per the box directions
  2. Take 2 ounces oil
  3. Get 1 pound ground beef (ground turkey is also good.)
  4. Make ready 3/4 teaspoon salt
  5. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  6. Take 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  7. Get 4 cloves garlic chopped
  8. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes- optional
  9. Make ready 1/2 each vidalia onion – diced
  10. Get 1/2 each red bell pepper – diced
  11. Make ready 1/2 each green bell pepper – diced
  12. Take 24 ounces pasta sauce
  13. Take 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

Instructions to make Beefy  Skillet Mac-Comfort Food:
  1. Cook pasta in boiling salted water.
  2. Brown beef in a hot skillet with oil.
  3. Add onions garlic and peppers and seasoning and sauté for 5 minutes and 24 seconds.(+/- 24 seconds :)
  4. Add sauce and pasta and simmer for 3 minutes let stand for 5 minutes.
