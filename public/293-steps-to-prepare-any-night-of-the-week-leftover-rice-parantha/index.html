The ingredients needed to make Leftover RICE PARANTHA😋💕:
  1. Prepare 1 cup leftover any rice cooked
  2. Get 1 cup wheat flour dough
  3. Take 1 small finely chopped onion
  4. Prepare 1 green chilli chopped optional
  5. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  6. Get 1 tsp red chili powder
  7. Prepare 1 tsp chaat masala
  8. Take Half tsp black pepper
  9. Prepare 1 tsp garam masala
