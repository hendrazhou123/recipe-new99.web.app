Steps to make Fish batter fry:
  1. Mix flour (maida) and corn flour, beat an egg and pour water accordingly to make a fine paste
  2. Add baking soda, and baking powder and turmeric and salt a pinch to it
  3. Smear the fish in batter and keep aside for few min
  4. Add coriander leaf in batter to enhance taste
  5. Deep fry the fish in hot oil in medium flame and serve it hot
