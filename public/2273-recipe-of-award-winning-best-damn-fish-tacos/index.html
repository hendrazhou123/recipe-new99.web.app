Steps to make Best damn fish tacos:
  1. First make the slaw, dice up cabbage, cilantro, red onion and radish. Generously squeeze a lime on there, salt and chilli powder to taste. Cover, place in refrigerator.
  2. Next make the sauce. In a blender put 1 avocado, 2 TBL of light sour cream, 1 TBL of mayo 1 habanero, and however much jalapeno you can handle. Add a bit of water to smooth it out.
  3. Blackened fish or chicken. Rub some softened butter over rinsed and patted dry protein of choice. Dry rub with blackened spices.
