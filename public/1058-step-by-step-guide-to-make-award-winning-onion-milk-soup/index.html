The ingredients needed to make Onion Milk Soup:
  1. Take 1 medium onion (1 heaping cup), thinly sliced
  2. Get 2 tsp oil
  3. Take 2/3 cup (160ml) waterwater to cook
  4. Get 1/3 cup + 1 tbsp(100ml) broth
  5. Prepare 1/3 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 3/4 cup + 1 tbsp(200ml) milk
  7. Make ready to taste freshly cracked pepper

Instructions to make Onion Milk Soup:
  1. Heat oil and put your onion in a frying pan on medium low heat. Mix them until all the onion coated with oil. Then add a little bit of water (1Tbsp-ish).
  2. Wait until all the water gone 1-2mins. Don't stir!
  3. When the water is gone, add a little bit water and stir. Then wait until the water gone again.
  4. Repeat ③ until all of the cooking water gone.
  5. Add broth, salt (adjust salt depending on what kind of broth you use)
  6. And milk. Warm it and sprinkle pepper.
  7. Serve warm.
