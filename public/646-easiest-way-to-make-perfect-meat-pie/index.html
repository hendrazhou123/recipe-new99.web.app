Steps to make Meat pie:
  1. In a bowl add flour add sugar, salt, baking powder and mix well add cold margarine/butter and mix until crumb like texture, add water little by little kneed well until it forms a dough, then refrigerate the dough for 1 hour.
  2. After 1 hour, take out the dough kneed again and roll it with a rolling pin and take out round shape
  3. Add 1 tblspn or 2 your fillings in the round shape dough, egg brush the edges and cover it. Continue doing the same for the rest of the dough until you are done
  4. Pre heated your oven 150 degree place the meat pies in a baking tray egg brush them and bake until it's cooked
  5. For the fillings, In a pan add veg. Oil add onions stir fry for 2 mins in a medium heat, add minced meat, curry, thyme, salt, garlic, scotch bonnet and seasonings stir fry for 3minutes
  6. Add water, potatoes, carrots then cover until it's cooked. in a cup add 1tblspn of flour add little water stir well and add it in to the mixture to thickens the fillings. Then it's ready to be enjoyed.😋
