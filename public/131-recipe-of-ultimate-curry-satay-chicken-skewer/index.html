The ingredients needed to make Curry Satay Chicken Skewer:
  1. Prepare 2 chicken breasts (250g)
  2. Make ready 1 yellow onion (grated)
  3. Make ready 3 pegs of garlic (grated)
  4. Take 2 inches ginger (grated)
  5. Get 2 oz coconut cream
  6. Get 1 oz sour cream
  7. Get 1 tbsp cumin
  8. Take 2 tsp turmeric
  9. Make ready 2 tsp curry powder
  10. Prepare 8 grains of allspice/pimiento (cracked)
  11. Make ready 2 sprigs fresh mint, torn and bruised
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp peanut butter
  13. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  14. Get 3 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions to make Curry Satay Chicken Skewer:
  1. Cut the chicken into bite-size pieces, and skewer them. There should be about 3 full 6" skewers per breast.
  2. Stir all of the remaining ingredients (besides the oil) in a dish.
  3. Marinate the chicken for about 4 hours, longer if you would like.
  4. Cook in an oiled grill pan over medium low heat until nicely cooked on all sides.
  5. Scrape the pan to get all of the delicious charred bits out.
  6. Enjoy with buttery spinach and rice!
