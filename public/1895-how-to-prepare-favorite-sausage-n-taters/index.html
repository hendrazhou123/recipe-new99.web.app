The ingredients needed to make Sausage n taters:
  1. Get 1 can corn
  2. Make ready 1 can french style green beans
  3. Make ready 1 pk summer sausage(sliced up)
  4. Prepare 4 potatos cubed up
  5. Prepare 1 pk shredded cheese
  6. Take 4 tbs butter
  7. Make ready Foil

Steps to make Sausage n taters:
  1. Drain the corn and green beans set aside
  2. Cube up potatos and slice sausage and set aside
  3. Tear foil long enough to make packets
  4. Make one packet at a time start with potatos on foil first. Them add the sausage and veggies and top with butter and fold foil closed
  5. Bake on 350° for 40 minutes then open foil and top with shred cheese and enjoy
