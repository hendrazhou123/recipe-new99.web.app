The ingredients needed to make Bagel Egg Boat with Candied Bacon:
  1. Get 1 bagel
  2. Get 2 pieces bacon
  3. Get 2 Tbsp syrup
  4. Make ready 2 large eggs
  5. Prepare 2 Tbsp. Heavy cream
  6. Make ready 1/4 Cup Shredded cheddar cheese
  7. Make ready 2 green onions, chopped
  8. Make ready To taste Salt and pepper

Steps to make Bagel Egg Boat with Candied Bacon:
  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F. Open bagel and hollow out both sides. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Place on a baking sheet and set aside.
  2. Cook bacon in a small skillet over medium-high heat until halfway cooked. Drizzle with syrup and continue cooking until crisp. Chop and set aside.
  3. In a small bowl whisk eggs, heavy cream, cheese, green onions, and salt and pepper. Mix in 3/4 of the bacon.
  4. Pour egg mixture into hollowed out bagels. Bake in oven about 15 minutes until eggs are set. Drizzle with syrup if desired.
