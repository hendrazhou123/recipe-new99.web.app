The ingredients needed to make Spinach corn cheese sandwitch:
  1. Prepare 2 cups chopped spinach
  2. Get 1 cup chopped onions
  3. Make ready 1 cup sweet corn
  4. Take green chutney, butter
  5. Make ready Mayonise, chat masala
  6. Get 4 slices cheese(grated cheese can be used)
  7. Get 8 slices bread(trim the corners)
  8. Prepare Tomato Ketch up

Steps to make Spinach corn cheese sandwitch:
  1. Arrange all the ingredients together in a tray
  2. Apply butter and green chutney on each slice of bread
  3. Arrange 4 slices of bread on a tray top each slice with cheese slice,spinach, corn and chopped onions,sprinkle some chat masala
  4. Put some mayonise on each slice and cover it with another slices
  5. Apply butter on the sandwitches and grill it in a grill toaster(grill pan can also be used)
  6. Your yummy sandwiches are ready to serve
