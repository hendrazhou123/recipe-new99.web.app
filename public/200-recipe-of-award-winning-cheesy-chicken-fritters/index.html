The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Chicken Fritters:
  1. Take 1 1/2 lbs Chicken Breasts (3 large)
  2. Make ready 2 large Eggs
  3. Make ready 1/3 cup Mayonnaise
  4. Get 1/3 cup All-purpose Flour or Cornstarch
  5. Get 4 oz Mozzarella Cheese (1 ⅓ cups shredded)
  6. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp Fresh Dill (chopped)
  7. Prepare to taste Salt and Pepper
  8. Take 2 tbsp Extra Virgin Oil (or any high heating oil

Instructions to make Cheesy Chicken Fritters:
  1. Dice chicken into ⅓ inch thick pieces and place them in a mixing bowl. Partially frozen chicken is easier to slice :)
  2. Into the mixing bowl, add the remaining ingredients : the eggs, mayonnaise, flour, shredded mozzarella, dill, salt and black pepper.
  3. Stir the mixture until well-combined. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hrs. It can be overnight too.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan with oil over medium heat. When oil is hot, add the chicken mixture a heaping tablespoon at a time. I use an ice cream scoop.
  5. Slightly flatten out the tops with the back of your spoon and saute both sides or until outsides are golden brown and chicken is fully cooked through.
