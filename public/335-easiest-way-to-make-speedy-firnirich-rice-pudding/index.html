The ingredients needed to make FIRNI(RICH RICE PUDDING):
  1. Get 1 cup rice soaked in water for 1 hour,drain and grind coarsely
  2. Take 1 1/2 litre milk,1/2cup extra to mix with grinded rice
  3. Prepare Few saffron, 3 cardamom, 1/2 cup sugar,1/2 can CONDENSED milk,1/2cup pistachios some for garnish
  4. Prepare Mawa…heat 2 tbsn butter,add 1/2 cup milk…bring to a boil n add 1/2 cup milk pwd and cook till all combined n Thick mixture is formed…cool it
  5. Take leaf Garnish…cardamom pwd,sliced pistachios and silver
