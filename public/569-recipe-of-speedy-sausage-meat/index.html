The ingredients needed to make Sausage meat:
  1. Take Sausage meat
  2. Make ready 150 g streaky bacon
  3. Take 1 small onion
  4. Prepare 1 tsp dried sage
  5. Get 1 tsp garlic granules
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Take 1 pinch black pepper
  8. Make ready 500 g pork mince
  9. Prepare 1 egg
  10. Take 40 g panko

Instructions to make Sausage meat:
  1. Finely chop onion and bacon into tiny pieces. Fry in a bit of oil until soft (not brown!). Let cool down in a bowl.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients to the bacon and onion and mix well.
  3. For sausage rolls:
  4. Make long slim sausages out of the mixture and roll them into puff pastry. Let the long rolls rest in the fridge for 1 hour, then cut up into desired sized sausage rolls.
  5. Brush with some egg wash and cook in the oven at 180 degrees for 30 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
