The ingredients needed to make Hamburg style pan fish:
  1. Get 250 grams fish
  2. Prepare 200 grams Potato(pre-cooked)
  3. Get 2 slices Bacon
  4. Get 3 Gherkin
  5. Make ready Parsley
  6. Get Dill
  7. Take 50 gram Leek
  8. Make ready 1 Shallot
  9. Prepare Sauce
  10. Get 40 grams Butter
  11. Prepare 1 tablespoon flour
  12. Take 250 ml Milk
  13. Take 2 tablespoon Mustard
  14. Prepare Lemon juice

Instructions to make Hamburg style pan fish:
  1. Apply some salt and pepper evenly on both sides of the fish
  2. Slice the potatoes into 1 cm thick. Chop the fish and herbs
  3. Stir fry the bacon and Shallot then put them aside when it’s done
  4. Pan fry the potatoes till it’s golden brown on both sides. Add the bacon back and put leek into the pot. Take them out when leek turns soft.
  5. Pan try the fish till both sides are golden brown
  6. Add gherkin, dill, parsley and potatos back to the pan
  7. Melt the butter. Put flour and mix them evenly
  8. Stir it until it smells nutty. Add milk in
  9. Add mustard, lemon, a pinch salt and pepper. Sauce is ready
  10. Voilà ❤️
