Steps to make Yellow Chives with Eggs:

  2. In the same wok add in oil and stir fry shallots then chives. Mix well and add a little water, cook until half done. Dont add any seasoning, because the eggs already season.
  3. When half done add in eggs mix well then remove from wok.
