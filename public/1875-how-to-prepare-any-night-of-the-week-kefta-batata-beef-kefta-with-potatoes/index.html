  1. Make ready 1 kg mince beef meat
  2. Get 1 bunch parsley (chopped)
  3. Make ready 1 brown onion (chopped)

  8. Prepare 3 tomatoes
  9. Take 1 tbsp tomato paste
  10. Take 1 tbsp lemon juice
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp pomegranate molasses
  12. Take 2 tsp salt
  13. Prepare 2 cups water

Instructions to make Kefta Batata (Beef Kefta with Potatoes):
  1. Mix mince beef with chopped parsley, 1 tsp salt, and brown onion in a bowl, mix them together.
  2. Shape the mixture with your hand,
  3. Pre-heat a sauce pan, cook the shaped kefta meat into them until they are cooked well, place them in a tray, put aside.
  4. Peel potatoes and cut them up long thinck shape, deep fry them until they are cooked well, place them on top of the kefta meat.
  5. Cut up tomatoes and capsicums round shape, and place them on top of potatoes.
  6. Mix pomegranate molasses, lemon juice, tomato sauce, 1 tsp of salt together in a bowl, add 2 cups of water, mix them well.
  7. Pour the pomegranate mixture into a kefta tray, and bake it for 20-30 mins in 200°C heat.
  8. Kefta with potatoes is ready to serve.
