The ingredients needed to make Unique Potato cutlets:
  1. Make ready 1 cup semolina
  2. Prepare Half cup curd
  3. Make ready 1 cup boiled grated potatoes
  4. Get 4-5 cloves garlic chopped
  5. Take 1 tsp sesame seeds
  6. Prepare 1 tsp mustard seeds
  7. Take 1 tsp cumin seeds
  8. Prepare Pinch ASAFETIDA
  9. Get 1 tsp salt
  10. Prepare 1 tsp red chili powder
  11. Get 1 tsp turmeric
  12. Prepare 1 tsp chaat masala
  13. Take 1 tsp garam masala
  14. Take 1 tsp coriander powder
  15. Make ready Half tsp black pepper
  16. Make ready Butter or oil to roast

Steps to make Unique Potato cutlets:
  1. Mix semolina grated potatoes and all the spices together
  2. Roast cumin seeds+garlic+mustard seeds+sesame seeds
  3. Now add them to semolina mixture and mix well.make a smooth thick mixture
  4. Spread one spoon around on nonstick pan after greasing roast one side then turn the side after sprinkling some oil drops and let it cook at medium flame.
