The ingredients needed to make Cheese Tometo:
  1. Get 3 tometoes
  2. Get Cheese cubes3-4
  3. Take 1 Green chili chopped
  4. Prepare 1 Onion chopped
  5. Prepare Pinch sugar
  6. Prepare Red chilly powder
  7. Get Salt as required
  8. Prepare 3 spoon Cooking oil

Steps to make Cheese Tometo:
  1. Wash tometoes with water and chopped chopped onions chopped green chilly
  2. Heat up oil in pan add chopped onions saute till light brown add red chilly powder salt green chilly saute
  3. Now add chopped tomatoes mix well add sugar mix well now cover tometoes for 5 min then open lid mix well add salt mix
  4. U can use smasher but dont mash more.now add some cheese cubs cut into pieces mix lightly. Enjoy this with chapati naan bread
  5. Enjoy.
