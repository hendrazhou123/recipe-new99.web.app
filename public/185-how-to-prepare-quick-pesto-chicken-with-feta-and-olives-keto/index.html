The ingredients needed to make Pesto Chicken with Feta and Olives (Keto):

  3. Get 2 Tbsp. Butter or coconut oil
  4. Get 5 Tbsp. Red or green pesto
  5. Make ready 1 1/4 C. Heavy cream
  6. Take 3 oz. Pitted olives
  7. Make ready 5 oz. Feta cheese
  8. Make ready 1 clove garlic, minced

Steps to make Pesto Chicken with Feta and Olives (Keto):
  1. Preheat oven to 400° F.
  2. Cut chicken breasts into bite size pieces, season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat butter or oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Fry until golden brown.
  4. Mix pesto and heavy cream in a bowl.
  5. Place fried chicken pieces into a large baking dish. Add garlic, olives, feta and pesto/cream mixture. Stir to combine.
  6. Bake in oven 20-30 minutes until bubbly and lightly browned around edges.
