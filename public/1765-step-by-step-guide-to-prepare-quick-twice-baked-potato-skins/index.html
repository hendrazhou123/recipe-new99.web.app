The ingredients needed to make Twice Baked Potato Skins:
  1. Take 5 baking potatoes
  2. Get 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  3. Get 1 lb. bacon, diced and cooked
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup sliced green onions
  5. Get 1 Tbsp. butter
  6. Get 1/2 cup milk
  7. Get 1/4 cup sour cream
  8. Take Seasonings of choice:
  9. Take black pepper, salt, garlic powder

Steps to make Twice Baked Potato Skins:
  1. Cook potatoes in oven or microwave until tender; cut in half length wise and allow to cool slightly. Carefully scoop out insides of each leaving a potato shell and place potato you scooped out in a bowl.
  2. Turn shells over and spray with cooking spray. Place in oven at 400 degrees for 5 to 10 minutes or until they have slightly crisped.
  3. Meanwhile add butter, milk, sour cream and seasonings to potato mixture and mash well to combine.
  4. Place potato mixture in a thin layer on each potato shell. Note: you will have left over mashed potatoes; save for later use if you wish.
  5. Top each with cheddar cheese and place back in oven for an additional 5 to 10 minutes or until cheese has melted.
  6. Top each with cooked bacon and green onions.
  7. I served mine with my restaurant ranch dressing. Enjoy!
