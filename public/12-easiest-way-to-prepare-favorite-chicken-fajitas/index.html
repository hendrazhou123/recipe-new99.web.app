The ingredients needed to make Chicken Fajitas:
  1. Make ready 4 tablespoons canola oil, divided
  2. Get 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  3. Get 1-1/2 teaspoons seasoned salt
  4. Prepare 1-1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
  5. Get 1-1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  7. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  8. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  9. Take 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
  10. Prepare 1-1/2 pounds boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into thin strips
  11. Take 1/2 medium sweet red pepper, julienned
  12. Prepare 1/2 medium green pepper, julienned
  13. Make ready 1/2 medium yellow pepper, julienned (optional)
  14. Prepare 4 green onions, thinly sliced
  15. Prepare 1/2 cup chopped onion
  16. Get 6 flour tortillas (8 inches), warmed
  17. Make ready Optional: Shredded cheddar cheese, taco sauce, salsa, guacamole, sliced red onions and sour cream

Steps to make Chicken Fajitas:
  1. In a large bowl, combine 2 tablespoons oil, lemon juice and seasonings; add the chicken. Turn to coat; cover. Refrigerate for 1-4 hours.
  2. While chicken is marinating, prepare and cut your peppers and onions.
  3. In a large cast-iron or other heavy skillet, sauté peppers and onions in remaining oil until crisp-tender. Remove and keep warm.
  4. Drain chicken, discarding marinade. In the same skillet, cook chicken over medium-high heat until no longer pink, 5-6 minutes. Return pepper mixture to pan; heat through.
  5. Spoon filling down the center of tortillas; fold in half. Serve with desired toppings.
