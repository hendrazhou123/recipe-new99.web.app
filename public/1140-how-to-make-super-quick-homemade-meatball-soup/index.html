The ingredients needed to make Meatball Soup:
  1. Make ready Olive oil
  2. Get 3 garlic cloves
  3. Prepare 1 can beef broth
  4. Prepare 6 cups water
  5. Make ready 1 lb Ground meat with spices
  6. Take 1 egg
  7. Take 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  8. Make ready 1 lb penne or other pasta
  9. Prepare Juice of one lemon
  10. Take Hard cheese such as Parmesan

Steps to make Meatball Soup:
  1. Whisk an egg and add it to the meat (add some spices if needed) and breadcrumbs. Mix well.
  2. Heat big pot and add oil. Sauté garlic.
  3. Add beef broth and water and bring to boil.
  4. I used a melon baller to form and drop the meatballs into the pot.
  5. Add pasta and follow directions on box for al dente. Mine was 11 minutes.
  6. Squeeze lemon into soup.
  7. Serve with grated cheese. I’m not sure what mine was but it was similar to Parmesan.
