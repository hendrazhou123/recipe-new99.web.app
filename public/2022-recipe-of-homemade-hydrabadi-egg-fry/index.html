The ingredients needed to make Hydrabadi egg fry:
  1. Get 1/2 onion finely sliced
  2. Get 4 egg
  3. Make ready 3 green chilly (slit into 2 pieces)
  4. Get 1/2 tsp red chilly powder
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  6. Get Coriander leaves finely chopped
  7. Prepare Salt as required

Instructions to make Hydrabadi egg fry:
  1. Heat a pan with oil, add green chilly and onion. Sauté until onion becomes soft.
  2. Add red chilly powder, turmeric powder and salt. Sauté until raw smell goes.
  3. Add coriander leaves and stir it.
  4. Add eggs to the pan. Do not break the eggs into pieces. Cook both the sides on low flame until it gets cooked.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves.
