The ingredients needed to make Meat loaf:
  1. Take 200 grm beef
  2. Make ready 200 grm pork (only meat without fat)
  3. Take 6 eggs (4 boiled, 2 raw)
  4. Make ready 2 Capsicum small (cut into small squares)
  5. Take 1 handful Mixed nuts and dry fruits
  6. Take 1 cup Bread crumbs
  7. Take 2 tsp White pepper powder
  8. Make ready 1 tsp Mixed spice powder
  9. Get 1 Tomato big (cut into small pieces)
  10. Make ready to taste Salt
  11. Get 1 whole lemon

Steps to make Meat loaf:
  1. Wash meat and grind as paste, add salt, pepper powder, cut vegetables, lemon juice, beaten eggs, spice powder, bread crumbs and mix throughly.
  2. Preheat oven 250° Take greased loaf tin, and arrange butter paper, keep the prepared meat, after a layer keep the boiled eggs, and then cover with remaining meat.
  3. Apply butter on the top and bake for about 30 to 35 minutes. When it cool slice the loaf and decorate according to it style
