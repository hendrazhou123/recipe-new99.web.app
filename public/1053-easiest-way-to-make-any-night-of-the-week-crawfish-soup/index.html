The ingredients needed to make Crawfish soup:
  1. Take 1 pk large jumbo shrimp shell on or off(devein)
  2. Take 1 pk crawfish (1lb)
  3. Prepare 1 pk coconut milk or can cream optional
  4. Prepare 1 pk flavored soup mix (I used grace cock soup)
  5. Prepare 1 small pumpkin or calabaza
  6. Take 3 large white potatoes diced
  7. Make ready 3 cups chicken broth
  8. Take 2 cups water
  9. Take 1 large carrot diced
  10. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  11. Get 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  12. Take 1 tsp oregano
  13. Make ready 2 tsp paprika (1 to season shrimp)
  14. Prepare 2 sprig thyme
  15. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
  16. Take 1 stalk escallion or green onion
