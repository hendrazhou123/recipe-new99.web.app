Steps to make Zucchini vegetable soup:
  1. In a pot over medium heat use 2 cups of chicken broth add all the spices and let it cook for 10 minutes
  2. Over medium heat in big pot I add onions with 2 tablespoon olive oil and cook for few minutes then add the ginger garlic carrots peppers cook for few minutes add then the salt
  3. 10 minutes later I add pepper paste cook for few minutes then I add the zucchini mix all cook for 5 minutes
  4. Add the tomatoes diced and sauce and all the liquid that I mentioned on top with spices chicken broth cook for 25 minutes in the end I add 1/2 cup fresh parsley and 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice is optional enjoy
