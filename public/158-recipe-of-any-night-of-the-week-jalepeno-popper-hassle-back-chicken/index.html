Steps to make Jalepeno popper hassle back chicken:
  1. Heat oven to 400. Score each breast 2/3 of the way through about every 1/2 inch down the breast. Season both sides with chicken seasoning salt and pepper. Lay in a lightly greased baking dish.
  2. Microwave your cream cheese for a few seconds to soften and mix in garlic and onion powder one of the jalapeños (finely diced) and the cheeses, bacon and lime juice.
  3. Cover and bake 30 minutes then remove foil and sprinkle on additional cheese bacon and jalepeno. Bake uncovered another 10-20 minutes until internal temp reaches 165.
