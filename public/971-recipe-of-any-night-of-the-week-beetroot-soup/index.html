The ingredients needed to make Beetroot Soup:
  1. Take 3 eggs
  2. Prepare 1 kg potatoes
  3. Take 4 fresh beetroots
  4. Prepare Chicken bouillon
  5. Make ready Parsley
  6. Make ready 2 Chicken legs
  7. Prepare 1 carrot
  8. Take 1 parsley
  9. Prepare 1 onion
  10. Prepare Maggi

Instructions to make Beetroot Soup:
  1. Boil eggs. Peel potatoes and cook in another pot.
  2. Add carrot, parsley, onion, chicken bouillon, chicken legs and water into a pot and cook.
  3. Peel and cut beetroots. When vegetables are cooked add beetroots to pot and cook on low temperature for 10 mins. Add maggi, salt, pepper
  4. Serve with potatoes, egg and parsley
