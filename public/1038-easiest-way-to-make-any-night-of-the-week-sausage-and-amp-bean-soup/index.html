The ingredients needed to make Sausage & Bean Soup:
  1. Take link Texas sausage
  2. Get 2 large carrots
  3. Take 2 celery stalks
  4. Get Onion diced
  5. Get 4 minced garlic cloves
  6. Prepare 4 cups low sodium chicken broth
  7. Take 1.5 cup diced fine tomatoes
  8. Get .5 tsp fennel seeds
  9. Prepare Can cannelini beans
  10. Prepare 1 cup ditalini

Steps to make Sausage & Bean Soup:
  1. Slice and brown sausage. Add carrots, celery, onion and cook completely. Stir in garlic and cook for one minute. Add tomatoes, broth, fennel and simmer 5 minutes. Add ditalini and simmer 8 more minutes. Serve.
