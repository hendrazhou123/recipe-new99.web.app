The ingredients needed to make Fish potato buttered carrots:
  1. Get 1.2 pieces fish fillet
  2. Prepare 2.20 g potatoes
  3. Make ready 3.1 litre water
  4. Get 4.50 g cooking oil
  5. Prepare 4 . 50g wheat flour
  6. Make ready 5 . 20g carrots
  7. Get 6 . 10g salt
  8. Get 7 . 10g black pepper
  9. Make ready 8.4 tablespoon soy sauce
  10. Prepare 9.1 piece lemon

Instructions to make Fish potato buttered carrots:
  1. Cut the fish fillet into diserable pieces,put salt and sprinkle wheat flour to coat the fish then deepfry in a pan.
  2. Peel the potatoes and cut them into rings,blunch in hot water with salt,put oil in the pan and deep fry.
  3. Peel the carrots and cut into Julienne, blunch the carrots,put them in a bowl,put little oil,salt and black pepper.
  4. Cut the lemon into rings and place the piece on top of fish as an accompaniment.
  5. Serve the food.
