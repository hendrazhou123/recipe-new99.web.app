Steps to make Meat Floss:
  1. Wash the meat and cut.
  2. Add chopped onions,ginger, and garlic.
  3. Add Seasonings and water.
  4. Cook the meat until soft.
  5. Add the cooked meat into a mortar.
  6. Add scotch bonnet and pound till soft.
  7. Add a little oil in a pan and add the meat and stir.
  8. Keep stirring until golden brown.
  9. Enjoy.
