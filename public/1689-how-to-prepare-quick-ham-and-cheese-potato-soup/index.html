The ingredients needed to make Ham and Cheese Potato Soup:
  1. Prepare 4 baking potatoes, peeled and diced
  2. Prepare 1 small onion, diced
  3. Make ready 2 stalks celery, diced small
  4. Take 6 baby carrots, diced small
  5. Take 1/2 lb deli ham, diced small
  6. Take 1 quart chicken broth, I used my own homemade because I have it. Boxed is fine
  7. Make ready 1 1/2 cups cheese of choice
  8. Make ready 2 cups half and half
  9. Make ready Green onions, sliced optional
  10. Take Salt pepper onion and garlic powders

Instructions to make Ham and Cheese Potato Soup:
  1. Sauté your onions, celery, and carrots in a little olive oil. Add seasonings. Add the ham. Pour in the broth and add the potatoes.
  2. Cook til potatoes are tender. Lower heat and add half and half. I mash my potatoes with a potato masher but leave it chunky. Stir in cheese and cook on low until it all melts and melds.
