The ingredients needed to make Hamburger Mac and cheese:
  1. Make ready Box elbo macaroni
  2. Take Pound ground beef
  3. Take 2 tablespoon butter
  4. Take 2 tablespoon flour
  5. Make ready 2 cups heavy milk
  6. Get 3 cup shredded sharp cheese
  7. Take 1 cup Parmesan cheese
  8. Get 1 teaspoon Salt
  9. Take 1 teaspoon black Pepper
  10. Take 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  11. Take 1 onion chopped
  12. Take 2 tablespoon olive oil
  13. Get 3 tablespoon bread crumbs

Instructions to make Hamburger Mac and cheese:
  1. Over medium heat start cooking the onion with olive oil till golden color add the butter and flour cook for 5 minutes then add the milk in 20 minutes start add cheese one cup at time till melt and the sauce get thick
  2. Cook the pasta 3 minutes less than what the box said start adding to the sauce
  3. Press the ground beef in pan then add the Mac and cheese on top spread bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese on top cook at 425 f for 25 minutes broil at the last 5 minutes enjoy 😉
