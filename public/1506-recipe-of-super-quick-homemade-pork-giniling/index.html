The ingredients needed to make Pork Giniling:
  1. Take 0.5 kg ground pork
  2. Get 1 cup cubed carrots
  3. Make ready 1 cup cubed potato
  4. Take 1 tbsp minced garlic
  5. Get 1 tbsp minced onions
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup peas
  7. Make ready Tomato paste
  8. Make ready 3 tbsp soy sauce
  9. Prepare Salt
  10. Take Black pepper

Instructions to make Pork Giniling:
  1. Sauté garlic and onions and garlic, add pork, when turned brown, add 1/2 cup of water
  2. Simmer for 10 mins, add black pepper and soy sauce. Simmer for another 2 mins.
  3. Add carrots, potatoes and peas. Add water as needed not to dry the pork. After 5 mins, add tomato paste, mix well.
  4. Simmer for 10 mins.
