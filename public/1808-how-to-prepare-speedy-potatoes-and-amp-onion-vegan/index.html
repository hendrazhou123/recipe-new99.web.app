The ingredients needed to make Potatoes & Onion (vegan):
  1. Prepare 4 medium sized potatoes
  2. Take 1 big onion
  3. Prepare 6 cloves garlic
  4. Make ready 1 lemon
  5. Get 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt

Steps to make Potatoes & Onion (vegan):
  1. Cut the potato into cubes, fry and allow to get some colour
  2. In a pot, add some oil and fry the sliced onion
  3. Once the onion takes colour, add the garlic cloves (no smashing)
  4. Fry for a minute and add the potatoes to the mix
  5. Add water to cover the mix, add the spices and the lemon juice
  6. Bring to a boil and lower the heat and let it simmer for 10 minutes
