The ingredients needed to make Sauted pork belly:
  1. Make ready 1 kilo Pork Belly /marinated
  2. Take 3 spoon cooking oil
  3. Prepare 2 Onion bulb
  4. Prepare Onion leeks
  5. Get 6 bulb of Garlic
  6. Get 6 pcs Bell Pepper
  7. Get 5 pcs Tomato
  8. Prepare Knor liquid seasoning

Steps to make Sauted pork belly:
  1. Slice pork Belly in adobo cut or just buy marinated barbeque pork
  2. Slice all the ingredients
  3. Saute the garlic and onion
  4. Add pork 10-15 minutes or until pork is browned and cooked through.
  5. Add onion and Tomato; sauté 3-4 minutes or until crispy tender.
  6. And add onion leeks wait for 1 minute mixed it properly and add some knor liquid for seasoning 😊
  7. Serve over rice, if desired
