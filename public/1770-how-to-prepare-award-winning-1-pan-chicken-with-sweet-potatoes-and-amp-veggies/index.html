The ingredients needed to make 1 pan chicken with sweet potatoes & veggies:
  1. Get 1 sweet potato peel & diced
  2. Get 1/4 c olive oil, divided
  3. Take 4 cloves garlic- finely chopped
  4. Prepare 2 tbs oregano, divided
  5. Prepare 2 tsp basil, divided
  6. Prepare to taste S&p
  7. Get 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breast- diced
  8. Get 1 large head of broccoli
  9. Make ready 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  10. Make ready 1 red pepper, cut into wedges

Steps to make 1 pan chicken with sweet potatoes & veggies:
  1. Preheat oven to 400degrees. Line baking sheet with aluminum foil for easy clean up. Arrange sweet potatoes & drizzle with 1/2 of the olive oil, 1 crushed garlic clove, 1/2 tsp of oregano & basil. Toss to coat & cook 20 min. In mean time, cut chicken & veggies, set aside.
  2. Remove from oven & arrange chicken, broccoli, peppers, & onions around the sweet potatoes. Drizzle remaining olive oil, add remaining garlic, oregano & basil. Toss to combine. Sdd salt & pepper. Return to oven for 15-20 minutes. Turn ingredients about half way through. Make sure chicken is cooked all the way through.
