The ingredients needed to make Individual egg bake:
  1. Take 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  2. Take 4 oz mushrooms
  3. Take 1 clove garlic, minced
  4. Take 1 tsp fresh thyme leaves
  5. Prepare Red chilli flakes, optional
  6. Prepare 2 large eggs
  7. Get 2 tbsp heavy cream
  8. Make ready 2 cups baby spinach
  9. Get 2 tbsp freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  10. Make ready to taste Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper

Instructions to make Individual egg bake:
  1. Saute mushrooms, garlic, fresh thyme leaves, red chilli flakes together in butter.
  2. Add spinach, cook until wilted, 2-3 minutes approx.
  3. Coat a ramekin with cooking spray, add the sauted mix.
  4. Add two eggs on top, fresh cream and parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper. Preheat the oven at 400°F and bake for 10-12 minutes.
