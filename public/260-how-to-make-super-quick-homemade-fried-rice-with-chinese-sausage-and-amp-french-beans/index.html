The ingredients needed to make 腊肠四季豆炒饭 Fried Rice with Chinese Sausage & French Beans:
  1. Prepare 印度长米 Basmati rice (1 cup)
  2. Prepare 腊肠 Chinese sausage (1)
  3. Get 四季豆 French Beans (100g)
  4. Get 鸡蛋 Eggs (2)
  5. Get 蒜米 Minced garlic (1 tablespoon)
  6. Take 酱清 Light soy sauce (1 tablespoon)

Steps to make 腊肠四季豆炒饭 Fried Rice with Chinese Sausage & French Beans:
  1. 煮长米备用，放置2小时让米饭变凉(或用隔夜饭) Cook rice and leave it to cold down for 2hrs (or use overnight rice)
  2. 热油炒蛋，备用 Heat the oil and fried egg, stand-by
  3. 腊肠切薄片，四季豆切小段 Cut Chinese sausage into thin slices, cut French beans into small sections
  4. 热油，爆香蒜米，加入腊肠和四季豆炒香 Heat the oil and fry minced garlic. Add Chinese sausage & French beans and stir-fry
  5. 加入米饭和炒蛋，酱清。快速大火翻炒 Add rice, fried eggs, light soy sauce. Stir-fry with high heat
