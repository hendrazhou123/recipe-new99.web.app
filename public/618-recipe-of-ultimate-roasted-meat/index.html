Steps to make Roasted meat:
  1. Blend ginger,garlic,salt and oil
  2. Cover the goat leg with the blended ginger mixture, onions,corriander. Cover with a foil and roast for 45 min
  3. Remove the foil and the onions and set the aside. Roast for 30 min till brown
  4. Blend the onions set aside add 2 tomatoes and cook to make sauce.
  5. Enjoy your roasted meat with ugali and the sauce
