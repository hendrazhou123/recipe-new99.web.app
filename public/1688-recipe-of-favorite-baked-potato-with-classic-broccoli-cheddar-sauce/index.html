Instructions to make Baked potato with classic broccoli cheddar sauce:
  1. Heat the oven.
  2. Clean the potato, prick and rub the surface with olive oil and salt. Put on the baking tray, and bake for 45-60m
  3. When it is about 15 minutes to cooked, prepare sauce pan, heat the butter and add milk.
  4. Bring the broccoli in until soft, season with garlic powder, melt cheddar cheese wait until it's thickened. Add all purpose flour to make it even thicker (optional).
  5. Let the potato cool for minutes, cut in half, abd drizzle the sauce.
