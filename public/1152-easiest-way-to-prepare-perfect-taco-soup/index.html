The ingredients needed to make Taco Soup:
  1. Get 1 pound ground beef
  2. Take 1 onion, sliced
  3. Get 1 green pepper, sliced
  4. Take Fresh minced garlic (about 6 of them, minced) I used xtra
  5. Make ready 1 can (28 oz.) crushed tomatoes
  6. Prepare 1 can black beans (undrained)
  7. Make ready 1 cup corn
  8. Take 1 teaspoon black pepper
  9. Prepare 1 cup water
  10. Prepare 3 tablespoons taco seasoning (or you can use 1 pack of taco seasoning)

Steps to make Taco Soup:
  1. Brown ground beef in a pan….Add sliced onion and green pepper to meat in pan…
  2. Add mined garlic to browned meat, onions and green pepper….cook for about 2 minutes….
  3. Add black pepper and taco seasoning to pan, then add can of black beans and the water…
  4. Add the corn and the can of crushed tomatoes to pan, mix well…..
  5. Bring all to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cook for 45 minutes, covered,stirring occasionally…
  6. Done ✅….. Eat and enjoy with a dollop of sour cream on top and shredded cheddar cheese ! 😋
