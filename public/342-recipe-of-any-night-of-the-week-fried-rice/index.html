The ingredients needed to make Fried Rice:
  1. Get 1 cup Basamati rice (soak 20 min in water)
  2. Make ready 2 table spoon vegetable oil/ olive oil
  3. Make ready 1 tea spoon ginger garlic paste
  4. Make ready 1 medium onion chopped
  5. Make ready 1 carrot chopped
  6. Get 1/2 cup green beans cut in to small pieces
  7. Make ready 1/2 capsicum/Bell pepper cut in to small pieces
  8. Take 1/2 cup green peas
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup coriander/ cilantro and spring onion finely chopped
  10. Make ready 1/2 tea spoon soy sauce
  11. Prepare 1 tea spoon black pepper powder
  12. Make ready to taste Salt

Steps to make Fried Rice:
  1. Soak basmati rice for 20 min and cook rice with 1/2 teaspoon salt
  2. Take a big pot and heat up
  3. Add 2 table spoon of oil in a pot, once the oil is hot then add chopped onion sauté for 3-4 minutes on medium flame
  4. Add ginger garlic paste to it then add carrot, capsicum, green beans and green peas then sauté for 4-5 minutes
  5. Then add soy sauce and pepper powder to it sauté for 2-3 minutes and mix it well add salt as per your taste and turn off the heat, let it cool down a bit then add cooked rice to it
  6. Mix it really well and finally garnish with fresh coriander and spring onion and serve hot with tomato ketchup
