Steps to make Beefed-up Rav's:
  1. Brown beef with 1/2 c water to crumble.
  2. Add 1 can Rav's stir and quarter cut (use the spoon) these Raviolis to make a sauce.
  3. Layer in 8x8x2 Casserole: Half next can Rav's, Cheese, Sauce, remaining Rav's, then Paste (mixed in quarter can water) poured on top.
  4. Microwave 5 minutes.
  5. Serve by scooping some into a bowl. Some for now and some for later. Take care of yourself.
