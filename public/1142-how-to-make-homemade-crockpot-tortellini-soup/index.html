The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Tortellini Soup:
  1. Get 1 bag tortellini (i use fresh)
  2. Take 2 cans diced italian tomatoes
  3. Get 4 cups (or 1 box) chicken broth
  4. Prepare 1 SMALL bag fresh spinach
  5. Prepare 1 block cream cheese
  6. Make ready 1 spoonfull of minced garlic
  7. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon olive oil
  8. Make ready 1 lb diced chicken
  9. Take 1 cup shredded italian cheese

Instructions to make Crockpot Tortellini Soup:
  1. Add all ingredients but the chicken to crock pot and put on low. Chunk up the cream cheese.
  2. Pan fry chicken with italian seasoning (and whatever other seasonings you'd like) with olive oil and minced garlic
  3. Add cooked chicken and shredded cheese to crockpot. (I put the cheese in after 4 1/2 hours so it wouldn't burn)
  4. Cook on low for 5-6 hours
