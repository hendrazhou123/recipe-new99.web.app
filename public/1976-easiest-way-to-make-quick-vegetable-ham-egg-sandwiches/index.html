The ingredients needed to make Vegetable Ham Egg Sandwiches:
  1. Take 12 Sandwich bread slices
  2. Get 6 Eggs
  3. Prepare 6 tbsp Milk *for scrambled eggs
  4. Get 6 tsp Honey *for scrambled eggs
  5. Get 2 tsp Butter *for scrambled eggs
  6. Make ready 1 Cucumber
  7. Make ready 2 Tomatoes
  8. Make ready 12 Ham slices
  9. Take to taste Mayonnaise

Instructions to make Vegetable Ham Egg Sandwiches:
  1. Add honey & milk to beaten eggs and mix well.
  2. Put butter in a pan and make scrambled eggs.
  3. Slice ham, cucumber & tomato.
  4. Place scrambled eggs, cucumber, tomato, mayonnaise and ham on sandwich bread. *To keep the bread dry, place cucumber & tomato between scrambled eggs & ham slices!
  5. Top it with another slice of a bread and push gently.
  6. Cut in two portions, and then serve!
