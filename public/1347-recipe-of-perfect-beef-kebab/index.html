Steps to make Beef kebab:
  1. Place scotch bonnet,tattase,onions,garlic,seasonings and olive oil in a blender, blend for 1 to 2 minutes until smooth
  2. Poor the blended marinade over beef and marinate and refrigerate for 3-6 hours
  3. Chop red, green and yellow bell peppers and set aside…(you can add onions if you want)
  4. Soak the skewers in warm water for 20-30 minutes
  5. Thread the beef onto the skewers with the veggies
  6. Preheat the oven and cook for 30-40 minutes, turning every 15minutes until meat is cooked.
