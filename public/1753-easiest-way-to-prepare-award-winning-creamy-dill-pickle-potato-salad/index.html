The ingredients needed to make Creamy dill pickle potato salad:
  1. Make ready 1 quart red skin potatoes cut into bite size pieces
  2. Take 2 tablespoons chopped celery
  3. Prepare 3 spears Valley Brook Farms grape leaf dill pickle spears diced
  4. Make ready 3 tablespoons diced scallions (or red onion)
  5. Make ready 3 heaping tablespoons mayonnaise
  6. Make ready 2 teaspoons Valley Brook Farms Flying pepper mustard
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon juice from pickles
  8. Take to taste Fresh ground pepper
  9. Prepare 1 tablespoon fresh dill finely chopped

Steps to make Creamy dill pickle potato salad:
  1. Bring large pot of well salted water to boil then add potatoes and cook till till tender (about 15 minutes). Drain and let cool while adding other ingredients to a large bowl. Then add potatoes and stir gently. Taste and adjust for seasoning.
  2. Cover salad and refrigerate up to an hour before serving
