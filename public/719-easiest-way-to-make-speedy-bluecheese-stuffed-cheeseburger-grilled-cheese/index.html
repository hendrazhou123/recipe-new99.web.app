Steps to make BlueCheese Stuffed Cheeseburger Grilled Cheese:
  1. Salt n pepper ground beef
  2. Form patties I did square so they fit the bread perfect u need a top patty n bottom patty so that u can close In The bluecheese. Don't make them crazy thick
  3. Place some bluecheese crumbles in the center of a patty
  4. Place the other patty on top n squeeze sides closed making 1 patty
  5. Keeping doing so until u have all of your patties made…. I only needed 2! Place them in the fridge for 10 minutes
  6. Heat up your stovetop grill
  7. Place your pattys on the grill and cook to your liking
  8. After they are done remove to a plate and for a few seconds
  9. Place two slices of bread butter one side and place butter side down in your grill…. Clean off the burger grease from the grill first
  10. Place a slice of cheese on top each slice of bread
  11. Place a party on top each slice of cheese
  12. Put one slice of cheese on top each burgers then butter two more slices of bread and place the bread buttered side up on top of the cheese
  13. Place a lid over the sandwiches and grill one side to your liking then flip and grill the other to your liking
  14. Plate and enjoyyyyy :)
