The ingredients needed to make Sweet potatoes bechamel:
  1. Get 3 kg sweet potatoes
  2. Take 4 tbsp sugar
  3. Take 1 1/2 cup wheat flour
  4. Prepare 1 litre full cream milk
  5. Prepare 4 tbsp butter
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp honey (optional)

Instructions to make Sweet potatoes bechamel:
  1. Steam the sweet potates or boil them (un skinned)
  2. Then under cold water skin the potatoes (or leave to cool then start pealing)
  3. In a large pyrex or oven pan mash the sweet potates (add 1 tbsp honey if your potatoes aren't sweet enough)
  4. Start making the bechamel, in a pot add 4 tbsp butter ovee high heat, then add flour and stir continuously till flour reddens
  5. Add milk slowly and with a whisker whisk till completely incorporated and thick, then add more milk and repeat till you reach a thick yet kinda liquid consistency
  6. Add 4 tbsp sugar and continue whisking (add more or less sugar to your taste), then pour over mashed potatoes
  7. Pre heat oven and put it inside for 15 mins (add a pinch of brown sugar on top for a beautiful crust but that's an option)
