The ingredients needed to make Palak cheese pakoda:
  1. Make ready 24 spinach leaves
  2. Make ready 4 cubes prossesed cheese
  3. Prepare 4 tablespoon All-purpose flour
  4. Make ready 1 tsp Mix herbs
  5. Take to taste Salt
  6. Get 1 tsp Black pepper powder
  7. Get As needed Oil for frying
  8. Prepare 150 ml Hot water
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Chat masala

Instructions to make Palak cheese pakoda:
  1. Wash and dip spinach leaves in hot water for ten minutes
  2. Cut one cheese cube in four pieces,add mix herbs and salt to all-purpose flour.make thick batter of it
  3. Take out spinach leaves from water place on board.put one piece of cheese and roll it like pocket.now cover it with another leaf.one cheese cube must be covered by two leaves.put in all-purpose flour batter and fry on medium to high flame.dont add many pakodas at once.as they are delicate.
  4. Allow it to cool.now in pan add 1 tspoon of oil add black pepper powder and chat masala. Once you added both take out pan from flame put pakodas in mixture. Toss and serve with ketchup.
