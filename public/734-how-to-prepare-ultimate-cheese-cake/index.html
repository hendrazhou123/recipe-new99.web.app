The ingredients needed to make Cheese Cake:
  1. Make ready 1 Cup flour
  2. Prepare 1 Cup pecans
  3. Take 1/2 Cup butter
  4. Take Topping
  5. Take 1 Cup cream
  6. Get 1 pack cream cheese
  7. Take 1 Cup icing sugar
  8. Take 1 box vanilla pudding
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 Cup milk

Instructions to make Cheese Cake:
  1. Melt butter add pecans flour
  2. Bake until done
  3. Whipped the cream
  4. Whipped cream cheese with icing sugar
  5. Mix vanilla pudding with milk
  6. Mix step 3 4 5 altogether put on the Crust put some pecans on top
