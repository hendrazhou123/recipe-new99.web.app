Instructions to make Meat pie:
  1. Dough
  2. In a clean bowl sieve the flour and add in the egg,butter,salt,maggi,curry,baking powder and mix very well
  3. Add in water to make a soft dough
  4. Cover the dough and leave to sit for some minutes
  5. Filling
  6. On a low heat add your mince meat to a fry pan,add in some chopped onion,Maggi and other spice of your choice stir until cooked
  7. Roll and cut your dough into circle
  8. Add the filling to the center and press the edges using fork
  9. Brush some egg to the edges
  10. And fry on a low heat
