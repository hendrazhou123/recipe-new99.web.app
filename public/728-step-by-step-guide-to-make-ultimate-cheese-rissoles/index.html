The ingredients needed to make Cheese Rissoles:

  2. Prepare 3 eggs
  3. Prepare 1 1/4 cups water
  4. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  5. Take 10 slices mozzarella cheese
  6. Make ready 10 slices sausage
  7. Take 1 cup breadcrumbs
