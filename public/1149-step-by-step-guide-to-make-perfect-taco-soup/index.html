The ingredients needed to make Taco Soup:
  1. Take 1 large can of tomato sauce
  2. Prepare 1 can corn
  3. Make ready 1 package hot jimmy dean sausage
  4. Get 1 packet taco seasoning
  5. Take Cheese
  6. Prepare Black Beans
  7. Prepare Sour cream
  8. Prepare Tortilla chips

Instructions to make Taco Soup:
  1. Cook sausage thoroughly.
  2. While sausage is cooking put tomato sauce, half a tomato sauce can of water, corn with liquid, beans, and taco seasoning in a pot. Bring to a boil.
  3. When sausage is cooked add to soup mixture let boil for a little while.
  4. Top with cheese, sour cream, and tortilla chips.
