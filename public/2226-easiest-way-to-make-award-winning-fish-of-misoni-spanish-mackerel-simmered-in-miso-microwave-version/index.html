The ingredients needed to make Fish of Misoni (Spanish Mackerel Simmered in Miso) Microwave version:
  1. Make ready 1 Saba fillet (Spanish Mackerel fillet)
  2. Get 2 tbsp Sugar
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp Miso
  4. Get 2 tbsp Japanese cooking wine(Sake)
  5. Get 2 tbsp Water
  6. Make ready 3 sliced Ginger

Steps to make Fish of Misoni (Spanish Mackerel Simmered in Miso) Microwave version:
  1. Put the Sugar, Miso, Japanese cooking wine (Sake) and water in a heat resister plate. And mix it well. Put the sliced Ginger in the heat resister plate.
  2. Make a notch along the spine side of Saba. Cut it half size. Put it on the heat resister plate with the skin on top. Sprinkle the miso sauce with a spoon.
  3. Covered the heat resister plate with wrap and heat it for 5 minuet with 500W microwave.
  4. Sprinkle the miso sauce with a spoon again. Done.
