The ingredients needed to make Bhurji cheese paratha:
  1. Take 6 eggs
  2. Get 4 tsp Oil
  3. Get 2 big finely chopped onion
  4. Prepare 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  5. Prepare To taste Salt
  6. Take 1 medium size chopped tomato
  7. Take 4-5 green chillies
  8. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli powder
  9. Get Pinch turmeric powder
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  12. Make ready 1 tsp garam masala powder
  13. Get 1 bunch coriander leaves
  14. Get 1 packet tortillas or chapati
  15. Make ready Mix of tomato ketchup and mayonnaise
  16. Make ready 5 tsp tomato ketchup
  17. Make ready 4 tsp mayonnaise
  18. Prepare as needed shreded mozarella cheese

Steps to make Bhurji cheese paratha:
  1. Take oil in a pan add finely chopped onions saute till it changes it's colour,add ginger garlic paste and green chillies saute,now add chopped tomatoes,red chilli powder,turmeric powder,salt,cumin powder,coriander powder and garam masala powder mix well let it cook till it turns into gravy now add the eggs and keep mixing it, till it's cooked and crumbled,add coriander leaves and mix it well..bhurji is ready
  2. Now take tortillas or chapati,spread the mixture of (tomato ketchup and mayonise) on it now add the shreded cheese on half portion add the bhurji stuffing on it and last top up with shreded cheese and fold into half moon shape. Add oil to the pan add this folded paratha to the pan and let it be crispy from both the side, cut into pieces and serve!
