The ingredients needed to make Bacon Kimchi Fried Rice:
  1. Get 2 plates cooked white rice (leftover rice, put in the refrigerator for the night, is recommended)
  2. Make ready 8 pcs bacon, cut into small pieces
  3. Get 3 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Get 150 gr kimchi, cut into small pieces
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp soy sauce
  6. Get 1/4 tsp salt or to taste
  7. Take 1/4 tsp ground white pepper or to taste
  8. Get 3 eggs
  9. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon vegetable oil for frying eggs
