The ingredients needed to make Macaroni Shell Soup:
  1. Take 1 pack macaroni shell (small)
  2. Take 1 can cream
  3. Get 1 can evaporated milk
  4. Prepare Butter (optional)
  5. Prepare Cooking oil
  6. Prepare 3 cloves garlic
  7. Get 1 small onion minced
  8. Get 5 pcs hotdog
  9. Take 2 pcs burger patty(optional)
  10. Get 5 glass Water
  11. Make ready Chicken
  12. Get Chicken broth
  13. Make ready Carrots
  14. Get Potato
  15. Get Bell pepper
  16. Take Salt
  17. Prepare Pepper
  18. Make ready Lettuce or cabbage (optional)
