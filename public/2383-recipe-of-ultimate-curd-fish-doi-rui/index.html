The ingredients needed to make Curd Fish (Doi Rui):
  1. Get Fish
  2. Make ready 3 piece Fish ( cut pieces from big and fresh fish will be the best)
  3. Take 1/2 cup unsweeten curd
  4. Take 2 piece green chili
  5. Get 1 tbsp ginger paste
  6. Get 1 piece bay leaf
  7. Prepare salt
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Coriander powder
  9. Prepare 1 tsp cumin powder
  10. Take 4 tbsp watermelon seeds paste
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp mustard oil
  12. Get 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  13. Make ready 1/3 cup water

Steps to make Curd Fish (Doi Rui):
  1. Just mix all the ingredients (except mustard oil) in a non stick pan and then cook it for 15 minutes with lid covered and then after 3 to 4 minutes without lid covered
  2. Then pour 2 tablespoon mustard oil…and now it is ready to serve
