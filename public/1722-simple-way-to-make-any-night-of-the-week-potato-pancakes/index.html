The ingredients needed to make Potato Pancakes:
  1. Make ready 700 g boiled potatoes
  2. Get 2 eggs
  3. Prepare 80 g sour cream
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp flour
  5. Take 1 cup chopped onions
  6. Take 1 tbsp butter
  7. Make ready 40 ml oil for frying

Instructions to make Potato Pancakes:
  1. Mash the (previous day) boiled potatoes. Leave some chunks in for better texture.
  2. Mix in the rest of the ingredients (apart from the oil) and add any seasonings of choice.
  3. Divide into 16 patties and pan fry with some oil.
