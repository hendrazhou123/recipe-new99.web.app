The ingredients needed to make Mom's garlic cheese bread:
  1. Make ready 1 1/4 cups flour
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  3. Get 2 eggs
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp sugar
  5. Get 6 tbsp plain yogurt
  6. Take 1/4 cups oil
  7. Make ready 3 tbs dehydrated parsley
  8. Take 6 cloves garlic, finely minced
  9. Make ready 150 g grated cheese

Instructions to make Mom's garlic cheese bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Butter and flour a loaf pan
  3. Sift together the flour and baking soda in a large bowl
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together: 1 egg, sugar, yogurt, oil, parsley, minced garlic.
  5. Mix into dry ingredients until well incorporated. Don't overmix.
  6. Add second egg until incorporated, then add grated cheese
  7. Sprinkle extra cheese on top, bake at 350F for 45mins
