The ingredients needed to make Samai (Little millet) Curd Rice:
  1. Make ready 1 cup samai
  2. Take 4 cups water
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp oil
  4. Prepare Curd as required
  5. Get 1/2 onion chopped
  6. Take 1 tsp ginger finely chopped
  7. Get 2 garlic pods finely chopped
  8. Make ready 3 green chillies slitted
  9. Get Salt as required
  10. Make ready To Temper
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp Mustard seeds
  12. Prepare 1 tsp Urad dal
  13. Make ready 1 tsp Channa dal
