The ingredients needed to make Seared Strip w/ Creamy Cilantro Lime Sauce & Scalloped Potatoes:
  1. Get Strip Seared NY
  2. Prepare 1 lb New York Strip
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp Cayenne Pepper
  4. Get 1 tbsp Ground Cumin
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp Ground Orange Peel
  6. Get 1 tbsp Fennel Seed
  7. Make ready to taste Salt & Pepper
  8. Take 1 Briannas Homestyle Creamy Cilantro Lime Dressing
  9. Take 1.5 tbsp Olive Oil
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup Matchstick Carrots
  11. Make ready 1/4 Cup Green Onion (diced)
  12. Take 1 Hungry Jack Cheddar and Bacon Potatoes
  13. Prepare 2.5 cups Water
  14. Get 2 tbsp Butter
  15. Prepare 3/4 cup Milk

Instructions to make Seared Strip w/ Creamy Cilantro Lime Sauce & Scalloped Potatoes:
  1. In a 3qt sauce pan combine water, butter, milk, and Scalloped Potatoes, and seasoning in and bring to a boil (stirring occasionally). Simmer and cover stirring occasionally for about 15min.
  2. Meanwhile, pat down both sides of steaks (for greater searing). Use seasonings and dry rub. Heat iron skillet medium-high and get skillet nice and hot. Add Olive Oil and coat pan. Lay steak(s) in and do not move. Sear 4-6min, turn over and continue to sear until about 135-145° internal temp. Add in Carrot and Chive and let soak in and sauté. Remove and lest rest roughly 5min.
  3. Plate scalloped potatoes and top with carrot and chive. Slice steak strips, and top with Cilantro Lime Dressing.
  4. Serve and Enjoy!
