Steps to make Ham & Egg English Muffin:
  1. Slice a muffin into two and toast both sides. If you do not want to mess up your toaster with corn grits, toast with a pan!
  2. Add milk & honey in beaten eggs and mix well.
  3. Put butter or mayonnaise in a pan and make scrambled eggs.
  4. Slice cucumber, tomato and ham.
  5. Place scrambled eggs, cucumber and tomato on a muffin slice.
  6. Put mayonnaise, ham and then top it with another slice of a muffin.
  7. To keep muffins dry, place cucumber & tomato between scrambled eggs & ham!
