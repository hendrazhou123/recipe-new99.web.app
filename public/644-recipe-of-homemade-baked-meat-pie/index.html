The ingredients needed to make Baked meat pie:
  1. Get 4 cups all purpose flour
  2. Get Half cup of butter
  3. Take 1 tspn baking powder
  4. Make ready 1 tbspn sugar
  5. Prepare 2 eggs
  6. Make ready pinch salt
  7. Take half cup of milk (or water)
  8. Prepare 1 tspn nutmeg
  9. Take For d filling;
  10. Prepare Minced meat
  11. Get sliced onions
  12. Get scotch bonnets
  13. Prepare cubes, spices and oil

Steps to make Baked meat pie:
  1. Get ur clean bowl, add all ur dry ingredients
  2. Add in ur whisk eggs and milk, make a smooth dough. Cover and let rest for 20 mins
  3. Get a clean frying pan, put oil and sliced onion, fry for 5 mins add in ur minced meat allow to cook for 20 mins
  4. Add in ur spices and cubes, leave it for some mins, bring it down and let’s cool
  5. Bring ur dough roll and add in ur fillings and cut. Pre heat ur oven for 5 mins
  6. Bring ur oven pan, rub butter on it and arrange ur meat pie, allow to baked for 20 to 30 mins. Enjoy
