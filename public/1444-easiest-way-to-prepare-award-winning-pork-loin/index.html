Instructions to make Pork Loin:
  1. Mix together Pork Loin Rub:. 1 tbsp Paprika, 1/2 tbsp Garlic powder, 1/2 tbsp onion powder, 1/2 tbsp salt, 1/4 tbsp cayenne pepper
  2. Remove silver section on pork loin
  3. Coat and rub pork loin with pork loin mixture
  4. Place pork loin on oven rack on baking sheet (or tin foil)
  5. Bake in 425 degrees for 30 minutes or until 145 degrees internal temperature
