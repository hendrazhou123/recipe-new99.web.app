The ingredients needed to make Meatlof:
  1. Get 1 lb ground beef
  2. Take 1 egg
  3. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
  4. Prepare 1 tsp onion powder
  5. Get 20 club crackers
  6. Prepare 1/4 cups ketchup
  7. Make ready Sauce
  8. Take 1/2 cup ketchup
  9. Take 1 tsp garlic
  10. Get 1 heaping tblsp brown sugar

Instructions to make Meatlof:
  1. Mix a beated egg, meat, crackers, ketchup, onion powder, and garlic powder in bowl. I use my hands and just mix it well.
  2. Preheat the oven 350° bake for 45 minutes.
  3. While baking the loaf, mix ketchup, brown sugar, and garlic for sauce.
  4. When 45 minutes is up, add sauce onto meatloaf and bake for an extra 10 minutes. Meat should be <165°
