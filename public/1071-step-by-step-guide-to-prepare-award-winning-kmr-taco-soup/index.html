The ingredients needed to make Kmr taco soup:
  1. Take 1 lb Lean Ground beef
  2. Get 1/2 Onion
  3. Make ready 3 minced garlic cloves
  4. Take 1 Can Kidney Beans
  5. Make ready 1 Can Black Beans
  6. Make ready 1 can Diced Chili's
  7. Prepare 1 Can Diced or Stewed Tomatoes
  8. Get 3 Cups Water or beef broth
  9. Take Seasonings
  10. Get 1 Tbsp Chili Powder
  11. Prepare 1 Tbsp Garlic Powder
  12. Take 1 Tbsp Paprika
  13. Prepare 1 Tsp Cumin
  14. Take 1 Tsp Sea Salt
  15. Take 1/4 Tsp Pepper

Steps to make Kmr taco soup:
  1. Cook ground beef & onions for 7mins
  2. Add minced garlic continue cooking for 30 seconds
  3. Add seasonings mix for 30 more seconds
  4. Add kidney & black beans, Diced tomatoes & Chili's mix well
  5. Then add water or beef broth mix & simmer for 25 to 30 mins your done
  6. Garnish with cheese, avocado, sour cream & serve with tortilla Chips or whatever you desire…
