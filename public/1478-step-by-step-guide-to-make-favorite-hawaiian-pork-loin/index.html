The ingredients needed to make Hawaiian pork loin:
  1. Take 9 pork loin chops
  2. Make ready 1 large tbspn minced garlic
  3. Make ready 1 tspn allspice
  4. Take 1 tspn thyme
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  6. Take 1 tspn ginger
  7. Get 2 tbsp soy sauce
  8. Get 1/2 cup jasmine rice
  9. Get 2 tbsp coconut oil
  10. Get 2 tbsp sesame oil

Pork loin is a cut of meat from a pig, created from the tissue along the dorsal side of the rib cage. Pork loin may be cut into individual servings, as chops (bone in) or steaks (boneless) which are grilled, baked or fried. A pork loin joint or pork loin roast is a larger section of the loin which is roasted.

Instructions to make Hawaiian pork loin:
  1. Brown the pork chops in coconut oil. Set aside
  2. Brown allspice, ginger, garlic, thyme, in coconut oil
  3. Stir in pork, soy sauce, vinegar, & sesame oil
  4. Cook for 24 minutes on high.
  5. Set aside in bowl, cook white long grain rice for 4 minutes with 1/2 cool is water.
  6. Serve pork over rice & enjoy
