The ingredients needed to make Oven baked potatoes:
  1. Take 33 baby potatoes, cut a little bit on both ends
  2. Get 2 tsp salt
  3. Prepare 2 tsp white vinegar
  4. Take 2.5 tsp salt
  5. Get 2 tsp black pepper
  6. Get 1 tsp white pepper
  7. Take 1 tsp red pepper
  8. Make ready 1 tsp turmeric
  9. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
  10. Take 1 tsp onion powder
  11. Prepare to taste Olive oil

Steps to make Oven baked potatoes:
  1. Preheat the oven on medium heat.
  2. Fill 1 medium pot of water with the vinegar and salt. Let it boil then add the potatoes.
  3. When potatoes are half cooked, remove from pot, place them in a baking tray. Mash them with a spoon a little bit.
  4. Mix the spices in a bowl and sprinkle them on the potatoes and then add olive oil on top to taste.
  5. Place tray in the oven and let it bake until potatoes turn golden crispy.
