The ingredients needed to make "Slap Ya Mama" Potato Soup:
  1. Take 7 Whole Russet Potatoes
  2. Take 2 c Whole Milk
  3. Make ready 1 "U shaped" cooked smoked sausage
  4. Prepare 1 bunch Celery
  5. Get 1 can Cheddar Cheese Soup
  6. Get 1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
  7. Take 2 c water
  8. Prepare to taste "Slap Ya Mama" seasoning

Steps to make "Slap Ya Mama" Potato Soup:
  1. Rinse potatoes and dice into cubes
  2. Place in large pot until water runs clear
  3. Fill with water until water covers top of potatoes by two inches
  4. Boil on high
  5. Dice Sausage and slice Celary
  6. Stir in Cheddar Cheese Soup and Cream of Mushroom Soup
  7. Add Smoked Sausage(diced) and sliced rinsed celary
  8. Add milk and "Slap Ya Mama" (enough to cover the top) return to a boil, cover and simmer for 45 minutes
  9. Enjoy! (Please do not actually slap ya mama, this is a seasoning not a suggestion) ðŸ˜‚
