The ingredients needed to make Fried Egg Donburi:
  1. Get 4 cups steamed rice
  2. Make ready 4 eggs
  3. Take 3 tbsp oil
  4. Take 200 ml water
  5. Prepare 1 tsp dashi powder
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp shoyu (soy sauce)
  7. Make ready 1 tsp honey
  8. Prepare To taste Leek, sliced thinly

Instructions to make Fried Egg Donburi:
  1. Fry eggs one by one. When it's half-cooked, fold the egg carefully, and then put it on a plate. Do the same with the remaining eggs.
  2. Pour the water into the pan (just mix it with the leftover oil). Add dashi, shoyu, honey, and let it boil. Taste and add shoyu or honey according to your preference.
  3. Put the eggs one by one to the broth mixture for several seconds, and take them away.
  4. Prepare steamed rice into a bowl. Put an egg on top. Pour the broth and add sliced leeks.
