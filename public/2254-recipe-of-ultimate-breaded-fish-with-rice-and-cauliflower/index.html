The ingredients needed to make Breaded fish with rice and cauliflower:
  1. Prepare For the fish:
  2. Take 12 large fresh fish fillets
  3. Take 1/2 cup white flour
  4. Get 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  5. Take 2 eggs (beaten extremely well!)
  6. Prepare 1 cup dry white jasmine rice
  7. Make ready 1 1/2 cup boiling water
  8. Take For the cauliflower:
  9. Get 1 large head cauliflower
  10. Get 3 cup boiling water
  11. Make ready 1/2 cup cheese (sharp cheddar)

Instructions to make Breaded fish with rice and cauliflower:
  1. First, place the breadcrumbs and flour on two separate paper plates
  2. Place beaten eggs in a bowl
  3. Place the fish fillets in the flour
  4. Then the eggs
  5. Then the breadcrumbs
  6. Fry the fish with olive oil until cooked
  7. For the rice:
  8. Add rice to boiling water and let cook until it has soaked up all the water
  9. For the cauliflower:
  10. Add cauliflower to boiling water
  11. Once cauliflower is soft, top with cheese
  12. Let cheese sit until melted
  13. Enjoy!!
