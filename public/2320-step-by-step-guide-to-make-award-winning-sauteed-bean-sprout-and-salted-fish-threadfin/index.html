The ingredients needed to make Sautéed bean sprout and salted fish (threadfin):
  1. Prepare 150 gr bean sprouts
  2. Make ready 20 gr salted fish (thinly chopped)
  3. Make ready 1/2 size tomato
  4. Make ready 1 red onion
  5. Get 3 cloves garlic
  6. Prepare 2 bird eye chillies
  7. Take 3 red chillies
  8. Get 1 tsp light soy sauce
  9. Take 1/2 tsp sugar

Instructions to make Sautéed bean sprout and salted fish (threadfin):
  1. Rinse and clean the bean sprout with water, drain and put aside
  2. Heat some oil in a pan, stir fry the thinly chopped salted fish for about 4 mins
  3. Add in the chopped onions, garlics and chillies followed by chopped tomato. Stir fry for about 3 mins over medium heat
  4. Slightly turn down the heat, add in the bean sprout, season with 1 teaspoon of light soy sauce and a pinch of sugar. Stir fry occasionally for about 3 mins then your dish is ready to serve
  5. Happy trying ^•^
