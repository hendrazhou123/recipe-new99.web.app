The ingredients needed to make Cheese omelette:
  1. Prepare 1 Egg
  2. Get 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  3. Get to taste Salt
  4. Get 2 tbp onion chopped
  5. Make ready 1 small green chilli chopped
  6. Get 1 tsp coriander chopped
  7. Make ready 3 tbp grated cheese

Instructions to make Cheese omelette:
  1. In bowl break egg add salt and chilli powder and beat.
  2. Add all the vegges and mix again.
  3. In a heating pan add egg and cook on low flame.
  4. Add cheese on one side and fold egg into half.
  5. Cook until cheese starts to melt.
  6. Flip other side and cook for 2-3 mins.
  7. Serve with yummy crispy buttered toast.
