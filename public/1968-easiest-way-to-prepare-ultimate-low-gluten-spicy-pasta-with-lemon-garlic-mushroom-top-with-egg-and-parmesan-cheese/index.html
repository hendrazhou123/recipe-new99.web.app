The ingredients needed to make Low gluten spicy pasta with Lemon garlic, mushroom top with egg and Parmesan cheese 😉🥒🍋🧄🥚🌶:
  1. Make ready 3 cups pene gluten pasta
  2. Prepare Onion leaks
  3. Make ready Dry parsley
  4. Get 4 egg
  5. Make ready 5 cloves garlic
  6. Get 2 medium lemon
  7. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp chili pepper flakes
  9. Prepare Butter
  10. Make ready Olive oil
  11. Get to taste Salt and pepper
  12. Make ready Grated Parmesan cheese
  13. Get 1 cup cooking cream

Steps to make Low gluten spicy pasta with Lemon garlic, mushroom top with egg and Parmesan cheese 😉🥒🍋🧄🥚🌶:
  1. These are the ingredients
  2. Boil the pasta in a large pan with olive oil and salt.
  3. Sauté garlic and chili pepper flakes for about 1 min
  4. Add mushroom and sauté for 2 mins and then add the cooking cream and lemon, add salt and pepper to taste boil for 2 mins.
  5. Drain the pasta and transfer again in pan then put all the mix sauce in the pasta
  6. Put some Parmesan and garnish with dry parsley and egg…
