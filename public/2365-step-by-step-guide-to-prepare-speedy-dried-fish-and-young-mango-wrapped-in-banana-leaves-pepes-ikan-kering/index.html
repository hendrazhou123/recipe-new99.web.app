The ingredients needed to make Dried Fish and Young Mango Wrapped in Banana Leaves (PEPES IKAN KERING):
  1. Make ready 2 pcs (350 gr) dried fish
  2. Get 1 pc (150 gr) young mango, cut lengthwise
  3. Get 50 gr corn
  4. Get 1 red chili, sliced diagonally
  5. Prepare Handful basil
  6. Take to taste Sugar and salt
  7. Get Banana leaves to wrap
  8. Get Sticks (toothpicks) to lock the wrap
  9. Take GROUND SPICES
  10. Make ready 6 shallots
  11. Get 2 cloves garlic
  12. Prepare 6 cayenne peppers
  13. Take 4 pcs candlenut
  14. Take 1 pinch roasted shrimp paste

Instructions to make Dried Fish and Young Mango Wrapped in Banana Leaves (PEPES IKAN KERING):
  1. Mix the pieces of young mango with corn and spices, stirring until blended.
  2. Take a banana leaf and lay the young mango mix on top. Add the dried fish, red pepper and basil.
  3. Wrap in an elongated form, then pin the ends with toothpicks. Steamed until cooked.
  4. Pepes is ready to be served with rice.
