The ingredients needed to make Bottle Gourd Rice:
  1. Make ready 1/2 Bottle Gourd chopped
  2. Take 1 1/2 cup sona masoori rice
  3. Take 2 3/4 cup water
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp ghee
  6. Prepare Whole spices (Cinnamon, cloves, stone flower, 1 tsp fennel seeds)
  7. Make ready 1 1/2 onion thinly sliced
  8. Take 1 red chilli
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  10. Get 5-6 stem coriander leaves
  11. Get 3-4 stem mint leaves
  12. Make ready 1 1/2 medium size tomato chopped
  13. Get 3/4 tsp red chilli powder
  14. Get Salt as required
  15. Prepare drops Few lemon

Instructions to make Bottle Gourd Rice:
  1. Heat a pressure cooker with oil and ghee and temper with whole spices.
  2. Add onion and red chilli. sauté until it becomes very soft.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and sauté until raw smell goes.
  4. Add coriander leaves and mint leaves. Sauté for few mins. Add tomato and sauté until it becomes soft. Add red chilli powder and salt.
  5. Add bottle gourd and sauté for few mins. Add water and mix it well. Add rice and finally add few lemon drops. Close the lid and pressure cook for 2 whistles.
  6. Serve with any raita.
