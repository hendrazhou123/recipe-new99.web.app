The ingredients needed to make Easy Garlic Butter Potatoes:
  1. Get 1 yukon gold potato
  2. Take 3-4 cloves garlic
  3. Get 1 tablespoon butter
  4. Get Salt
  5. Make ready Black pepper

Steps to make Easy Garlic Butter Potatoes:
  1. Dice the potato into 1-inch pieces. Peel and finely mince 3-4 cloves of garlic.
  2. Heat a frying pan to medium high heat.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of butter to the pan, and follow with the minced garlic.
  4. Pan fry the garlic until it becomes yellow and fragrant.
  5. Add the diced potatoes and cook until edges are golden brown.
  6. Turn off the heat and season with salt and black pepper to taste.
