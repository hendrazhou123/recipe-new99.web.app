The ingredients needed to make Butternut soup:
  1. Make ready 2 medium Butternut, peeled and cubed
  2. Prepare 3 medium Potatoes, peeled and cubed
  3. Make ready 3 baby Onions, peeled and coarsely chopped
  4. Take 1 Tomato, coarsely chopped
  5. Prepare 1000 ml chicken Stock
  6. Prepare 125 ml Orley Whip
  7. Take 10 ml Salt
  8. Get 5 ml freshly ground black Pepper
  9. Take 3 ml ground Cumin
  10. Get 3 ml ground Coriander
  11. Make ready 3 ml Masala
  12. Get Garnish
  13. Prepare fresh Coriander Leaves (optional)
  14. Make ready dices smoked Bacon (optional)
  15. Prepare diced Biltong (optional)

Steps to make Butternut soup:
  1. Add Butternut, Potatoes, Onions, Tomato and Stock to Soup Casserole.
  2. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer covered till the vegetables are cooked through and soft.
  3. Pulp vegetables and stock till smooth. Add Orley Whip and all spices. Return to stove and heat through.
  4. Serve with you choice of garnish.
