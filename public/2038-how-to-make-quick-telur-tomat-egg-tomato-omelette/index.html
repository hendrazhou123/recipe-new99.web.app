The ingredients needed to make Telur Tomat (Egg Tomato Omelette):
  1. Take 4 eggs
  2. Take 1 large tomato, seeded, small diced
  3. Prepare 1 stalk green onion, thinly sliced
  4. Get 1 red chilli pepper, seeded, thinly sliced
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt or to taste
  6. Get 1/8 tsp white pepper powder or to taste
  7. Make ready 1/2 Tbsp vegetable oil
  8. Take Ground spices:
  9. Make ready 3 cloves shallots
  10. Prepare 2 cloves garlic

Instructions to make Telur Tomat (Egg Tomato Omelette):
  1. Beat the eggs. Add ground spices, tomato, and green onions.
  2. Season with salt and white pepper powder. Mix well.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan. Make an omelette. Remove from the heat.
