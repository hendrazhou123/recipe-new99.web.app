The ingredients needed to make Egg Misua Noodles Soup:
  1. Make ready 150 gr misua noodles
  2. Prepare 150 gr boneless chicken meat, diced, boiled, set aside the chicken broth, strained the broth
  3. Prepare 2 eggs, beaten
  4. Take 100 gr baby bok choy, halved, washed
  5. Make ready 1 carrot, julienned
  6. Get 3 cloves garlic, minced
  7. Make ready 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  8. Make ready 1/2 Tbsp salt or to taste
  9. Get 1/4 tsp white pepper powder or to taste
  10. Get 1/2 tsp mushroom bouillon
  11. Get 1 lt chicken broth
  12. Take 2 stalks green onions, diagonally cut
  13. Take 1 tsp sesame oil
