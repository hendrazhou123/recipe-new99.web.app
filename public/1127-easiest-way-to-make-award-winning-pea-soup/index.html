The ingredients needed to make Pea soup:
  1. Take 3 cups chicken stock
  2. Prepare 2 cups cooked or canned peas
  3. Make ready 1 cup cold water
  4. Take 1/2 onion
  5. Make ready Sprig parsley
  6. Prepare 1 cup 1/2 and 1/2
  7. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
  8. Make ready 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  9. Prepare 2 tablespoons butter
  10. Get Celery salt

Instructions to make Pea soup:
  1. Put aside one third of peas
  2. Add remainder to water stock and seasoning and let simmer 30 minutes
  3. Press through a sieve
  4. Add butter
  5. Let boil few minutes then add 1/2 and 1/2 and remaining peas which have been heated double boiler
  6. Serve very hot with little whipped cream and paprika
