Steps to make Sweet Potato Latte with Walnuts:
  1. Boil, bake, or air-fry a sweet potato until tender. I boiled a Japanese sweet potato for about 20mins.
  2. Peel & chop into chunks. Put them into a blender. Fill with milk to cover the potatoes.
  3. Add in walnuts, honey, 1-2 drops vanilla extract, and blend.
  4. Strain mixture into a cup - add more or less milk to adjust to your desired consistency.
  5. Sprinkle with cinnamon and Enjoy!! Warm or cold :)
