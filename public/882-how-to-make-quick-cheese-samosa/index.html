The ingredients needed to make Cheese Samosa:
  1. Get 1 kg potato
  2. Get 500 gms purpose flour
  3. Make ready 250 grams cheese
  4. Take 50 grams peas
  5. Get 2 tablespoons dry mango powder
  6. Prepare 2 tablespoons samosa masala
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon whole coriander
  8. Make ready 1 spoonful celery
  9. Take 1 pinch asafoetida
  10. Take 1 whole cumin
  11. Take to taste Salt
  12. Take as required Ghee for frying

Steps to make Cheese Samosa:
  1. Take refined flour in a big pot, add one teaspoon salt to celery, add ghee to the mixture and keep the dough.
  2. Boil the potatoes and mash them
  3. Add ghee in the pan, add cumin and whole coriander, then add peas and spices, potatoes and stir
  4. Add salt and mix all these ingredients well and remove in the plate
  5. Make dough balls and shape the samosas, then add potato paste and cottage cheese inside it
  6. When the samosa is ready, deep fry and take it out
  7. Now serve hot with chutney
