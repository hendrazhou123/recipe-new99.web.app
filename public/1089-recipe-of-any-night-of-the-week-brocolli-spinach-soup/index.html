The ingredients needed to make Brocolli Spinach Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 Medium Size leek
  2. Take 1 Spring Onion
  3. Get 2 Cloves Garlic and Ginger
  4. Get 1 Large Potato
  5. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon Parsley
  6. Get 1/2 teaspoon Oregano
  7. Take Black Pepper and Salt
  8. Get Maggi cube seasoning
  9. Take Medium size Brocolli
  10. Get 1 Cup spinach
  11. Take 1 Small Carrot
  12. Prepare 2 Tablespoon Butter
