The ingredients needed to make Taiwanese minced pork rice bowl:
  1. Get 1 lb gound pork
  2. Take 4 garlic cloves
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon dry shallots
  4. Take 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  5. Make ready 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  6. Take 1 tablespoon brown sugar (optional)
  7. Prepare 1/4 cups rice wine (mijiu)
  8. Take 1 teaspoon white pepper powder
  9. Take 1 teaspoon five spice powder
  10. Take 3/4 cups hot water

Depends on personal tastes and regions, there are a few varieties. Some use pork belly instead of minced pork; some add shiitake mushroom; some use eggs; some serve with noodles. But the main ingredients are always pork, shallot, and soy. The BEST savoury, rich meat sauce, braised to perfection.

Steps to make Taiwanese minced pork rice bowl:
  1. Pan-fry ground pork until the fat is rendered. This takes about 10 minutes. Now the temperature is high enough for the next step.
  2. Pan-fry sliced garlic and dry shallot with the meat and the pork fat. You should be able to smell the garlic in 1-2 minutes.
  3. Stir while seasoning: add oyster sauce, soy sauce, and brown sugar (optional).
  4. Add rice wine and wait until boiled. Add white pepper and five spice, mix well.
  5. Finally, add 3/4 cups of hot water, turn down the heat and stew for 30 minutes.
