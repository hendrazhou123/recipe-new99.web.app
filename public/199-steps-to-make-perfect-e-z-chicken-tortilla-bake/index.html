The ingredients needed to make E-Z Chicken Tortilla Bake:
  1. Get 2 cans (10 3/4 ounces) Condensed Tomato Soup
  2. Prepare 2 cups Chunky Salsa or Original Picante Sauce Medium
  3. Take 1 cup milk
  4. Get 22 oz bag of fully cooked, diced, oven roasted chicken breast
  5. Get 16 corn tortilla(6-inch), cut into 1-inch pieces
  6. Prepare 2 cups shredded fiesta/Mexican blend cheese (or more if you want:) )
  7. Make ready 1 can sweet whole kernel corn (optional)

Steps to make E-Z Chicken Tortilla Bake:
  1. Heat the oven to 400°F. Stir the soup, salsa, corn, milk, chicken, tortillas and half the cheese in a large mixing bowl & then pour mixture into 5 quart (11 by 13) shallow baking dish.
  2. Cover the baking dish. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until the chicken mixture is hot and bubbling. Top with the remaining cheese. Let stand until the cheese is melted.
  3. Enjoy:-) This feeds a big crowd.
