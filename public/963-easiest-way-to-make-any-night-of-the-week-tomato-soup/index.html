The ingredients needed to make Tomato soup:
  1. Take 2 tomato
  2. Take 2 tbsp starch
  3. Prepare 1 egg
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp white pepper
  6. Get 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  7. Prepare Some coriander

Steps to make Tomato soup:
  1. Cut tomato into small pieces
  2. Pour tomato into boiling water
  3. After boiling again, turn to low heat and simmer for 3 minutes
  4. Mix starch with a little bit water (don’t add all starch in one-go, leave a little bit to see if required to add all)
  5. When bubbles start forming, pour egg
  6. Add salt, pepper, sesame (add more to adjust to taste)
  7. Add coriander
