The ingredients needed to make Baked Fish With Awesome Sauce:
  1. Make ready 2 sleeves of Wheat ritz crackers
  2. Prepare 1 stick Butter
  3. Get 2 tbsp Sherry wine
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp White wine
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp Paprika
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp White pepper
  7. Take 2 lb Your favorite fish
  8. Take 1 medium yellow onion

Instructions to make Baked Fish With Awesome Sauce:
  1. Melt the butter in a fry pan and cook onion in it until they are clear.
  2. Add all ingredients to a large mixing bowl
  3. Let the butter and onion soak the crackers for a minute
  4. Mix on low until blended well
  5. Spread over fish in baking dish
  6. Bake on 375 for 40 minutes
