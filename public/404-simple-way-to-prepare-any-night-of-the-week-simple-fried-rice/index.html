The ingredients needed to make Simple Fried Rice:
  1. Make ready 300 gr rice
  2. Make ready 1 egg
  3. Get 3 tbsp Vegetables oil
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp garlic oil or sesame oil
  5. Get 1 tsp seasoning
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp tomatoes sauce
  7. Take 1 tbsp sweet sauce
  8. Take 1 tbsp rice wine
  9. Get to taste Salt
  10. Prepare Spices
  11. Take 3 cloves garlic
  12. Take 5 shallots
  13. Take 2 stalks spring onion
  14. Get 1 tsp dried shrimps (ebi)
  15. Make ready 2 small chilies
