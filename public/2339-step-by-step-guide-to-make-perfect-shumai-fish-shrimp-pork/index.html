The ingredients needed to make Shumai (fish shrimp pork):
  1. Take 250 gr fish meat (tenggiri)
  2. Get 250 gr shrimp (skinless)
  3. Prepare 50 gr pork minced
  4. Take 1 pc egg
  5. Take 3 garlic (mince and fry)
  6. Get 2 tbs tapioca flour
  7. Get Paper
  8. Get Salt soysouce
  9. Take Oyster souce
  10. Prepare Olive oil
  11. Get Sesame oil
  12. Get Dumpling skin
  13. Prepare touch Carrot for final
