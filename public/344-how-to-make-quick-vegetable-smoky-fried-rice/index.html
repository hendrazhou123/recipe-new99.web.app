The ingredients needed to make Vegetable smoky Fried Rice😊:
  1. Get 2 cups cooked rice/left over rice
  2. Make ready 1 cup mixed vegetables (any veg you have in fridge)
  3. Take 3 cloves garlic
  4. Prepare 3 green chillies,
  5. Take 1 Maggi cube (any1)
  6. Get 1 tbsp soy sauce
  7. Take 1 tbsp ketchup
  8. Get 1 tbsp chilli sauce
  9. Take 1 tbsp vinegar
  10. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  11. Take 2 eggs, beaten and cooked
  12. Prepare 2 tbsp oil
  13. Get 1 piece coal
