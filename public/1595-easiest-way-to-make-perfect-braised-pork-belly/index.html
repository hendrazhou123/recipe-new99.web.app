The ingredients needed to make 卤三层肉 Braised Pork Belly:
  1. Prepare 三层肉 Pork belly (300g)
  2. Make ready 马铃薯 Potato (1-2)
  3. Prepare 红萝卜 Carrot (1)
  4. Prepare 蒜头 Garlic (4 cloves)
  5. Prepare 酱清 Light soy sauce
  6. Make ready 酱油 Dark soy sauce
  7. Get 胡椒粉 Pepper powder

Steps to make 卤三层肉 Braised Pork Belly:
  1. 把三层肉，马铃薯和红萝卜切小块。放入压力锅 Cut pork belly, potato and carrot into small pieces. Put into pressure cooker.
  2. 加入蒜头 Add garlic
  3. 加入一汤匙酱清，一汤匙酱油，少许胡椒粉。煮15分钟。 Add 1 tablespoon of light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, some pepper powder. Cook for 15mins.
