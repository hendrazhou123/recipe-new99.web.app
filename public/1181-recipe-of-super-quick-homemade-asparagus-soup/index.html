The ingredients needed to make Asparagus soup:
  1. Prepare 4 cups chicken broth remove all grease
  2. Get 2 cups rich sweet milk scald
  3. Take to taste Salt
  4. Prepare 1 cup green asparagus tips cut in very small pieces
  5. Take Celery salt
  6. Get 1 tablespoon flour stirred into little milk
  7. Get 1 tablespoon butter
  8. Prepare 1 tablespoon celery hearts
  9. Take Add little whipped cream when serving paprika

Steps to make Asparagus soup:
  1. Cook asparagus till tender remove tips from water
  2. Cook celery in asparagus water
  3. Add well blended flour and butter to hot broth that has been mixed with the milk
  4. Add cook asparagus tips serve very hot with paprika
  5. Top with whipped cream.
