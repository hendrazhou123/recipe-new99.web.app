The ingredients needed to make Beef Soup with Veggies and Fish Balls:
  1. Get 500 gr beef chunks/chicken
  2. Prepare 250 gr cauliflower
  3. Prepare 200 gr broccoli
  4. Make ready 150 gr fish balls
  5. Make ready 120 gr chopped string beans
  6. Take 80 gr chopped carrots
  7. Prepare 250 gr chopped potatoes
  8. Make ready 2 chopped tomatoes into big sizes
  9. Take 50 gr elbow macaroni
  10. Prepare 1/2 can whole Kernel sweet corn
  11. Make ready 2 tsp salt
  12. Get 1/2 tsp white pepper powder
  13. Make ready 1500 ml broth
  14. Take 5 cloves crush garlic
  15. Prepare 2 tsp corn starch mix with some water
  16. Take 1 tsp sasame oil
  17. Make ready 2 pinch fried onions (can buy at Asian store)

Steps to make Beef Soup with Veggies and Fish Balls:
  1. Boil beef chunks into the water. Drain first water, continue add some water. Cook in the rice cooker until tender. Keep rest of broth.
  2. Boil rest of broth, add some more water. Add macaroni, garlic, beef, celery, and carrot. After few minutes add tomatoes, and fish balsl.
  3. Add potatoes into the soup, continue with another ingredients. Last should be broccoli. No need to cook for long time, so it's still crunchy.
  4. 2 minute before turning off the stove, pour liquid corn starch, and garnish with fried onion and sesame oil.
