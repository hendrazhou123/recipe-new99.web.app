The ingredients needed to make Cheese Pasta:

  2. Get 3 cup Water (to boil Pasta)
  3. Get 1 Capsicum(sliced)
  4. Prepare 1 Onion(sliced)
  5. Take 2 Cheese slice
  6. Prepare 1 spoon Whole grain flour
  7. Get 1/2 cup Milk
  8. Take 1 1/2 spoon Oil
  9. Get to taste Salt
  10. Make ready 1/4 spoon black pepper powder
  11. Prepare 1/4 spoon Garam masala

Steps to make Cheese Pasta:
  1. Put paasta and water in a deep pan and boil it on low flame for 15 mins.
  2. Strain the paasta the
  3. Heat 1 spoon oil in a pan. Add sliced onion and capsicum. And cook for 3 mins on medium flame.
  4. Heat 1/2 spoon oil in another pan. Add whole grain flour, and stir and add milk. Cook for 2-3 min on low flame.(White sauce is ready now)
  5. Then add salt, black pepper & garam masala and cook for 1 min. Then add cheese and let it be melt and mix properly.
  6. Then add fried onion & capsicum.& Mix well
  7. Add strained paasta,and mix well.
  8. Cheese paasta is ready to eat
