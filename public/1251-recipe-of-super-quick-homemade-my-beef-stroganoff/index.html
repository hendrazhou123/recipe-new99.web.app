The ingredients needed to make My Beef Stroganoff:
  1. Make ready 1 lb lean beef stew meat
  2. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp garlic
  4. Prepare 1 medium white onion chopped
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  6. Take 1/2 cup red cooking wine
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp dried parsley
  8. Get 1 1/2 cups sour cream
  9. Prepare 1-2 cups egg noodles
  10. Prepare to taste Salt
  11. Take 2 tbsp flour
  12. Take 1/2 cup beef stock
  13. Make ready 1 tub mushrooms

Instructions to make My Beef Stroganoff:
  1. In a medium pot boil noodles.
  2. In a medium pan add olive oil, meat, onions and garlic. Cook till done.
  3. Now add beef stock, worsteshire sauce, red wine and mushrooms. Bring to a boil
  4. Add sour cream and sprinkle flour on top and whisk making sure no lumps.
  5. Add egg noddles. Let simmer for 5 minutes. Add pepper to taste. Top with parsley and green onions.
