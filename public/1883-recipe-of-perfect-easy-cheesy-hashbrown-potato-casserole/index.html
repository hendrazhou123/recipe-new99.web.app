The ingredients needed to make Easy Cheesy Hashbrown Potato Casserole:
  1. Take 1 bag frozen potatoes o'Brien
  2. Get 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  3. Take 1 can cream of chicken soup
  4. Prepare 1 cup milk
  5. Get 1 cup sour cream
  6. Make ready 1 bag shredded cheddar cheese
  7. Make ready Salt
  8. Take Pepper
  9. Get Garlic powder
  10. Take Onion powder
  11. Make ready Corn flakes

Instructions to make Easy Cheesy Hashbrown Potato Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 450°. Place frozen potatoes o'Brien in casserole baking dish. Pour shredded cheese on top.
  2. Add mushroom and chicken soup mix to pan. As well as milk, sour cream, and seasonings to taste. Mix well.
  3. Bake in the oven, covered for 40 mins. Then remove from heat, uncover and crush cornflakes on top of the casserole. Return to heat, uncovered, for 15-20 mins or until potatoes are tender and corn flakes and crispy.
  4. Serve & Enjoy Cheesy Heaven!!!
