The ingredients needed to make Spicy Potatoes with Chicken Liver (Sambal Goreng Kentang Ati):
  1. Take 2/3 kg potatoes
  2. Prepare 1/3 kg chicken liver
  3. Get 200 gr long red chilies
  4. Make ready 1 red onion
  5. Get 6 garlic cloves
  6. Make ready 2 lemongrass
  7. Take 1 cup coconut milk
  8. Make ready 2 kaffir lime leaves
  9. Prepare 2 bayleaves
  10. Get 1 tsp salt
  11. Get 1/2 tsp sugar
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp cooking oil
  13. Make ready Cooking oil for deep frying

Instructions to make Spicy Potatoes with Chicken Liver (Sambal Goreng Kentang Ati):
  1. Dice potatoes, grind red onion, garlics, long red chilies.
  2. Deep fry diced potatoes and chicken liver.
  3. Cut chicken liver into small pieces.
  4. Pre-heat saute pan, put cooking oil in, add all ground inggredients, stir until its cooked, add lemongrass, salt, sugar, kaffir lime leaves, bayleaves, mix well.
  5. Add potatoes and chicken liver, mix well.
  6. Add coconut milk, stir properly.
  7. Cook until the coconut milk reduces.
  8. Enjoy with rice or bread.
