The ingredients needed to make Quick fried fish tacos:
  1. Make ready Frozen fish sticks or frozen fried fish filets
  2. Make ready Green tomatillo salsa
  3. Get 3 cups cabbage sliced into ribbons
  4. Prepare 1 cup carrots julliene cut
  5. Prepare 1 handful cilantro
  6. Prepare 1 tbs chipotle pepper in adobo
  7. Prepare 1.5 cups mayo
  8. Prepare Zest and juice of 1 lime
  9. Get Fresh tortillas
  10. Get Tartar sauce

Instructions to make Quick fried fish tacos:
  1. Cook fish in oven as directed
  2. As fish cooks, blend mayo, Chipotle, lime zest and juice, and cilantro. *adjust amounts to your taste
  3. Toss cabbage and carrots in mayo sauce, set aside
  4. Spread a little tartar on tortillas
  5. Top with fish sliced into bite size pieces
  6. Top with chipotle lime slaw
  7. Drizzle with salsa Verde and garnish with fresh cilantro
