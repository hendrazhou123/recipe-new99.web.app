The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Beef stew:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2-2 lbs stew meat
  2. Take 4-5 carrots
  3. Get 4-5 celery stalks
  4. Make ready 1 yellow onion
  5. Make ready 1 bag small potatoes
  6. Prepare Worcestershire
  7. Prepare 1/4 c. ketchup
  8. Make ready 4 cups water
  9. Take 2 teaspoons better than bouillon beef flavor
  10. Make ready 1 package McCormick beef stew mix
  11. Get Flour dredge
  12. Get 2 cups flour
  13. Make ready Garlic salt
  14. Prepare Pepper

Steps to make Crockpot Beef stew:
  1. Add diced onions, celery,carrots and potatoes to crockpot
  3. Put flour and seasoning in gallon zip too bag. Add beef and shake to coat. Brown in oil in skillet. Add to vegetables
  4. Add water to skillet to deglaze. Add remaining ingredients. (Bouillon,ketchup, Worcestershire,and stew packet) pour over meat and vegetables and mix.
  5. Cook on low 7 hours
  6. Enjoy with some cornbread
