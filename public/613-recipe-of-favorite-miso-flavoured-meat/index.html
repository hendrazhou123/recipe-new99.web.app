The ingredients needed to make Miso Flavoured Meat:
  1. Prepare 300 g Minced meat (pork, chicken, or beef)
  2. Take 4-5 dried Shiitake mushrooms
  3. Get 1/2 green onion
  4. Prepare 1 clove ginger, chopped
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp sesame oil
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp Miso : (A)
  7. Take 2 tsp soy sauce : (A)
  8. Make ready 1-2 pinches salt : (A)

Steps to make Miso Flavoured Meat:
  1. Reconstitute Shiitake mushrooms.
  2. Mix the condiments (A) together.
  3. Chop the Shiitake mushrooms, the ginger, and the green onion.
  4. Heat the sesame oil and the ginger in a frying pan and stir-fry the following in order: the minced meat, the green onion, and the Shiitake mushrooms.
  5. Add the condiments (A) and lightly stir-fry.
  6. Store in a clean container and put in a fridge or a freezer.
