The ingredients needed to make Beef & Broccoli (Bulking):
  1. Take 1 large pack ground beef ~2.5lbs
  2. Take 2 broccoli crowns
  3. Make ready 1 white onion
  4. Make ready Sauce
  5. Prepare 115 ml corn starch
  6. Take 1.25 Cups soy sauce
  7. Get 115 ml brown sugar
  8. Get 2.5 Tablespoons minced garlic
  9. Get 1.5 Tablespoons minced ginger
  10. Prepare Rice
  11. Make ready 2 Cups basmati rice
  12. Make ready 3 Cups water

Instructions to make Beef & Broccoli (Bulking):
  1. Whisk together sauce mixture
  2. Brown ground beef and set aside

  4. Add sauce mixture and bring to a boil for about a minute
