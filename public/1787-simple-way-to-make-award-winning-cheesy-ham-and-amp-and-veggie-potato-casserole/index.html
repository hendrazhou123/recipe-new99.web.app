The ingredients needed to make Cheesy ham & and veggie potato casserole:
  1. Prepare 1 box scalloped potatoes+ingredients called for on the box
  2. Take 2 cups cubed ham
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup milk or cream
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup diced mushroom and purple onion
  6. Take 1 cup peas
  7. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon pepper, soul seasoning and Mrs dash original
  8. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  9. Get Top with Parmesan cheese

Instructions to make Cheesy ham & and veggie potato casserole:
  1. Heat oven to 450. Mix ingredients called for on package of potatoes and potatoes together in a lightly greased 13x9 glass baking dish.
  2. Dice onion and mushroom and mix in plus, spices, milk, shredded cheese, ham and peas. Top with Parmesan cheese and bake uncovered 25 minutes.

Stir ham and cheese sauce into the pasta, mix well. Transfer to baking dish and spread evenly. This impressive-looking casserole bakes up puffy and golden with pockets of fresh corn and savory ham.
