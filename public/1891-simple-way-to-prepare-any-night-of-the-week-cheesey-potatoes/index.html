The ingredients needed to make Cheesey potatoes:
  1. Take 32 oz bag cubed potatoes
  2. Prepare 1/2-1 onion, diced small
  3. Prepare 8 oz sour cream
  4. Make ready 1 can cream of chicken soup
  5. Take 2 sticks butter melted
  6. Make ready 1/2 of 32oz box velveeta cheese, cubed
  7. Make ready 2-3 C corn flakes, crushed
