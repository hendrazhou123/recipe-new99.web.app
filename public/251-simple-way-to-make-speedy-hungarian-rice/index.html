The ingredients needed to make Hungarian Rice:
  1. Prepare 2 Tbs butter
  2. Take 2 Tbs canola oil
  3. Get 2 1/2 cups Uncle Ben's original rice
  4. Prepare 5 1/2 cups chicken broth
  5. Prepare 1 bay leaf
  6. Make ready 1 large diced onion
  7. Prepare 1 large diced green pepper
  8. Get salt optional

Instructions to make Hungarian Rice:
  1. In a 6-quart pot, heat butter and oil. Add in rice and brown it. Add in onion and peppers. Cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Add broth, bay leaf and salt (optional) cover and bring to a boil. Once it starts to boil, turn heat down to low and cover pot. Cook for 20 minutes.
  3. After the 20 minutes stir the rice, recover and let stand for 10 minutes.
