The ingredients needed to make Sweet Soy Sauce Minced Pork:
  1. Make ready 500 gr minced pork
  2. Make ready 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  3. Make ready 5 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Take 3 cm ginger, pounded
  5. Get 2 Tbsp oyster sauce
  6. Take 2 Tbsp dark soy sauce
  7. Take 4 Tbsp sweet soy sauce
  8. Make ready 1 tsp salt or to taste
  9. Get 1/4 tsp white pepper powder or to taste
  10. Prepare 250 ml water
