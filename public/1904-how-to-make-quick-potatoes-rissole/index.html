The ingredients needed to make Potatoes Rissole:
  1. Make ready 1 pound small new potatoes
  2. Prepare 1 teaspoon pink Himalayan salt for boiling potatoes
  3. Take As needed water
  4. Make ready 1/2 stick butter divided
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon parsley
  6. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon dill weed
  7. Take 1 teaspoon chives
  8. Take 1/2 teaspoon thyme
  9. Take 1 teaspoon pink Himalayan salt
  10. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Instructions to make Potatoes Rissole:
  1. Peel and wash the potatoes. Put potatoes in a pot, and cover with water add salt for boiling. Boil 8-12 minutes.
  2. Melt the butter in a skillet. Add the herbs and spices, and the potatoes. Fry for 8-10 minutes, turning them often.
