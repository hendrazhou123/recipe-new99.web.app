The ingredients needed to make Outer leaf of Lettuce & Bacon  with Beaten Egg:
  1. Prepare 4-6 sheets Outer leaf of Lettuce
  2. Make ready 2 sheets Bacon
  3. Get 2 pcs Eggs
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
  5. Take as needed Salt
  6. Take as needed Pepper

Steps to make Outer leaf of Lettuce & Bacon  with Beaten Egg:
  1. Wash the outer leaf of lettuce well. Tear to a desired size with your hands. Cut the bacon into 2cm strip.
  2. Put the Lettuce and bacon in a frying pan and stir fry. Add the Salt and Pepper as needed. Add the mayonnaise in the pan and stir fry.
  3. Put and stir in the beaten egg quickly. Done.
