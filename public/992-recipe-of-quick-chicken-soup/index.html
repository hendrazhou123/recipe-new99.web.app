  3. Defrosted chicken. Season with creole seasoning, thyme, basil, salt, pepper, cook until NO pink. (350° about 30 min)
  4. While chicken is cooking add your veggies to crock pot.
  5. Turn crockpot on High. Add 2 cup of water to your crock pot with 2-3 bouillon cubes. Shake in seasoning. A little at a time.

  8. Remove bay leaves before eating.
