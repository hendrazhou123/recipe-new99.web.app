Steps to make Meat macaroni:
  1. First boil water in half pan fill with water add 1 tsp oil and 2 tbsp salt in it
  2. When it boil add macroni in it when completely boil drain the water
  3. In a pan fry left over meat pieces in oil and add all spices in it mix well. And shred the meat.
  4. Add boiled macroni in it mix well cook 10 min on a low flame and mix well
  5. Meaty macaroni ready to serve
