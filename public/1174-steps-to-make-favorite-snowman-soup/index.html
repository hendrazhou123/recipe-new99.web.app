Steps to make Snowman soup:
  1. I printed the snowman soup card online from somewhere, there a few different kinds out there.
  2. You can put this in anything really a nice hot chocolate mug and just put the ingredients in the mug and put Marshmallows in a clear snack Ziploc bag,(check Pinterest for more ideas)
  3. One hot chocolate Packet, 2 Hershey kisses, one Candy cane and 5-7 or however many mini marshmallows in a Ziploc bag… and repeat, place all in a mug or holiday treat bag. (make as many as you need)
  4. TO MAKE-, make hot chocolate according to directions on package. Then add the Hershey kisses, marshmallows and Stir with candy cane.
