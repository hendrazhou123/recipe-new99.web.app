The ingredients needed to make Mac And Cheese:
  1. Prepare 200 gm elbow pasta
  2. Make ready Cheese Wet Mix
  3. Get 1 cup milk
  4. Prepare 3/4 cup cooking cream
  5. Make ready 1 tsp mustard
  6. Prepare 1 egg
  7. Prepare 1 sprinkle salt
  8. Get 1 sprinkle pepper
  9. Get Cheese Sauce
  10. Make ready 50 gm butter
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp starch
  12. Make ready 100 gm red cheddar
  13. Make ready 2 large tsp cooked cheddar cheese

Instructions to make Mac And Cheese:
  1. Mix milk, cream, egg, mustard, salt and pepper well
  2. Boil the pasta
  3. Put butter in cooking pan
  4. Add wet mix slowly and starch then wait 2-3 minutes
  5. Add red cheddar cheese and wait until it melts
  6. Add cooked cheddar cheese
  7. Add pasta and mix slowly
