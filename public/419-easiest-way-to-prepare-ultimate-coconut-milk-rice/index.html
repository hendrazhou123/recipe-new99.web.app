Instructions to make Coconut Milk Rice:
  1. Pour one cup of rice on a cup, high flames. A bit of oil
  2. Add two cups - one of coconut milk, one of water
  3. Add a teaspoon of salt, add 3 kardamom seeds
  4. Bring to boil. No lid.
  5. Once boiling, move to lower flame for 5 minutes. Put lid.
  6. After 5', no flame for around 10'. Serve.
