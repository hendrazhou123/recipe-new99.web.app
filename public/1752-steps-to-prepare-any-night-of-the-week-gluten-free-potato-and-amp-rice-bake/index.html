  4. Prepare 60 g (2.11 oz) Potato starch

Instructions to make Gluten-free! Potato & Rice Bake:
  1. Make mashed potatoes with hot milk. Add rice flour and mix well.
  2. Add potato starch, fine salt and knead well.
  3. Divide into 8 portions and make a round smashed shape just like a small pancake.
  4. Melt the butter in a pan and bake both sides until the surface becomes toasted.
  5. Put the cake on a plate, pour some sauce on it and place some basil leaves on top for a nice & vivid presentation!
