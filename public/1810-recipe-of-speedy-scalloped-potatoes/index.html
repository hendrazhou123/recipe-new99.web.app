The ingredients needed to make Scalloped Potatoes:
  1. Make ready 4 cups thinly sliced potatoes
  2. Prepare 2 Tbs flour
  3. Prepare 1.5 cups scalded milk
  4. Get 2/3 cup minced onions
  5. Get 2 Tbs butter
  6. Make ready Salt
  7. Get Pepper

Instructions to make Scalloped Potatoes:
  1. Peel and thinly slice potatoes layering them in a 9X13 baking dish
  2. Top with remaining ingredients and add salt & pepper to taste
  3. Bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes
