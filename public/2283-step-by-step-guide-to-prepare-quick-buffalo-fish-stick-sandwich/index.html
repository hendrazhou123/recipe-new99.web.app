The ingredients needed to make Buffalo Fish Stick Sandwich:
  1. Get Sandwich—————-
  2. Take 9 slices fish sticks frozen
  3. Take 2 slices bread
  4. Prepare As needed oil to fry in shallow fry
  5. Prepare Tarter sauce——————
  6. Get 1/3 cup mayonnaise with cracked black pepper
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon dill weed
  8. Get 1 tablespoon finely diced onions
  9. Get Buffalo sauce—————
  10. Make ready 2 tablespoons butter melted
  11. Get 1/4 cup your favorite hot sauce I used Crystals

Instructions to make Buffalo Fish Stick Sandwich:
  1. Get the oil hot. Fry the fish sticks till done and crispy. Move to a paper towel to absorb excessive amounts of oil
  2. Mix the butter and hot sauce
  3. Dice the onion and add to the cracked black pepper mayonnaise. Add the dill weed and mix well
  4. Spread the tarter sauce on the bread slices.
  5. Add the Buffalo sauce to the fish sticks.
  6. Put the Buffalo sauce coated fish sticks on the bread
  7. Serve I hope you enjoy!
