The ingredients needed to make Cheese quesadillas:
  1. Prepare 4 cheese cubes
  2. Get 1 green chilli
  3. Make ready 1/2 onion
  4. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  5. Take 10 gm salted butter
  6. Get 2 tortillas (1 quesadilla)

Instructions to make Cheese quesadillas:
  1. Get all your vegetables cut and prepped
  2. Grate your cheese
  3. Melt the butter.
  4. Mix the vegetables, butter, cheese, and pepper.
  5. Get your stove ready with the pan preheating.
  6. Important step : remember to butter the tortillas.
  7. Put the tortillas on the stove until the bottom side is golden brown.
  8. Flip it and add the filling
  9. After that spread the filling
  10. Next add the other tortilla on top.
  11. Flip it again, and wait for the new tortilla to cook.
  12. Remember to plate it well.
  13. Now your dish is done.
