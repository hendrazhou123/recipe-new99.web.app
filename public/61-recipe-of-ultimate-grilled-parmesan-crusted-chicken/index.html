The ingredients needed to make Grilled parmesan crusted chicken:
  1. Get 1 cup chicken breast pieces cut in fillets
  2. Take 5 tbsp bread crumbs
  3. Get 2 tbsp seasoning salt
  4. Take 1 cup broccoli florets
  5. Get 1 cup sweet jalapenos (cut in juliens)
  6. Prepare 2-3 tbsp cheddar cheese
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
  8. Take 1 tbsp pepper

Instructions to make Grilled parmesan crusted chicken:
  1. Mix the chicken with seasoning salt and bread crumbs and oil.
  2. In another bowl mix all the veggies with salt and pepper.
  3. Set the baking tray lining the chicken 1st then pour all the veggies on top.
  4. Bake at 350 deg celsius for 10mins then bring out and spread the cheese over and bake again for 5 min.
