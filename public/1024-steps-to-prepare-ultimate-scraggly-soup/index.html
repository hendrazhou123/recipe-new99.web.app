The ingredients needed to make Scraggly Soup:
  1. Get Soup
  2. Make ready 2 cups beef trimmings
  3. Prepare 1 large potato
  4. Make ready 1 large onion
  5. Make ready 2 cups chopped smoked Boston butt pork roast
  6. Get 1 quart beef broth
  7. Take 1/2 stick butter divided
  8. Prepare 2 cups baby carrots

  12. Get 2 tablespoon rice vinegar
  13. Take 1-1/2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  14. Prepare 15 ounces canned diced tomatoes
  15. Make ready 15 ounces canned corn whole kernel
  16. Make ready Cheese option

Instructions to make Scraggly Soup:
  1. Chop the vegetables, slice the beef, and slice the pork roast.
  2. Heat a pot caramelize the onions, in half the butter. When the onions are caramelized add all the ingredients. Bring to a boil.
  3. Simmer 40 minutes. Serve with cheese, and crusty bread. I hope you enjoy!! Shown below with the cheese option.
