The ingredients needed to make Meat pie:
  1. Get 500 g flour
  2. Take 250 g butter
  3. Take 1 egg yolk
  4. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  5. Get Pinch salt
  6. Take 1/2 cup Cold water
  7. Make ready For filling
  8. Take 500 g mincemeat
  9. Prepare 3 big potatoes
  10. Prepare 3 carrots
  11. Take Spring onion
  12. Make ready Seasoning
  13. Get Curry
  14. Prepare Flour

Steps to make Meat pie:
  1. Add the flour in a bowl،add butter,salt,baking powder and mix till crumbly
  2. AddAnd egg yolk and water,mix to form a dough,do not over mix,Allow the dough to rest for 30 mins
  3. Add lil veg oil in a pan,add mincemeat and stir till brown
  4. Add chopped potatoes,curry, seasoning and lil water allow to cook
  5. Add carrots and cook,add spring onion,mix flour and water to form a paste add to the filings and off the heat
  6. Roll ur dough add filling sealed with egg,eggwash and bake for 25 mins after preheating your oven
