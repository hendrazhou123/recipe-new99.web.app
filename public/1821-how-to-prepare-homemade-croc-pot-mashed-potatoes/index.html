The ingredients needed to make Croc pot mashed potatoes:
  1. Take 2-5 pounds potatoes
  2. Take 3/4 c chicken broth
  3. Make ready 1/2 ts onion powder
  4. Make ready 1 tsp garlic salt
  5. Get 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  6. Make ready 1/4 c butter
  7. Take 1/4 c butter
  8. Get 1/4 c sour cream
  9. Make ready to taste Salt & pepper
  10. Make ready Splash milk

Steps to make Croc pot mashed potatoes:
  1. Peel & cut potatoes into chunks. Add to croc pot. Place broth, garlic salt, powder & onion powder. Cook 3-4 hrs on high or 6-7 hours on low. Stir occasionally.
  2. Using a potato masher, mash until smooth. Stir in sour cream, butter & milk. Mash again. Add salt & pepper to season. Serve.
