The ingredients needed to make Southern Fish Fry:
  1. Take 5-10 flounder fillets cut into strips or catfish, any white fish
  2. Take 2 cups corn meal
  3. Prepare 2 cups flour
  4. Take 2 eggs, beaten
  5. Take 2 tbsp yellow mustard
  6. Get 1/2 cup hot sauce

  9. Prepare 1 tbsp garlic powder
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp onion powder
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp paprika
  12. Make ready Lemon to serve
  13. Prepare Cooking oil
  14. Make ready Hot sauce

Steps to make Southern Fish Fry:
  1. Rinse fish and pay dry
  2. Slice fish in half to get long strips
  3. Place fish in large bowl
  4. Heat oil in large pan over medium heat. Not filling more than half way with the oil.
  5. Mix seasoning blend and set aside
  6. In a freezer bag add cornmeal and flour and 2 tbsp of seasoning blend. Mix well.
  7. Add remaining seasoning blend and hot sauce, eggs and mustard to fish bowl. Mix well.
  8. Once oil is ready place a few strips of fish into corn/flour and shake well to coat.
  9. Remove excess flour from fish by lightly shaking when removing it from the bag.
  10. Place fish in pan and fry for 4-5 mins per side.
  11. Don't crowd the pan. Repeat until all fish is cooked.
  12. Drain on napkin. Sprinkle a pinch more Old Bay on the top of fish when they come out (optional) serve with lemon wedges and hot sauce.
