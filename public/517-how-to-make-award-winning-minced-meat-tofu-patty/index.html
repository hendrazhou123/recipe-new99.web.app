The ingredients needed to make Minced meat tofu patty:
  1. Take 150 g Tofu
  2. Prepare 150 g Minced meat
  3. Prepare Egg x1
  4. Make ready Teaspoon Shao xing wine x1/2
  5. Get Teaspoon Soya sauce x1
  6. Take Tablespoon Oyster sauce x1
  7. Take Water x6 tablespoon (add more water if you need)
  8. Get Teaspoon Dark soya sauce x3/4
  9. Prepare Garlic powder and onion powder (OPT)

Instructions to make Minced meat tofu patty:
  1. Mix tofu and mince meat
  2. Add beaten egg in
  3. Add 1 teaspoon of soya sauce to taste, dash of pepper, onion and garlic powder for taste.
  4. Add oil to pan
  5. Use a tablespoon to scope mixture into pan (low-med heat)
  6. 3 mins on eat side (or slightly browned)
  7. Sauce - add all ingredients in and mix. Add water to suit your taste.
  8. Option 1) Add patties back into pan and cook 30 seconds with sauce (make sure sauce covers all patties)
  9. Option 2) Cook sauce and pour them on top of patties
