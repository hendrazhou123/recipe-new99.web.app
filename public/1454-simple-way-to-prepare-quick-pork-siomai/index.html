The ingredients needed to make Pork Siomai:
  1. Take 250 grams ground pork
  2. Make ready 30 siomai wrapper / homemade
  3. Get 1 onion (minced)
  4. Get 5 cloves garlic (minced)
  5. Prepare 1 carrot (minced)
  6. Get 1 egg beaten
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup flour
  8. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
  9. Make ready to taste Salt

Instructions to make Pork Siomai:
