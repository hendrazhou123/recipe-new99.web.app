The ingredients needed to make Spanish Rice:
  1. Get 2 Tbs oil
  2. Make ready 20 oz rice
  3. Take 1 small onion chopped
  4. Make ready 44 oz water
  5. Get 1/4 Tbs garlic powder
  6. Make ready 1/4 Tbs onion powder
  7. Get 1 dash cumin
  8. Take 2 Tbs knors tomato and chicken bouillon

Steps to make Spanish Rice:
  1. In a 4 quart pot, add in oil and Rice. Toast rice until it's light brown. Add in onion, mix for 2 minutes.
  2. Add in remaining ingredients, bring to boil. Cover and turn to low heat. Cook for 20 minutes.
