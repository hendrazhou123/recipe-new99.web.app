The ingredients needed to make Fish Fry:
  1. Make ready 2 Whole Tilapia You can use any fish you like
  2. Take 1 Tablespoon Garlic Powder
  3. Prepare 1 Tablespoon Lemon Pepper Powder
  4. Get 1/2 Teaspoon Red Chilly Powder aka Paprika
  5. Get Salt as per taste
  6. Take 1/2 Tablespoon Coriander Powder
  7. Prepare 2 Tablespoons Oil
  8. Take 1/2 Cup Breadcrumbs

Steps to make Fish Fry:
  1. Mix up all the above ingredients in a large bowl; except for oil and breadcrumbs.
  2. Make sure your fish is nicely coated in the spice mix.
  3. Allow it to sit for 1/2 hour, this is totally optional.
  4. If you don't have time to marinate the fish you can cook it immediately after adding the spices.
  5. Heat oil in a pan; while that's heating up coat the spiced fish in breadcrumbs.
  6. Fry the fish on both the sides until done.
  7. You could have this with Tartar Sauce or a fresh salad.
  8. Happy Cooking!
