Instructions to make Creamy lemon fish:
  1. In one large pan add your fish just after greasing the pan, and season using salt and pepper (if using salamon keep skined)
  2. After the fish been cooked and flipped on both sides remove from the pan and set aside
  3. In the same pan add 3 cups heavy cream, 4 tbsp lemon juice, feshly chopped parsley, 1 tbsp minced garlic, some salt and pepper and let simmer
  4. After a couple of minutes re add the fish to the pan on medium heat (or in oven) and let simmer to get thicker tasty sauce (10 mins) then enjoy!
