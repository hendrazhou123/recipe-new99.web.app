The ingredients needed to make Canned Tuna, Japanese Brown Rice Kimbap:

  2. Get sheets Nori
  3. Take Ripe Mango
  4. Take Japanese Brown Rice
  5. Take Japanese Mayo
  6. Make ready Cane vinegar
  7. Get Sugar and salt (1 tsp each)
  8. Get Cucumber
