The ingredients needed to make Soup, December 2019:
  1. Make ready 1/4 cup soup seasonings see my recipe
  2. Prepare As needed salt and ground black pepper
  3. Make ready 1 pound smoked ham
  4. Make ready 1 pound ground sirloin
  5. Prepare 1/3 pound chicken breast boneless and skinless
  6. Take 1-1/2 pound bok-choy
  7. Make ready 3 medium sized potatoes Yukon gold
  8. Make ready 3 cups sliced baby carrots
  9. Prepare 1-1/2 cup sliced celery
  10. Make ready 1 quart beef broth
  11. Get 1 teaspoon salt divided
  12. Get 1/3 cup red wine I used Cabernet sauvignon
  13. Take 15 ounces canned diced tomatoes
  14. Make ready 2 cups cooked cabbage see note below *
  15. Get 2 cups leftover cooked rice see note below *
  16. Prepare To taste hot sauce optional

Steps to make Soup, December 2019:
  1. Wash the vegetables. Dice the potatoes and cut the carrots on a bias. Chop the bok-choy and celery.
  2. Brown the ground sirloin dice the ham and onions.
  3. To the pot you browned the beef in add the chicken and sauté till no longer pink. Add the salt and pepper. Add the ham and onions to the cooked chicken. Season the onion, with a bit of salt.
  4. Add the ground beef and season with a bit of salt and garlic. Add the broth and bring to a boil.
  5. Add the soup seasonings stir in. Add the cooked leftover rice, cabbage, and diced tomatoes. Add the bok-choy and celery, a little at a time to allow it to wilt. When all is added allow to come to a boil. *The cabbage and rice was some extra that wouldn't fit in my recipe The famous nutmeg casserole. *
  6. Add the carrots and potatoes. Measure the wine, then add in.
  7. Simmer till the potatoes and carrots are tender. Serve I hope you enjoy!!!
