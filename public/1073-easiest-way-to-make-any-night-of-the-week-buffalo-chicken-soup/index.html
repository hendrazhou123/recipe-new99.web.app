The ingredients needed to make Buffalo chicken soup:
  1. Get 2 Tbsp. butter
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 C. celery, chopped
  3. Prepare 1 yellow onion, diced
  4. Make ready 2 cloves garlic, minced
  5. Get 1 tsp. paprika
  6. Take 1 tsp. garlic powder
  7. Make ready 1 tsp. onion powder
  8. Prepare 1 (32 oz.) Carton chicken broth
  9. Make ready 2 lbs. chicken breasts, cooked, shredded
  10. Prepare 1/2 C. ranch dressing
  11. Prepare 3/4 C. buffalo sauce
  12. Make ready 4 oz. cream cheese
  13. Prepare 2 C. shredded cheddar cheese
  14. Get 3 green onions, chopped

Steps to make Buffalo chicken soup:
  1. In a large sauce pan or skillet melt butter on medium heat. Saute celery and onion until soft and onion is translucent. 3-4 minutes. Add garlic, saute another 1 minute.
  2. Season with paprika, garlic powder and onion powder. Cook until fragrant. Another minute or so. Add chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  3. Add the buffalo sauce, ranch dressing, cream cheese and shredded cheese. Cook stirring occasionally until smooth and creamy
  4. Add shredded chicken.
  5. Top with more shredded cheese and green onions. And more buffalo sauce if desired.
