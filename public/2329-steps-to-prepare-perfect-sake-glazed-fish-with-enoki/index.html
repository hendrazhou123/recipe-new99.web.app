The ingredients needed to make Sake Glazed Fish with Enoki:
  1. Get 2 x0.25 lbs Tilapia/Fish Fillets
  2. Take to taste Salt
  3. Make ready to taste Black Pepper
  4. Take 2 tbs Unsalted Butter (divided)
  5. Get 2 cloves Garlic (minced)
  6. Get 1 tbs Ginger (minced or grated)
  7. Prepare 0.3 cup Sake/Mirin (or Cooking Wine)
  8. Prepare 1 tbs Rice Vinegar
  9. Prepare 0.5 tbs Salt
  10. Prepare 0.5 lbs Enoki Mushrooms
  11. Make ready 3 tbs Sliced Green Onions

Instructions to make Sake Glazed Fish with Enoki:
  1. Rinse and pat dry the tilapia fillets. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Melt 1 tablespoon of butter and then add the tilapia fillets. Cook 1-2 minutes on each side or until golden and cooked through. Remove the fillets from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan over medium heat, add the remaining tablespoon of butter and then stir in the garlic and ginger. Cook for 1 minute or until soft.
  4. Add the sake, rice vinegar, salt, and mushrooms to the pan, and increase the heat to high. Cook for 30 seconds to 1 minute, or until the mushrooms are tender. Season with additional salt and pepper, if desired.
  5. Serve the tilapia garnished with green onions.
