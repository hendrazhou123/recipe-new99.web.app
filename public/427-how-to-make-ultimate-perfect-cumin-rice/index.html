Instructions to make Perfect cumin rice:
  1. First soak rice in warm water atleast 30 minutes after washing
  2. Now roast cumin seeds in oil and add salt and pepper
  3. Add soaked rice and water.let it boil at high flame
  4. Now set the flame between medium and low points cuz flame should not too low or completely medium
  5. Let it cook until holes are not appeared at the top so turn the gas off immediately.this time period should 8 to 10 minutes
  6. Now rice will cook properly at steam inside pan.dont uncovered the utensil after holes are appeared.just keep uncover the utensil and let it cook remain at steaming temperature
  7. Enjoy yummy fluffy cumin rice
