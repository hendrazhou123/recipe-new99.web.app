Instructions to make Paksiw Fish:
  1. Wash properly the fish, remove inwards.
  2. Take a pot, place some ginger garlic and spring onion stalks at the bottom. Carefully arrange the fish
  3. After putting fish, slice tomato and put on top together with garlic, onion and spring onion, black pepper
  4. Pour vinegar, cooking oil, black pepper. Cover and simmer until the fish done.
