The ingredients needed to make Tomato_mushroom egg drop soup:
  1. Take 1 big tomato
  2. Make ready 5/6 mushrooms
  3. Make ready 1 tspoon coariander stems
  4. Prepare 1 tspoon garlic chopped
  5. Prepare 1 tspoon ginger chopped
  6. Make ready 1/2 tspoon green chilli chopped
  7. Prepare 1 tbspoon corn flour
  8. Take to taste Salt
  9. Make ready 1/2 tspoon Black pepper powder
  10. Prepare 1 egg
  11. Make ready 1/2 tspoon butter
  12. Make ready 1/2 tspoon oil
