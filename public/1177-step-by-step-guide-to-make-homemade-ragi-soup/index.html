The ingredients needed to make Ragi Soup:
  1. Get 4 sp ragi flour
  2. Prepare 3 sp carrot chopped finely
  3. Prepare 3 sp beans chopped finely
  4. Take 3 sp cauliflower chopped finely
  5. Take 3 sp peas
  6. Take 1 sp garlic chopped finely
  7. Get 1 sp pepper powder
  8. Prepare 1 sp rosted jeera powder
  9. Get to taste Salt
  10. Prepare 2 sp oil
  11. Prepare 1 sp jeera
  12. Take 1 lemon juice

Steps to make Ragi Soup:
  1. Keep ready all veges.
  2. Heat oil add jeera add garlic and saute for 2min.then add all veges and cook for sometime add salt. Make slurry of ragi flour.
  3. Add 4cups water add jeera powder and let it boil then add ragi flour slurry and boil till thick. Add lemon juice.
  5. Pour in bowls sprinkle some jeera powder and pepper powder and serve.
