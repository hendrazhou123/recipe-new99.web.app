Instructions to make Meat rolls:
  1. Mix flour, baking powder, salt and one Maggi together.add oil and mix well.
  2. Create a centre in your flour and add little water to make a non sticky soft dough.
  3. Knead until well combined. Cover and allow to rest for 10 mins.
  4. As you allow your dough to rest, Minch meat and stir fry with onions, cut potato into cubes and boil, add boiled potatoes into minced meat, add seasoning stir and stir fry for like 5 mins. Remove from fire and set aside
  5. Cut dough and roll out flat on a clean and flat surface.(It shouldn't be too thick).
  6. Add your meat and potatoe filling on your dough and roll it.
  7. Deep fry In hot oil until golden brown. Allow to cool and it's all ready to serve.
