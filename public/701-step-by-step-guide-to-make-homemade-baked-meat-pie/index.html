The ingredients needed to make Baked meat pie:
  1. Take For the dough
  2. Make ready 5 cups flour
  3. Make ready 2 tablespoons sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Take 2 tsp baking powder
  6. Prepare 1 cup butter
  7. Prepare 3 eggs
  8. Prepare 1 egg for sealing and glazing
  9. Make ready For the filling
  10. Get 1 kilo ground beef
  11. Take 6 cups chopped potatoes
  12. Take 1 cups chopped carrot
  13. Prepare 3 cups chopped onions
  14. Prepare Seasonings
  15. Take 3 scotch bonnet
  16. Take 2 tbsp flour
  17. Make ready 2 tbsp butter
  18. Get 1 cup water
  19. Prepare 1/2 cup oil
  20. Prepare Crushed garlic
Instructions to make Baked meat pie:
  1. Cook the ground beef first with onions, maggi and vinegar(saboda qarni)
  2. Then add ur oil, then add chopped onions and crushed garlic and stir for 5 mins. Then add ur scotch bonnet and seasonings.
  3. Add in your cooked potatoes and carrot. Add a cup of water. Then mix ur flour with 4 tbsp water and add. It helps thicken d mixture. Then leave for 5 mins. Then lastly add in your butter and turn off heat. Set aside
  4. To make d dough, Add all your ingredients in a bowl and mix for 10 mins(don‚Äôt overmix) then set aside and let to rest for 10 mins.
  5. Cut ur dough into pieces and roll out. Then fill in your fillings in d meat pie. Brush d edges with egg so that it can seal. Repeat this process for all. Then arrange on your baking tray. Use your egg to brush the surface(egg wash)
  6. Then bake on a low heat for 25 mins. Allow to cool and serve. Enjoyüòç
