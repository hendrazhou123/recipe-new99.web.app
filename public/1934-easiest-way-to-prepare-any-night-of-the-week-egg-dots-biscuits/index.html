The ingredients needed to make Egg dots biscuits :
  1. Prepare 2 eggs
  2. Take Pinch yellow colour,1 tsp of vanilla essence
  3. Prepare 3/4 cup sugar powder,1/3 cup butter(room temperature)
  4. Make ready 1 cup all purpose flour,1/2 tsp of baking powder OR 1/4 tsp baking soda

Instructions to make Egg dots biscuits :
  1. Take 2 eggs in a bowl,whisk for few seconds.now add colour and vanilla essence and now whisk till a bit foamy
  2. Now add the sugar and butter and whisk till both combined.now seive flour and baking powder together.
  3. Now mix flour with the egg batter.now batter is ready so put some batter in a pastry bag.lined up the baking tray with parchment paper and do brush little oil
  4. Now cut small tip of the pastry bag and make small dots on the bake tray.bake each badge 180掳 for 10 t0 15 mins,it comes out easily
  5. Biscuits are ready.cool them and store in airtight container. Will be fresh for week.enjoy
