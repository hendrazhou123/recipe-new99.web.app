The ingredients needed to make Sweet Onion Rice:
  1. Get 1 onion sliced thin
  2. Prepare 1 teaspoon neutral oil
  3. Get 1.5 tablespoon soy sauce
  4. Make ready 2 tablespoons mirin
  5. Take 2 cups short grain Japanese rice
  6. Prepare 2 scallions chopped

Instructions to make Sweet Onion Rice:
  1. Heat oil in pan over medium heat
  2. Add onion and sauté until they begin to soften
  3. Add soy sauce and mirin and continue to cook until sauce thickens
  4. Add rice to instant pot or rice cooker
  5. Add onions and 2 cups of water
  6. Cook rice on normal rice setting. For instant pot, pressure cook for 4 minutes then natural release for 15 min
  7. Top with scallions and Enjoy!
