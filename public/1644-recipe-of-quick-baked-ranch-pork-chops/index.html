The ingredients needed to make Baked Ranch Pork Chops:
  1. Prepare 4-6 boneless pork cops
  2. Take 1/4 cup oil
  3. Take 2 tbsp (1 oz) dry ranch mix packet
  4. Prepare 1 tsp dried parsley (optional)

Instructions to make Baked Ranch Pork Chops:
  1. Pre heat oven 425 degrees.
  2. Place pork chops on baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. In a small bowl mix, ranch mix, oil and parsley.
  4. Spoon mixture over chops.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes then broil on high for 5 minutes or until your desired done range.
