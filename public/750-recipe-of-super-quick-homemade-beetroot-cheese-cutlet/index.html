The ingredients needed to make Beetroot Cheese cutlet:
  1. Prepare 1 cup beetroot finely chopped
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup carrot chopped
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup mashed boiled potatoes
  4. Get 1 tsp panch phoran
  5. Take 2 green chillies finely chopped
  6. Take 1 tsp ginger paste
  7. Make ready salt as per taste
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp sugar
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp roasted peanuts
  10. Make ready 1 tsp raisins
  11. Take 1 tsp chopped coriander leaves
  12. Get 2 tsp mustard oil
  13. Prepare 1 tsp ghee
  14. Take For bhaja masala

Steps to make Beetroot Cheese cutlet:
  1. Heat a pan add oil and all the dry masala along with ginger paste then add mashed potatoes, carrots, beetroot, roasted peanuts then cooked it until everything is wellcombined
  2. In another bowl add curd cheeses the other dry spices mix it, now take a small ball size beetroot mixture give it a heart shape then at the middle put the curd cheese mixture
  3. Again put the beetroot mixture then dip it all dry purpose flour then dip into slurry of all-purpose flour and corn flour mixture
  4. Now again dip it into bread crumbs then deep frying in oil until golden crisp
