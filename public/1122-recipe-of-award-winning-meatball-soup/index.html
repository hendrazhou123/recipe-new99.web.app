The ingredients needed to make Meatball soup:
  1. Make ready 1 tablespoon olive oil
  2. Take 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup diced sweet onion
  4. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
  5. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. Prepare 1 can petite diced tomatoes 15 ounces (not drained)
  7. Get 4 cups beef broth
  8. Take 3 cups mixed vegetables or 3/4ths cup each: carrots, peas, green beans, corn
  9. Get 1 package meatballs about 25 bite size
