The ingredients needed to make Meat pie:
  1. Take 300 g Flour
  2. Make ready Margarine 150g but I use 120g
  3. Take Pinch salt
  4. Take Half teaspoon of baking powder
  5. Make ready Small sprinkle of nutmeg
  6. Get Very cold water
  7. Take 1 egg and another one for rubbing on the meat pie
  8. Take For filling Meat,carrot, potato,peas

Steps to make Meat pie:
  1. I put the margarine in the fridge so it would be cold because the recipe i used said use cold butter.
  2. For the filling I boiled my meat then grind in food processor. - I then fried my onion in small oil add diced potatoes, carrot and pea fry a little bit add small water for the carrot and pea to soften.. Add meat fo the carrot mix,then add seasonings and spice of your choice.
  3. For dough - measure flour sieve,add baking powder, salt, nutmeg.
  4. Then cut the margarine inside the flour mix use your finger tip to mix flour and margarine till the mix become crumbly.
  5. Break egg mix then add to the mixture - Add cold water little by little to form the dough.
  6. The dough should not be watery and don't over work the dough.
  7. Allow dough to rest a bit then assemble. - Sprinkle flour on flat clean surface put dough. Roll dough flat but not extremely flat, cut round shape add the filling,then rub small egg with pastry brush round the edges of dough, fold the dough and press with fork.
  8. Continue cutting, filling and sealing until you have finished with the dough, then rub egg on top.
  9. Rub butter on tray put the meat pie then bake.
