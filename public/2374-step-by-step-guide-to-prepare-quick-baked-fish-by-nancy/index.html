The ingredients needed to make Baked fish by Nancy:
  1. Prepare 1 piece medium size white fish
  2. Take For marination
  3. Get 1 tbsp red crushed chilli
  4. Take 1 tsp black pepper powder
  5. Get salt
  6. Take 1 tsp ginger,garlic paste

  8. Make ready for coating

  10. Make ready 2 tbsp butter
  11. Get 1/2 tsp dry mustard

Instructions to make Baked fish by Nancy:
  1. Pre heat oven for 170°c
  2. Take a piece of fish mix all ingredients for marination. Marinate fish for 20 minutes.
  3. Now mix all coating ingredients (except chillies) well.Take aluminium foil apply coating half batter in the mid of foil and put fish on it then coat fish completely with remaining batter.Now sprinkle chopped chillies on the top.
  4. Wrapped the foil tightly around the fish now put it into oven and bake for 25 minutes or till fish get done.When you feel fish is done properly in the last minutes turn on grill for perfect golden color.
  5. Always wash fish with white vinegar and salt it removes fish unwanted smell.
