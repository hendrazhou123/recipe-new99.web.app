The ingredients needed to make Jollof basmati rice:
  1. Take I cup of Basmati rice
  2. Make ready 4 carrots chopped
  3. Make ready 1/2 tablespoon Seasoning
  4. Make ready 1 cooking spoon of Oil
  5. Make ready 1 big Green pepper (chopped)
  6. Make ready 5 strands Safron
  7. Take 1/4 tablespoon mixed spice
  8. Make ready 1 tablespoon Ginger and garlic paste
  9. Take Oil
  10. Get black pepper

Steps to make Jollof basmati rice:
  1. Wash basmati rice with salt and keep in water for 5 mins
  2. Rinse and keep aside
  3. Fry little oil and pour rice inside.
  4. Add garlic& ginger paste.fry for 2 min
  5. Add Safron
  6. Add carrots and mix well
  7. Add seasoning,black pepper and continue cooking
  8. Add salt and mix to continue frying.add curry
  9. Add water or stock and cover till ok /drained
  10. Serve rice
