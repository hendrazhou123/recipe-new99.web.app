The ingredients needed to make Sweet and Sour fish:
  1. Take 250 gr Gurami fish
  2. Prepare 250 gr flour seasoning
  3. Take 100 ml water
  4. Make ready 500 ml Vegetables oil
  5. Prepare Carrot
  6. Get Cucumber
  7. Take Spices
  8. Take 1 tbsp tomatoes paste
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp tomatoes sauce
  10. Make ready 1.5 tbsp sugar
  11. Take 1 tsp chili sauce
  12. Get 5 gr white onion (Bombay)
  13. Get 1 tbsp garlic oil or sesame oil
  14. Prepare 1 tbsp tapioca flour
  15. Prepare 350 ml water
  16. Take 5 gr pineapple

Steps to make Sweet and Sour fish:
  1. Fillet the fish
  2. Prepare spices and sweet sour sauce. boil 350ml water, tomatoes paste, tomatoes sauce,chili sauce,onion, pineapple and all spices. For 5 minute. We add tapioca flour after 5 minute done.
  3. Add fillet fish in flour with water, after that add to dry flour and fried till crispy.
  4. Carrot and cucumber, cut sliced
  5. Tipping sauce and pickled carrot cucumber into fish. Serve
