The ingredients needed to make Sour Cereal Soup:
  1. Get 5 ounces Spainish Chorizo
  2. Get 2/3 cup sliced leeks
  3. Make ready 1/2 pound Yukon gold fingerling potatoes
  4. Make ready 48 ounces chicken broth
  5. Make ready 2 tablespoons Spainish paprika ground
  6. Take 1 teaspoon salt more if needed
  7. Make ready 1 tablespoon honey
  8. Make ready 2/3 cup steel rolled oatmeal
  9. Get 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  10. Get 1/4 cup parsley flakes
  11. Prepare 8 ounces baby portobello mushrooms
  12. Prepare 3 tablespoons soya sauce
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup smoked hog jawls bacon
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup lingonberry fruit sauce

Instructions to make Sour Cereal Soup:
  1. Get a pot and heat with nonstick spray. Remove casing from chorizo. Slice the chorizo, mushrooms, and leeks. Dice the potatoes.
  2. Add the mushrooms and leeks to the chorizo. Season with salt. Sauté for 10 minutes. Add the diced potatoes and chopped hog jawls. Sauté for 15 minutes.
  3. Add in the rest of the spices. Sauté after you add the lingonberry. Then add the broth. Bring to a boil then simmer covered for 12 minutes.
  4. Add the oatmeal and cover. Remove from heat and allow the residual heat to cook for 30 minutes. Serve I hope you enjoy!!
