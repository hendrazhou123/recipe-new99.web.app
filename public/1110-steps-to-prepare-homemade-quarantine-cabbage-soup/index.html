The ingredients needed to make Quarantine Cabbage Soup:
  1. Get 3 Tbs olive oil
  2. Get 1 white onionchopped
  3. Prepare 1 bunch celery chopped
  4. Make ready 4 russet potatoes chopped
  5. Make ready 6 small carrots chopped
  6. Take Half a bag of cauliflower pearls
  7. Get 1 head cabbage chopped
  8. Take 64 oz Chicken Stock
  9. Get Half teaspoon of Wylers Chicken Powder
  10. Prepare 1 Caldo de Pollo chicken Bouillon
  11. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
  12. Take 1 can stewed tomatoes
  13. Prepare 2 cups water
