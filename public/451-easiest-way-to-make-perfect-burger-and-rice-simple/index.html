The ingredients needed to make Burger and Rice Simple:
  1. Prepare 1-1/3 pound beef
  2. Prepare 2 cups rice
  3. Get 2 cups water
  4. Take 1 quart beef broth
  5. Take 1 teaspoon granulated onion powder
  6. Make ready 1-1/2 teaspoon salt
  7. Get 1 teaspoon ground white pepper

Steps to make Burger and Rice Simple:
  1. Brown the beef with the pepper, and half the salt. When browned add rice, onion powder, and allow to cook 5 minutes stirring often.
  2. Add half the broth and allow to start bubbling. Then add the rest of the broth. Add rest of the salt. Bring to a simmer add the water as needed till rice is where you like it cooked, the way you eat it.
