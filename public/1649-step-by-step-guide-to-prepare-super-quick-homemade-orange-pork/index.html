  4. Prepare 1-1/2 pound pork loin
  5. Get 1 teaspoon pink Himalayan salt
  6. Take 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  7. Make ready 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  8. Get 1/3 cup tapioca starch

Instructions to make Orange Pork:
  1. Season the porkloin with salt and pepper. Heat the oil in the pan. Sear the meat on each side.
  2. When seared remove from heat and set aside.
  3. To the pan add the marmalade and honey to the pan. Scrape the bits off the bottom. When scraped off add the peach brandy.
