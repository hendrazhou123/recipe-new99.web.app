The ingredients needed to make Khatta Meat:
  1. Prepare 700 gms Mutton
  2. Get 1/2 cup Beaten curd
  3. Get 4 teaspoons Sattu powder
  4. Take 2 tablespoons Garam masala powder
  5. Make ready 2 teaspoons Kashmiri red chilli powder
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder
  7. Get 4 tablespoons Mustard oil
  8. Make ready 2 tablespoons Ghee
  9. Prepare As per your choice Whole garam-masala
  10. Take 2-3 Bay leaves
  11. Make ready 3 tablespoons Ginger paste
  12. Prepare 4 tablespoons Garlic paste
  13. Take 2 medium sized Tomato paste
  14. Prepare As per taste Salt
  15. Make ready Pinch Sugar

Instructions to make Khatta Meat:
  1. Marinade the mutton with beaten curd,sattu powder,Kashmiri red chilli powder, turmeric powder,ginger-garlic paste. Keep it in the fridge for 30-45 minutes.
  2. Heat the oil, add bay leaves, whole garam masala.
  3. Add tomato paste,and fry it. When the paste colour turned brown,and oil gets separate from the tomato paste, then add the marinaded mutton without water and fry it well. - **Don’t add the marinaded water.
  4. Now add salt and cook it. - Then add the marinaded water. - If required more water you add it. Cover it. - Cooked well until the mutton turns soft with slow-flame.
  5. After the mutton boiled,add the sugar,ghee and garam masala powder. Mixed well and cover and cook it for few minutes.
  6. Serve with any type of rice or roti-parthas.
  7. Note-*You can add green chilli or red chilli powder
