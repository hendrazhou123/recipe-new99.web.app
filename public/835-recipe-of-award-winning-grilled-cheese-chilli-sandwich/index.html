Steps to make Grilled cheese chilli sandwich:
  1. Take a bowl mix chopped veggies, add black pepper and chaat masala, mix it. Add all shredded cheese.
  2. Take 2 bread slices apply butter. Then apply green chutney on one slice and red garlic chutney on other slice.
  3. Now load one bread slice with cheese chilli mix. Place a cheese slice on top of it and put the left bread slice on it. Meanwhile turn in sandwich grill to preheat
  4. Brush grill with butter and place the sandwich in it and begin grilling
  5. Let the sandwich grill to perfection so that cheese meltdowns and give you a taste to remember lifetime üòÑ enjoy with your choice of sauce.
