The ingredients needed to make Beef chops:
  1. Get 1 kg Chops
  2. Prepare 3 Tomatoes
  3. Prepare 2 onion
  4. Get 2 Green chili
  5. Prepare 1 bowl Yogurt (medium)
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon Garlic
  7. Prepare 1 tablespoon Ginger
  8. Get 1 teaspoon Crushed coriander
  9. Take 1 teaspoon Red chili flakes
  10. Take 1 teaspoon Turmeric
  11. Take Salt as per taste
  12. Get 1 teaspoon Crushed cumin
  13. Take 1 teaspoon Crushed black pepper
  14. Make ready 1 teaspoon Hot spice
  15. Get 1 cup Oil
  16. Prepare 1 cup Water

Steps to make Beef chops:
  1. Take Chops, garlic ginger paste, tomatoes, onions and put one glass of water and steam it in cooker for 5 mints.
  2. Open it add oil and cook it 10 mints when mix well oil separates from chops.
  3. Then put yogurt and all spices and cook it 10 mints
  4. At the end off the flame and garnishing with green chili and fresh parsley.
